Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about starting a project with us

Our start dates vary depending on current workload and the complexity of your project. Typically, we can begin new builds within 4-8 weeks of contract signing, while extensions and renovations may start within 2-6 weeks. During our initial consultation, we'll provide a realistic timeline for your specific project.

Yes, we offer free, no-obligation estimates for all projects. After discussing your requirements, we'll provide a detailed breakdown of costs. For more complex projects, we may recommend a paid consultation to develop preliminary designs before providing accurate pricing.

We primarily serve Banbridge and surrounding areas within a 20-mile radius, including Dromore, Rathfriland, and Gilford. For larger projects beyond this area, we're happy to discuss options - please contact us to see if we can accommodate your location.

Project durations vary significantly based on scope. Here are typical timelines:

  • Single-story extensions: 6-10 weeks
  • Two-story extensions: 10-16 weeks
  • New build homes: 6-12 months
  • Whole house renovations: 8-20 weeks

We'll provide a detailed schedule during the planning phase.
