With energy prices at record highs and environmental concerns growing, building an energy efficient home in Northern Ireland has never been more important. At GK Contracts Build, we specialize in sustainable construction methods that can reduce your energy bills by up to 60% compared to traditional builds.

1. Superior Insulation: The Foundation of Efficiency

Northern Ireland's climate demands excellent insulation. We recommend:

Wall Insulation

  • 300mm mineral wool for cavity walls
  • PIR insulation boards for internal walls
  • Airtight membranes to prevent heat loss

Roof & Floor Insulation

  • 400mm loft insulation exceeds regulations
  • Insulated foundation systems prevent ground chill
  • Thermal breaks at structural connections

Case Study: Banbridge Eco-Home

"Our 2022 project in Banbridge achieved an A2 BER rating through 350mm insulated walls, triple glazing, and an air source heat pump. The homeowners report heating bills 65% lower than their previous property of similar size." - Sarah Thompson, Sustainability Specialist
