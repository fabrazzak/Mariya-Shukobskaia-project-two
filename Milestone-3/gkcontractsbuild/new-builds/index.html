New Build FAQs

Answers to common questions about our new build service

The timeline for a new build home varies depending on size, complexity, and weather conditions. Typically, a 3-4 bedroom home takes between 6-12 months from groundworks to completion. We provide a detailed timeline during the planning stage and keep you updated throughout the process.

Yes, we offer complete planning assistance. Our team includes architectural designers who can prepare all necessary drawings and documentation for your planning application. We'll guide you through the entire process and liaise with the local planning authority on your behalf.

Our comprehensive service includes:

  • Initial consultation and site assessment
  • Architectural design and planning applications
  • Groundworks and foundations
  • Construction of the property shell
  • First and second fixings
  • All internal finishes and external landscaping
  • Final snagging and handover

While we encourage finalizing all details during the planning stage, we understand that changes may be needed. We accommodate reasonable changes during construction, though these may affect the timeline and budget. Our project managers will discuss any implications before proceeding with changes.

All our new builds come with a 10-year structural warranty from a recognized provider. Additionally, we offer a 2-year warranty on all workmanship and materials. Our aftercare service ensures any issues are promptly addressed during the warranty period.
