OUR PROCESS

From Concept to Completion

A clear, structured approach to delivering your perfect extension

1

Initial Consultation

We visit your home to discuss your needs, assess feasibility, and provide initial advice on design options, budget, and timeline.

Duration: 1-2 weeks

2

Design & Planning

Our architects create detailed plans, submit for planning permission if required, and finalize all specifications with you.

Duration: 4-8 weeks

3

Preparation

We finalize contracts, schedule materials, and prepare the site. You'll receive a detailed project timeline and regular updates.

Duration: 2-4 weeks

4

Construction

Our skilled team builds your extension with minimal disruption. We maintain clean workspaces and protect your existing property.

Duration: 4-16 weeks

5

Completion

Final inspections, snagging, and handover. We provide all necessary certificates and warranties for your records.

Duration: 1-2 weeks
