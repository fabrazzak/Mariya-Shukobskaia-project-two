Top Golf Destinations for 2025

Dr. Sarah Johnson

Sports Psychologist | March 22, 2025

10 min read

Featured Destinations:

  • Scottish Highlands: Golf's historic home
  • Algarve, Portugal: Mediterranean golf paradise
  • Scottsdale, Arizona: Desert golf at its finest
  • Kiawah Island: Coastal Carolina gem
  • Bali, Indonesia: Tropical golf adventure

As we look ahead to 2025, golf travelers have more incredible destinations to choose from than ever before. Whether you're seeking historic links courses, luxury resort experiences, or hidden gems off the beaten path, this guide highlights the top destinations that should be on every golfer's radar.

1. Scottish Highlands, Scotland

The spiritual home of golf continues to reign supreme for purists. Beyond the famous Open Championship venues, the Highlands offer breathtaking courses like Royal Dornoch and Castle Stuart that combine challenging layouts with stunning natural beauty.

Why Visit in 2025?

2025 marks the 150th anniversary of Royal Dornoch Golf Club, with special events planned throughout the season. The newly renovated Wyndham Inverness provides the perfect base to explore multiple courses in the region.

Pro Tip:

Book early for summer months and consider shoulder seasons (April-May or September-October) for better rates and fewer crowds while still enjoying good weather.

2. Algarve, Portugal

Portugal's southern coast has emerged as Europe's premier golf destination, with over 40 courses featuring dramatic cliffside layouts and perfect year-round weather.

  • Must-Play Courses: Monte Rei, San Lorenzo, Vale do Lobo
  • Best Time to Visit: March-June for ideal temperatures
  • Wyndham Property: Wyndham Grand Algarve (5-star oceanfront resort)
"The Algarve combines world-class golf with incredible value compared to other European destinations. The seafood and local wines are an added bonus." — Maria Silva, PGA Professional

3. Scottsdale, Arizona

The American Southwest offers a completely different golf experience, with cactus-lined fairways and stunning desert landscapes. Scottsdale's 200+ courses cater to every skill level and budget.

Top Public Courses

  • TPC Scottsdale (Stadium Course)
  • We-Ko-Pa Golf Club
  • Troon North

Best Luxury Resorts

  • Wyndham Desert Oasis
  • The Boulders Resort
  • Four Seasons Scottsdale

4. Kiawah Island, South Carolina

This barrier island off the coast of Charleston boasts five championship courses, including the famous Ocean Course that hosted the 2021 PGA Championship.

2025 Special Events

  • PGA Professional Championship (February)
  • Kiawah Island Golf Festival (April)
  • Wyndham Member-Guest Tournament (October)

5. Bali, Indonesia

For adventurous golfers looking beyond traditional destinations, Bali offers a tropical golf experience unlike any other. The island's courses blend challenging layouts with Indonesian culture and breathtaking scenery.

Top Bali Golf Experiences

Bali National Golf Club

Greg Norman-designed course with volcanic views

New Kuta Golf

Cliffside course with Indian Ocean vistas

Wyndham Bali Retreat

Luxury beachfront villas with golf packages

Honorable Mentions

These emerging destinations deserve consideration for your 2025 travel plans:

Costa del Sol, Spain

Over 70 courses along Spain's sunny southern coast, with new luxury developments opening in 2025.

Sand Valley, Wisconsin

America's newest golf mecca featuring Coore & Crenshaw-designed courses in pristine natural surroundings.

Hanoi, Vietnam

Vietnam's golf scene continues to grow with world-class courses at surprisingly affordable prices.

Tasmania, Australia

Remote island with spectacular courses like Barnbougle that rival the world's best.

Final Thoughts

Whether you're planning a buddies trip, romantic getaway, or family golf vacation, 2025 offers endless possibilities. Consider mixing traditional golf destinations with new experiences to create unforgettable memories.
