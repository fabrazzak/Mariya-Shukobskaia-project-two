The 2025 equipment season brings exciting innovations that can genuinely help golfers of all levels play better. After testing hundreds of products with our Wyndham golf pros, we've identified the standouts that deliver real performance benefits—not just marketing hype. Here's our comprehensive guide to the year's best equipment.

Drivers: Distance Meets Forgiveness

This year's drivers showcase remarkable advancements in artificial intelligence design and materials science. We tested 18 new models on our resort launch monitors with players of various skill levels.

Callaway Paradym AI Smoke Triple Diamond

4.5/5 (Pro Choice Award)

Callaway's new AI-designed face optimization creates the hottest driver we've ever tested. The Triple Diamond version offers low spin for better players without sacrificing forgiveness.

Pros:

  • 17% larger sweet spot than 2024 model
  • Adjustable weights for shot shaping
  • Premium sound and feel

Cons:

  • Premium price point
  • Not ideal for high handicappers

Irons: Precision Engineering

Iron technology continues to evolve, with manufacturers finding new ways to combine playability with forgiveness. Our testing focused on three key metrics: distance consistency, forgiveness on mishits, and feel.

Titleist T350 Irons

5/5 (Editor's Choice)

Titleist has finally created a game-improvement iron that doesn't sacrifice the brand's renowned feel. The T350 offers exceptional distance with surprising workability.

Pros:

  • Consistent 5-7 yard distance gains
  • Premium forged feel
  • Clean player's look at address

Cons:

  • Not the most forgiving in category
  • Limited stock shaft options

Putters: Stroke Science

Putting accounts for 40% of strokes in a round, yet many golfers use outdated flatsticks. The 2025 models feature innovative alignment systems and face technologies that actually help improve stroke consistency.

Odyssey Ai-One Cruiser

5/5

Odyssey's AI-designed face insert produces incredibly consistent roll and distance control. The Cruiser model's high-MOI design is extremely forgiving.

LAB Golf DF3

4/5

The Lie Angle Balance technology eliminates torque for straighter strokes. Not for everyone, but extremely effective for players who adapt to the unique look.

Final Verdict: What's Worth Upgrading?

After extensive testing with our resort guests and pros, here's our upgrade priority list for 2025:

  1. Driver: The AI face designs deliver real gains (10+ yards for most players)
  2. Putter: New alignment and face tech can shave strokes immediately
  3. Game Tracker: Data reveals true weaknesses (Arccos or Shot Scope)
  4. Irons: Only if yours are 5+ years old (modern forgiveness helps)
  5. Wedges: Fresh grooves matter if you play often (replace every 75 rounds)

Remember that proper fitting is just as important as the equipment itself. Visit our resort pro shops for complimentary basic fittings with any club purchase.
