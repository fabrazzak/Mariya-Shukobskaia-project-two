Cookies Policy

Last Updated: May 15, 2025

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer, smartphone, or other device when you visit a website or use an online service. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently, as well as to provide information to the website owners.

Like most online services, UNIMOB Global Empire ("we," "us," or "our") uses cookies and similar technologies (such as web beacons, pixels, and local storage) to provide and secure our websites and mobile games, to remember your preferences, and to analyze how our services perform.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for our website to function and cannot be switched off in our systems. They are usually only set in response to actions made by you such as setting your privacy preferences, logging in, or filling in forms.

Examples include:

  • Authentication cookies to keep you logged in during your session
  • Security cookies to protect against fraudulent logins
  • Load-balancing cookies for site performance

Analytics Cookies

These cookies allow us to count visits and traffic sources so we can measure and improve the performance of our site. They help us know which pages are the most and least popular and see how visitors move around the site.

Examples include:

  • Google Analytics cookies to understand user behavior
  • Hotjar cookies for heatmaps and session recordings
  • Mixpanel cookies for product analytics

Preference Cookies

These cookies enable the website to provide enhanced functionality and personalization. They may be set by us or by third-party providers whose services we have added to our pages.

Examples include:

  • Language preference cookies
  • Region selection cookies
  • Theme preference cookies (light/dark mode)

Marketing Cookies

These cookies may be set through our site by our advertising partners. They may be used by those companies to build a profile of your interests and show you relevant advertisements on other sites.

Examples include:

  • Facebook Pixel cookies for ad targeting
  • Google Ads cookies for conversion tracking
  • LinkedIn Insight Tag cookies for ad performance

3. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for several purposes:

  • Authentication: To recognize you when you visit our website and authenticate you when you log into your account
  • Preferences: To remember your preferences and settings, such as language and region selection
  • Security: To protect your account from unauthorized access and detect malicious activity
  • Analytics: To understand how users interact with our website and improve our services
  • Advertising: To deliver relevant advertisements and measure their effectiveness

The specific cookies we use may change over time as we improve and update our services. We will periodically update this policy to reflect these changes.

4. Third-Party Cookies

In addition to our own cookies, we may also use various third-party cookies to report usage statistics of the service, deliver advertisements on and through the service, and so on.

Some common third-party services that may set cookies when you use our website include:

  • Google Analytics for website analytics
  • Facebook Pixel for advertising and analytics
  • Hotjar for user behavior analysis
  • Google Ads for advertising conversion tracking
  • Cloudflare for security and performance

These third-party services have their own privacy policies and cookie policies. We recommend you review them to understand how they use cookies and your data.

5. Managing Cookies

You have several options to control or limit how cookies are used on your device:

Browser Settings

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings preferences. However, if you limit the ability of websites to set cookies, you may worsen your overall user experience as some features may not work as intended.

Common browser cookie settings can be found here:

  • Google Chrome
  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Apple Safari
  • Microsoft Edge

Opt-Out Tools

You can opt out of certain types of cookies, particularly those used for advertising:

  • Digital Advertising Alliance opt-out
  • European Interactive Digital Advertising Alliance
  • Google Analytics opt-out

Our Cookie Consent Tool

When you first visit our website, you'll see a cookie consent banner where you can choose which types of cookies you accept, except for essential cookies which are necessary for the website to function. You can change your preferences at any time by clicking the "Cookie Settings" link in the footer of our website.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookies Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. When we make changes, we will revise the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy.

We encourage you to periodically review this page for the latest information on our cookie practices. Your continued use of our website after any changes to this policy constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
