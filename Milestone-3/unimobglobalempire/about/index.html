Our Story

Founded in 2020 in Hanoi, Vietnam, UNIMOB Global Empire began with a small team of passionate game developers who shared a common vision: to create accessible, enjoyable mobile games that bring joy to players around the world.

Our journey started with simple hyper-casual games, but we quickly found our niche in the idle/tycoon genre with the release of Food Fever in 2025. The game's success allowed us to expand our team and refine our craft.

Today, we're proud to have reached over 100,000 players worldwide and continue to grow as we develop new gaming experiences.

Our Mission

To create accessible, engaging mobile games that provide relaxing entertainment while challenging players' strategic thinking. We believe games should be easy to pick up but offer depth for those who seek it.

Our Values

  • Player-first design philosophy
  • Ethical monetization practices
  • Continuous learning and improvement
  • Celebrating Vietnamese creativity
