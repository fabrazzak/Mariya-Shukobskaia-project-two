The Rise of Game Development in Vietnam

How Vietnam is becoming a hotspot for mobile game innovation and what it means for UNIMOB

Le Nguyen

Marketing Director

May 12, 2025 • 12 min read
Image: UNIMOB team at the Vietnam Game Developers Conference 2025

Vietnam's game development industry has undergone a remarkable transformation in the past decade. From being primarily an outsourcing destination to becoming a hub for original IP creation, the country is now home to some of the most innovative mobile game studios in Southeast Asia. As the founder of UNIMOB Global Empire, I've witnessed this evolution firsthand.

From Outsourcing to Original IP

The Vietnamese game development scene began in the early 2010s with studios primarily providing art and programming services for foreign companies. However, several key factors have propelled the industry forward:

"What started as cost-effective labor has evolved into genuine creative leadership. Vietnamese studios are now creating global hits that compete directly with Western and Asian powerhouses."

2010-2015: The Outsourcing Era

  • Primarily art and programming services
  • Few original titles
  • Limited international presence

2020-Present: The Creative Era

  • Multiple global chart-toppers
  • Original IP development
  • Strong regional partnerships

Why Vietnam? The Competitive Advantages

Several unique factors make Vietnam particularly well-suited for mobile game development:

Technical Talent

Strong STEM education produces skilled engineers

Creative Culture

Rich artistic traditions inform game design

Growing Market

Domestic mobile gaming market expanding rapidly

The UNIMOB Journey

Our story mirrors Vietnam's gaming industry evolution. Founded in 2016 as a small team of 5, we initially took on contract work while developing our own prototypes. The breakthrough came with Food Fever Premium in 2021, which demonstrated our ability to create globally competitive titles.

Key milestones in our growth:

1

2016 - Founding

Small team working on outsourced projects and prototypes

2

2019 - First Original Title

Released Cafe Story with moderate success in Southeast Asia

3

2021 - Breakthrough

Food Fever Premium launched, reaching top 50 in 28 countries

4

2025 - Expansion

Two new titles in development, team growing to 60+

Challenges and Opportunities

While Vietnam's game industry has made impressive strides, challenges remain:

Challenges

  • Limited access to early-stage funding
  • Intense global competition
  • Talent retention in a hot market

Opportunities

  • Growing domestic player base
  • Government support programs
  • Unique cultural perspectives in game design

The Future of Vietnamese Game Development

Looking ahead, we see several exciting developments for Vietnam's game industry:

Regional Leadership

Vietnam is poised to become Southeast Asia's leading game development hub, with studios like UNIMOB leading the charge in innovation and quality.

Genre Expansion

Beyond mobile casual games, Vietnamese studios are beginning to explore mid-core, PC, and console titles with global appeal.

At UNIMOB, we're proud to be part of Vietnam's gaming revolution. Our success with Food Fever Premium was just the beginning - we have ambitious plans to put Vietnamese game development firmly on the global map.
