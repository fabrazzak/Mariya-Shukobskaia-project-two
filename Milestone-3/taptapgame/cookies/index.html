Cookies Policy

Last Updated: May 15, 2025

This Cookies Policy explains what cookies are, how we use them, and your choices regarding cookies. Please read this policy carefully to understand our views and practices regarding cookies.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website or use an app. They are widely used to make websites and apps work more efficiently, as well as to provide information to the owners.

Cookies may be either "persistent" cookies or "session" cookies. Persistent cookies remain on your device when you go offline, while session cookies are deleted as soon as you close your web browser.

2. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for several purposes:

  • Essential Cookies: Necessary for the website to function and cannot be switched off.
  • Performance Cookies: Help us understand how visitors interact with our website.
  • Functional Cookies: Enable enhanced functionality and personalization.
  • Targeting Cookies: Used to deliver relevant ads to you.

We may also use various third-party cookies to report usage statistics and deliver advertisements:

  • Google Analytics: Helps us understand how users engage with our website.

  • Ad Networks: Used to serve relevant advertisements.

These third-party services may use cookies to collect information about your activities on this and other websites.

5. Your Cookie Choices

You have several options to control or limit how we and our partners use cookies:

  • Browser Controls: Most browsers allow you to refuse or accept cookies. Refer to your browser's help documentation for instructions.
  • Opt-Out Tools: The Digital Advertising Alliance and Network Advertising Initiative provide opt-out tools for targeted advertising.
  • Mobile Settings: Your mobile device may include settings to limit ad tracking.
  • Our Consent Banner: When you first visit our website, you can choose which types of cookies to accept.

Please note that disabling cookies may affect your experience and some features may not work properly.
