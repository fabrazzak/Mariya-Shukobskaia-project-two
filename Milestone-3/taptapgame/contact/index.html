Frequently Asked Questions

How do I report a bug?

Please use the contact form above to report any bugs you encounter. Include details about your device, game version, and steps to reproduce the issue. Screenshots or screen recordings are very helpful!

Can I suggest new features for TapTap?

Absolutely! We welcome all feature suggestions. Use the contact form and select "Game Feedback" as your subject. The most popular suggestions may be implemented in future updates.

Do you offer refunds for in-app purchases?

All purchases are processed through the Apple App Store or Google Play Store. Refund requests must be made through those platforms according to their policies.

How can I advertise with TapTap?

For advertising and partnership opportunities, please contact us using the form and select "Business Inquiry" as your subject. Include details about your proposal.
