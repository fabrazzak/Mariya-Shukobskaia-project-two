How TapTap Works

1

Watch the Ball

A vibrant ball rotates smoothly in the center of your screen, moving in a mesmerizing circular pattern. Your focus begins here.

2

Follow the Line

A dynamic target line appears and shifts positions randomly, testing your reflexes and keeping you alert at every moment.

3

Tap at the Right Moment

Precision matters! Tap exactly when the spinning ball aligns perfectly with the target line to earn points and boost your accuracy.

4

Beat Your High Score

Every successful tap adds to your total. Stay focused and keep tapping to break your personal record and climb the leaderboard!
