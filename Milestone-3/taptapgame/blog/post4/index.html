Over 10,000 players participated in our first global community challenge

Our first-ever TapTap Community Challenge has concluded, and what an incredible event it was! For two weeks, players from around the world competed to set the highest scores and win amazing prizes. The participation exceeded all our expectations, with over 10,000 entries across iOS and Android. Today, we're excited to share the results, highlight the winners, and showcase some of the most impressive performances from this historic event.
