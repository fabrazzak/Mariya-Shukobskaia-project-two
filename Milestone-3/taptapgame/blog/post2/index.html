New Game Modes

Version 2.0 introduces two exciting new ways to play TapTap, each offering unique challenges and rewards.

1. Time Attack Mode

Race against the clock in this intense new mode where speed is everything. Key features include:

  • 60-second rounds with increasing difficulty
  • Special time bonuses for perfect taps
  • Global leaderboards for fastest times
  • New power-ups that affect time flow

Pro Tip: The line changes position more frequently as time winds down, so stay focused!

2. Zen Mode

For players who prefer a more relaxed experience, Zen Mode removes the pressure while maintaining the fun:

  • No game-over for missed taps
  • Soothing color schemes and sounds
  • Focuses on perfecting your rhythm
  • Tracks consecutive perfect taps

Pro Tip: Great for practicing your timing without pressure before switching to competitive modes.
