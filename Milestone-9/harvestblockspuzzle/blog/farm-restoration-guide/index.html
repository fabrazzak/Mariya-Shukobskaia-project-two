Farm Restoration Guide: Unlocking All Locations

Published on January 15, 2025

One of the most rewarding aspects of Harvest Block is restoring the charming farm locations scattered throughout your land. Each restored area unlocks new gameplay elements, visual upgrades, and sometimes even special rewards. This comprehensive guide will walk you through every farm location, how to unlock them, and what benefits they provide.

The Farm Restoration System

As you progress through Harvest Block, you'll discover various dilapidated farm locations. Restoring these requires completing specific puzzle levels and sometimes meeting additional requirements. Here's how the system works:

  • Each location has a restoration progress meter
  • Completing associated levels fills the meter
  • Some locations require collecting special items during gameplay
  • Fully restored locations provide permanent benefits

The Stable (First Restoration)

Unlock Requirement: Complete Level 10

Restoration Tasks:

  • Clear 20 rows containing hay bale blocks
  • Collect 15 horseshoes from matching near the stable area

Benefits:

  • Unlocks the Horse power-up (occasional extra moves)
  • Adds horse animations to your farm
  • Grants 500 bonus coins upon completion

The Apiary

Unlock Requirement: Complete Level 25

Restoration Tasks:

  • Match 50 honeycomb blocks
  • Collect 30 bees during gameplay (random appearances)
  • Complete 3 levels without using power-ups

Benefits:

  • Unlocks Honey power-up (slows block arrival for 5 moves)
  • Adds beehives and pollinating bees to your farm scenery
  • Increases flower spawn rate by 15%

Mid-Game Locations

After restoring the initial locations, you'll gain access to more specialized farm areas that offer greater rewards.

The Workshop

Unlock Requirement: Restore both Stable and Apiary

Restoration Tasks:

  • Use Hammer power-up 25 times near workshop area
  • Collect 40 wooden planks (from clearing wooden crate obstacles)
  • Complete 5 levels with 3+ Saw Blade uses

Benefits:

  • Unlocks Workshop Tools (permanent +1 to all power-up capacities)
  • Adds animated crafting scenes to your farm
  • Occasionally provides free power-ups after certain levels

The Mill

Unlock Requirement: Reach Level 50

Restoration Tasks:

  • Collect 60 sacks of flour (from windmill-themed blocks)
  • Complete 10 levels with Windmill power-up
  • Score 5,000+ points in a single level

Benefits:

  • Unlocks Wind Boost (occasional free Windmill shuffles)
  • Adds working windmill animation to your farm
  • Grants 1,000 bonus coins and rare farm decoration

Late-Game Locations

These prestigious locations require significant progress but offer powerful rewards.

Chicken Coop

Unlock Requirement: Restore 4 previous locations

Restoration Tasks:

  • Collect 100 eggs (from white blocks matched vertically)
  • Complete 15 levels without losing a single chicken (special blocks)
  • Score 3 consecutive levels with 3,000+ points each

Benefits:

  • Unlocks Egg Surprise (random bonus after some levels)
  • Adds animated chickens roaming your farm
  • Provides daily bonus of 50 coins

Wine Cellar

Unlock Requirement: Reach Level 100

Restoration Tasks:

  • Collect 80 grapes (from purple blocks matched in L-shapes)
  • Complete 5 levels with perfect clears (no blocks left)
  • Use Darts power-up to pop 25 cork obstacles

Benefits:

  • Unlocks Vintage Bonus (multiplies end-of-level coins occasionally)
  • Adds elegant vineyard visuals to your farm
  • Grants 2,000 bonus coins and exclusive decoration

Restoration Tips & Strategies

Restoring all farm locations requires planning and strategy. Here are some expert tips:

Prioritization

  • Focus on locations that provide power-ups first (Stable, Apiary, Workshop)
  • The Mill should be a mid-game priority for its coin bonuses
  • Save Wine Cellar for last as it's mostly cosmetic

Efficient Collection

  • When you need specific items, replay earlier levels that feature them prominently
  • Use power-ups strategically to maximize collection of restoration items
  • Pay attention to level previews - some are better for certain collection tasks

Event Synergy

  • Special events often provide bonus restoration items
  • Time your restoration pushes during relevant events (e.g., Grape Harvest helps with Wine Cellar)
  • Check the event calendar for opportunities
