Event Preview

Upcoming Events Calendar for 2025

Published on January 5, 2025

Harvest Block's event system brings fresh challenges and special rewards throughout the year. This complete 2025 calendar reveals all the exciting events planned, including returning favorites and brand new activities. Mark your calendars and prepare for a year of farming puzzle fun!

Seasonal Events

Our seasonal events celebrate the changing harvests and farming activities throughout the year. These week-long events feature special mechanics and exclusive rewards.

Spring Blossom Festival (March 20-27)

Event Mechanics:

  • Special flower blocks appear on the board
  • Matching near flowers causes them to bloom
  • Bloomed flowers release pollen that clears adjacent blocks

Rewards:

  • Spring-themed farm decorations
  • Exclusive Bee Keeper avatar
  • Bonus coins for each flower bloomed

Summer Sunshine Celebration (June 21-28)

Event Mechanics:

  • Sun blocks appear that charge when adjacent to matches
  • Fully charged suns explode, clearing a 3x3 area
  • Special lemonade stand gives temporary power-up boosts

Rewards:

  • Summer fruit-themed block skins
  • Sun Hat avatar accessory
  • Double coin weekends during event

Monthly Special Events

These recurring events offer unique gameplay twists and competitive elements.

Farm Races (First weekend of each month)

Event Mechanics:

  • Compete against other players in real-time
  • Complete puzzle boards as quickly as possible
  • Special speed boosts available during races
  • Leaderboards with tiered rewards

Rewards:

  • Trophy decorations for your farm
  • Exclusive vehicle skins for your farmer
  • Bonus coins based on finishing position

Fishing Time (Third Wednesday of each month)

Event Mechanics:

  • Special fishing rod power-up replaces one regular power-up
  • Catch fish by matching near water blocks
  • Different fish species have different point values
  • Rare golden fish appear occasionally

Rewards:

  • Aquarium decoration showing your catches
  • Fishing gear avatars
  • Bonus coins for each fish caught

New for 2025

We're introducing several brand new events this year based on player feedback.

Strawberry Jam (February 10-17)

Event Mechanics:

  • Special strawberry blocks appear on the board
  • Collect strawberries by clearing rows containing them
  • Jam meter fills as you collect strawberries
  • Full jam meter triggers a board-clearing jam explosion

Rewards:

  • Strawberry-themed farm decorations
  • Jam Maker title for your profile
  • Special rewards at collection milestones

Winter Market (December 15-22)

Event Mechanics:

  • Special holiday market stalls appear on the board edges
  • Deliver matching blocks to stalls to "sell" them
  • Different stalls request different colored blocks
  • Complete orders to earn holiday tokens

Rewards:

  • Festive farm decorations
  • Holiday-themed block skins
  • Exchange tokens for special items in the event shop

Event Participation Tips

Make the most of every event with these expert strategies:

Preparation

  • Check event requirements in advance and stock up on relevant power-ups
  • Complete farm restorations that provide event bonuses
  • Clear your schedule for time-sensitive events like Farm Races

During Events

  • Focus on the event mechanics rather than regular gameplay
  • Watch for event-specific power-up combinations
  • Check leaderboards regularly to gauge your progress

Reward Maximization

  • Prioritize event-specific rewards over regular coins
  • Complete all event milestones even after getting the main reward
  • Save event-exclusive decorations for farm areas you showcase
