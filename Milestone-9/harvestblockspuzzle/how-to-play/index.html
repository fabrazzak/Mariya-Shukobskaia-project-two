How to Play Harvest Block

Master the unique blend of block puzzle and match-3 challenges with our comprehensive guide. Learn the rules, strategies, and tips to become a farming puzzle expert!

Basic Gameplay

Harvest Block combines the strategic thinking of block puzzles with the satisfying matching of match-3 games. Your goal is to restore the farm by solving puzzles across different locations.

1

Drag and Drop Blocks

Select colored tile blocks from the bottom and place them on the board by dragging and dropping.

2

Match and Clear

Fill complete rows or columns to make blocks disappear and score points.

3

Clear Obstacles

Remove pests, wooden crates, and ice blocks to progress through levels.

Advanced Strategies

Plan Ahead

Always look at the upcoming blocks in the queue. Plan your moves 2-3 steps ahead to maximize space efficiency.

Corner Strategy

Start placing blocks in corners and work your way inward. This gives you more flexibility as the board fills up.

Obstacle Priority

Always clear obstacles first. They take up valuable space and often block your ability to complete rows or columns.

Power-Up Timing

Save power-ups for critical moments. Using them too early might leave you without options when you really need them.

Combo Potential

Look for placements that could create multiple clears with a single block. This maximizes your score and efficiency.

Farm Focus

In Adventure mode, prioritize restoring farm buildings that give you permanent bonuses or new abilities.
