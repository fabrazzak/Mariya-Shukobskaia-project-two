About Harvest Block

Discover the story behind our unique blend of block puzzle and farm adventure gameplay

The Game Concept

Harvest Block was born from our love of classic block puzzle games and the relaxing charm of farming simulators. We wanted to create something that combined strategic thinking with the satisfaction of watching your efforts blossom into something beautiful.

The game's unique mechanics evolved from countless prototypes and player feedback sessions. Our goal was to make a puzzle game that felt familiar yet fresh, challenging yet accessible, and most importantly - fun for players of all ages.

The farm restoration theme adds purpose to the puzzles, giving players a sense of progression beyond just high scores. Each restored building tells a story and unlocks new gameplay possibilities.

Development Journey

Concept Phase

January 2024

Initial brainstorming and prototyping of the core block puzzle mechanics combined with farm progression elements.

First Playable

March 2024

Developed the basic gameplay loop with placeholder art. Conducted first round of player testing with focus groups.

Art Direction

May 2024

Finalized the vibrant farm aesthetic and character designs. Created all in-game assets and animations.

Beta Testing

October 2024

Soft launch in select markets to gather player feedback and balance game difficulty.

Global Launch

January 2025

Official worldwide release on Google Play with all planned features and content.

About Funmatica LTD

Funmatica LTD is an independent game studio based in Austria, specializing in creative puzzle and casual games for mobile platforms. Founded in 2018, our team of 15 passionate game developers believes in creating experiences that are easy to pick up but hard to put down.

We focus on innovative gameplay mechanics wrapped in polished, accessible packages. Our philosophy is that great games should challenge your mind while bringing you joy.
