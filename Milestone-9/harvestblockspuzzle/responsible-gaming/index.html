Responsible Gaming

Our commitment to healthy and enjoyable gaming experiences

At Funmatica LTD, we're committed to ensuring that Harvest Block: Blocks Puzzle remains an enjoyable and positive experience for all players. While gaming can be a fun and rewarding activity, we recognize the importance of maintaining a healthy balance with other aspects of life.

Our Commitment

We design our games to be entertaining and engaging, but we also take seriously our responsibility to promote healthy gaming habits. Our games are intended for entertainment purposes only and should not interfere with daily responsibilities, relationships, or wellbeing.

Warning Signs of Problem Gaming

Gaming may become a concern if it leads to:

  • Neglecting work, school, or family obligations
  • Losing track of time while playing
  • Using gaming to escape from real-life problems
  • Becoming irritable when not able to play
  • Spending money beyond your means on in-game purchases

Healthy Gaming Tips

To maintain a healthy relationship with gaming, we recommend:

  • Set time limits: Use device timers or parental controls to manage play time
  • Take regular breaks: Follow the 20-20-20 rule (every 20 minutes, look at something 20 feet away for 20 seconds)
  • Balance activities: Make sure gaming doesn't replace physical activity, social interaction, or other hobbies
  • Monitor spending: Set a budget for in-game purchases and stick to it
  • Play with purpose: Enjoy the game as entertainment, not as an escape from real-life challenges

Parental Controls

We encourage parents to be actively involved in their children's gaming experiences. Most devices and platforms offer parental control features that can help manage:

  • Play time limits
  • Purchase restrictions
  • Content filters
  • Social interaction controls

For instructions on setting up parental controls, please refer to your device manufacturer's guidelines or platform-specific instructions.

In-Game Purchases

Harvest Block: Blocks Puzzle is free to download and play, but offers optional in-game purchases. To prevent unintended purchases:

  • Use device-level purchase authentication (like fingerprint or password requirements)
  • Consider using gift cards instead of credit cards for in-game purchases
  • Regularly review your purchase history

You can manage your in-app purchase settings through your device's app store settings.
