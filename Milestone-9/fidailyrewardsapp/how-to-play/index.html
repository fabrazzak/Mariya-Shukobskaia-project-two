How to Play FI Daily Rewards

Follow this step-by-step guide to maximize your rewards and enhance your Family Island gaming experience.

Download Now
1

Download the App

Install FI Daily Rewards from the Google Play Store on your Android device. The app is compatible with all devices running Android 5.0 and up.

Go to Google Play Store
2

Launch the App

Open the FI Daily Rewards app after installation. You'll see the main dashboard showing available rewards for the day.

3

Check Available Rewards

Browse through the available rewards which may include free energy, rubies, or other bonuses for Family Island.

  • Energy rewards help you continue playing without waiting
  • Rubies can be used to speed up tasks or buy premium items
  • Special bonuses may be available during events
4

Claim Your Rewards

Tap on any reward link to claim it. This will open your Family Island game where the reward will be automatically applied to your account.

Pro Tip

Make sure your Family Island game is updated to the latest version before claiming rewards. Some rewards may not work with outdated versions of the game.

5

Enable Notifications

Turn on notifications to get alerts when new rewards become available. This ensures you never miss out on daily bonuses.

Notification Settings

Go to app settings > notifications and enable all notification types for best results.

Best Times

New rewards are typically added at 12:00 AM UTC. Check daily for maximum benefits.
