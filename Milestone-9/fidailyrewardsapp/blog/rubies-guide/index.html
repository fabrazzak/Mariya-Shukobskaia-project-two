The Ultimate Guide to Earning & Spending Rubies in Family Island

Master the art of ruby management to accelerate your progress and unlock premium content in Family Island.

Rubies are the premium currency in Family Island, offering shortcuts to valuable resources, decorations, and time-saving boosts. Unlike energy which replenishes over time, rubies are much harder to come by, making their management crucial for efficient gameplay. This comprehensive guide will teach you how to maximize your ruby earnings and spend them wisely for optimal progress.

Understanding Rubies in Family Island

Rubies serve multiple purposes in Family Island, from purchasing rare items to speeding up production. Before diving into earning strategies, it's important to understand their core uses:

  • Accelerating Production: Speed up buildings, crops, and crafting
  • Purchasing Premium Items: Buy decorations, outfits, and special buildings
  • Resource Conversion: Exchange rubies for hard-to-find materials
  • Event Advantages: Gain edges in time-limited events
  • Inventory Expansion: Increase your storage capacity

1. Top Methods to Earn Rubies

a) Complete Story Quests

The main storyline is the most reliable source of rubies in early and mid-game. Each major quest completion typically rewards 5-15 rubies. Focus on:

  • Main island storyline quests
  • Special character questlines
  • Island expansion milestones

b) Participate in Events

Limited-time events often offer rubies as rewards for reaching certain milestones:

  • Expedition Events: Complete island tasks for ruby rewards
  • Seasonal Events: Holiday-themed activities with ruby prizes
  • Leaderboard Events: Compete against others for top ruby rewards
Pro Tip: Save your energy before major events start to maximize your participation and ruby earnings potential.

c) Daily Login Rewards

The game rewards consistent players with rubies through:

  • 7-day login streaks (often includes 10-20 rubies)
  • Monthly calendar rewards
  • Special holiday login bonuses

2. Smart Ruby Spending Strategies

Earning rubies is only half the battle - spending them wisely is equally important. Follow these guidelines for optimal ruby expenditure:

a) Priority Purchases (High Value)

  • Permanent Upgrades: Inventory expansions and production boosts
  • Event Final Rewards: When you're close to completing an event's reward track
  • Time-Sensitive Deals: Limited-time offers with significant discounts

b) Moderate Purchases (Situational Value)

  • Rare Materials: When you're stuck on a quest and need specific items
  • Event Speedups: To complete time-limited challenges
  • Decoration Bundles: For players focused on island aesthetics

c) Low Priority (Avoid Unless Necessary)

  • Common Resources: Items you can easily gather yourself
  • Excessive Speedups: Unless for critical event completion
  • Cosmetics Early Game: Save these for when you're more established

3. Advanced Ruby Management Techniques

a) The 24-Hour Rule

Before making any ruby purchase over 20 rubies, wait 24 hours. This prevents impulse spending on items you might not actually need.

b) Energy vs. Ruby Value

Calculate whether it's better to spend rubies or wait for energy replenishment. As a general rule:

  • If the task would take less than 4 hours of energy regeneration, wait
  • If it's for an event ending soon, consider using rubies

c) Ruby-to-Resource Conversion Rates

Keep mental notes of standard conversion rates to identify good deals:

  • 10 rubies = ~50 energy (standard rate)
  • 20 rubies = ~3 rare materials
  • 50 rubies = ~1 premium decoration

4. Event-Specific Ruby Strategies

Special events often provide unique ruby opportunities:

a) Expedition Events

These multi-day events typically offer rubies at these milestones:

  • Complete 25% of tasks: 5 rubies
  • Complete 50% of tasks: 10 rubies
  • Complete 100% of tasks: 20+ rubies

b) Leaderboard Events

Competitive events reward top performers with rubies:

  • Top 3: 50-100 rubies
  • Top 10: 20-50 rubies
  • Top 50: 10-20 rubies
Advanced Tip: Track event schedules and save rubies to invest in events with the best return on investment.

5. Long-Term Ruby Accumulation

Building a substantial ruby reserve requires consistent habits:

  • Daily Minimum: Earn at least 5 rubies per day through various methods
  • Weekly Goals: Aim for 50+ rubies per week
  • Monthly Targets: Accumulate 200+ rubies for major purchases
