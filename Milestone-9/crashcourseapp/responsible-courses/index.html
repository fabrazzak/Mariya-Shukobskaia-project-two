Responsible courses Policy

At Crash Course, we're committed to providing a safe, balanced, and responsible learning environment for all our users.

Our Policy Help Resources

Our Commitment

We believe learning should be engaging but never compulsive. Here's how we promote responsible use of our app.

Age-Appropriate Content

All our content is rated for ages 3+ and carefully designed to be educational, not addictive. We don't employ manipulative design patterns.

Transparent Purchases

Any in-app purchases are clearly marked and never required for core educational content. We provide detailed receipts and easy purchase history access.

Usage Monitoring

Our app includes optional usage tracking to help you maintain healthy learning habits, with reminders to take breaks during extended sessions.

Parental Controls

We provide tools for parents to monitor and limit app usage, set educational goals, and control in-app purchases through family sharing settings.

No Pay-to-Learn

All core educational content remains completely free. Premium features only enhance the experience but never gate essential learning materials.

Data Privacy

We collect minimal data and never sell user information. All data collection is transparent and can be opted out of in settings.

Recognizing Unhealthy Patterns

While our app is designed to be educational rather than addictive, it's important to maintain balance. Be aware of these warning signs:

Prioritizing app use over responsibilities or relationships

Difficulty stopping even when you want to take a break

Neglecting sleep, meals, or hygiene to continue using the app

Feeling restless or irritable when not using the app

Spending more money than intended on in-app purchases

Healthy Learning Habits

To maintain a balanced approach to learning with our app, we recommend:

1

Set clear time limits for app usage each day

2

Take regular breaks (we suggest the 50/10 rule: 50 minutes learning, 10 minutes break)

3

Balance app use with other learning methods and physical activities

4

Use our app's built-in usage tracking to monitor your habits

5

Set learning goals rather than time-based goals
