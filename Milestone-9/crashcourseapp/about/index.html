Our Story

Crash Course was founded in 2012 with a simple idea: education should be free, high-quality, and most importantly, engaging. What started as a YouTube channel with a handful of science videos has grown into a global educational platform with millions of learners.

Our team of educators, animators, and content creators work tirelessly to produce courses that accompany high school and college-level classes ranging from the humanities to the sciences. We're proud to have built a community that believes, like us, that learning should be fun, engaging, and thoughtful.

The Crash Course app was launched in 2020 to bring our educational content directly to your mobile device, complete with supplemental flashcards and quizzes to reinforce your learning.

Our Educational Philosophy

We approach education differently because we believe learning should be different.

Accessibility First

We remove financial and geographical barriers to education. Our content is free, available worldwide, and optimized for mobile learning.

Engagement Through Fun

Learning should be enjoyable. We use humor, storytelling, and high-quality animations to make complex topics approachable and memorable.

Reinforcement Learning

Knowledge sticks when it's actively used. Our flashcards and quizzes help reinforce concepts through spaced repetition and active recall.
