Responsible Gaming

Enjoy Tap Away 3D Puzzle in a healthy, balanced way with our player-first guidelines.

Healthy Gaming Habits

At Kusya Games, we believe gaming should be fun, engaging, and balanced. Here are our recommendations for maintaining a healthy relationship with gaming:

Set Time Limits

Use our in-game timer or your device's screen time settings to manage play sessions. Regular breaks improve both enjoyment and performance.

Prioritize Real-World Activities

Balance gaming with physical activity, social interactions, and other hobbies. Games complement life, they shouldn't replace it.

Be Aware of Spending

Use parental controls if needed and set budgets for in-app purchases. All purchases are optional and never required to progress.
