What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit websites. They help the site remember information about your visit, which can make it easier to visit again and make the site more useful.

We use both session cookies (which expire when you close your browser) and persistent cookies (which stay on your device for a set period or until deleted).

How We Use Cookies

Tap Away 3D Puzzle uses cookies for:

  • Essential Operations: Keeping you logged in, maintaining game progress
  • Performance: Understanding how players interact with our game
  • Analytics: Improving game design based on usage patterns
  • Preferences: Remembering your settings and choices

Managing Cookie Preferences

You can control and/or delete cookies as you wish. Most web browsers allow some control through their settings:

  • Browser Settings: Typically found in "Options" or "Preferences"
  • Device Settings: For mobile devices, check your OS settings
  • Our Cookie Manager: Use the controls at the top of this page

Note that disabling cookies may affect your ability to use certain features of our game.
