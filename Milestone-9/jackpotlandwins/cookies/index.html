2. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device when you visit a website or use an app. They are widely used to make websites and apps work more efficiently, as well as to provide information to the owners of the site or app.

Cookies may be either "persistent" cookies or "session" cookies. Persistent cookies remain on your device for the period of time specified in the cookie, while session cookies are deleted when you close your browser or app.
