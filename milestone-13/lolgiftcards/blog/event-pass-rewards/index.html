Ranked Climbing Guide
Season 13 Mastery

The ultimate guide to climbing the ranked ladder in League of Legends Season 13 with proven strategies from challenger players.

July 22, 2025 â€¢ 12 min read

Climbing the ranked ladder in League of Legends can feel like an insurmountable challenge, especially with the ever-changing meta of Season 13. Whether you're stuck in Iron or pushing for Challenger, this comprehensive guide will provide you with the strategies, champion recommendations, and mental frameworks you need to consistently win games and climb ranks.

Understanding the Season 13 Meta

Season 13 has brought significant changes to the jungle, itemization, and objective control that every ranked player needs to understand:

  • Jungle Pets: The new jungle companion system has shifted power towards farming junglers who can scale effectively.
  • Objective Bounties: These have been adjusted to create more comeback opportunities for teams that fall behind early.
  • Item Changes: Several mythic items have been reworked, particularly for ADCs and bruisers.
  • Baron Changes: The Voidgrubs now provide permanent buffs that can snowball games when secured early.

Key Insight: The current meta favors champions with strong mid-game power spikes who can take advantage of the new Voidgrub objectives. Teams that prioritize these 5-minute objectives tend to have higher win rates across all elos.

Champion Selection for Optimal Climbing

Your champion pool is the foundation of your ranked success. Here are the most effective champions for climbing in each role during Season 13:

Top Lane

The island of top lane remains crucial for split-pushing and teleport plays. Current standout champions include:

  • Jax: Dominates with his reworked ultimate and scales incredibly well.
  • K'Sante: Provides incredible outplay potential and teamfight presence.
  • Malphite: Counters AD-heavy compositions and has simple execution.

Limit your champion pool to 2-3 champions per role. Mastery of a small pool yields better results than playing many champions at a mediocre level.

Jungle

Junglers who can efficiently clear and contest Voidgrubs are dominating:

  • Maokai: Tanky with incredible gank potential and objective control.
  • Bel'Veth: Scales infinitely and dominates late-game teamfights.
  • Zac: Consistent ganks and teamfight presence at all stages.

Mid Lane

Mid lane remains the most influential role for solo queue climbing:

  • Aurelion Sol: After his rework, his scaling is unmatched in the current meta.
  • Annie: Simple mechanics with devastating teamfight potential.
  • Zed: Continues to dominate with his snowball potential.

Bot Lane (ADC)

Marksmen who spike at two items are currently strongest:

  • Jinx: Resets make her teamfight cleanup unparalleled.
  • Kai'Sa: Flexible build paths adapt to any situation.
  • Ezreal: Safe pick that scales well into late game.

Support

Engage supports dominate the current meta:

  • Nautilus: Reliable CC and tankiness.
  • Rakan: Mobile playmaking potential.
  • Renata Glasc: Counters engage and enables hypercarries.

Macro Play: The Key to Consistent Wins

Mechanics will only take you so far. To truly climb, you must master macro play:

Early Game (0-15 minutes)

  • Prioritize Voidgrubs over early dragons when possible
  • Track enemy jungler and ping their location
  • Focus on CS while looking for favorable trades

Mid Game (15-25 minutes)

  • Rotate to important objectives 1 minute before spawn
  • Create slow pushes in side lanes before grouping
  • Secure vision around Baron pit

Late Game (25+ minutes)

  • Never facecheck bushes without vision
  • Play around your strongest member's power spikes
  • Bait Baron to force favorable fights

Pro Tip: The minimap is your most important tool. Glance at it every 3-5 seconds to track enemy movements and make informed decisions.

Mental Game and Consistency

Your mindset is just as important as your mechanics:

  • Mute toxic players immediately - They provide no value and will tilt you
  • Take breaks after losses - Playing while tilted leads to more losses
  • Focus on improvement, not LP - The ranks will come with consistent play
  • Review your games - Identify 1-2 mistakes to fix each session

Advanced Strategies for Each Rank

Iron to Silver

  • Focus on last hitting - aim for 6+ CS per minute
  • Play simple champions to master fundamentals
  • Group for every objective after laning phase

Gold to Platinum

  • Learn wave management concepts
  • Track enemy summoner spell cooldowns
  • Start coordinating with your jungler

Diamond+

  • Master matchup knowledge for your main champions
  • Optimize recall timings and item spikes
  • Control vision around key objectives

Common Mistakes to Avoid

After analyzing thousands of ranked games, these are the most common mistakes holding players back:

  • Overstaying after objectives: Get what you can and reset
  • Facechecking bushes: Always use abilities or wards first
  • Fighting without vision: No vision = assume enemies are there
  • Tunneling on kills: Objectives win games, not kill counts
  • Ignoring side waves: Letting waves crash loses games

Record your games and review them later. You'll spot mistakes you didn't notice in the heat of the moment.

  • Victorious Anivia: Earn this skin by reaching Gold+
  • Ranked Borders: Display your highest achieved rank
  • Eternals: Exclusive ranked series eternals
  • Chroma Variants: For players reaching Platinum+

Final Thoughts

Climbing in League of Legends is a marathon, not a sprint. By focusing on consistent improvement rather than short-term LP gains, you'll find yourself steadily rising through the ranks. Remember these key takeaways:

  • Limit your champion pool and master your picks
  • Develop strong macro awareness and decision-making
  • Maintain a positive mental attitude and avoid tilt
  • Focus on objectives over kills
  • Review your games to identify areas for improvement

With these strategies and the right mindset, you'll be well on your way to reaching your ranked goals in Season 13. The climb may be challenging, but the satisfaction of seeing your skills and rank improve is worth the effort.
