How to Get the Most RP
for Your Money

Maximize your League of Legends purchases with our comprehensive guide to getting the best value from your RP.

RP Expert • July 8, 2025 • 6 min read

Riot Points (RP) are the lifeblood of League of Legends cosmetics, but they can get expensive if you're not careful. Whether you're looking to buy skins, champions, or event passes, getting the most value from your RP is crucial. This guide will show you exactly how to stretch your RP further and get more bang for your buck.

Understanding RP Pricing

Before we dive into saving strategies, it's important to understand how RP pricing works. Riot Games offers RP in several denominations, with larger bundles offering better value per dollar.

RP Amount USD Price RP per Dollar Bonus RP
650 RP $5.00 130 RP/$ -
1380 RP $10.00 138 RP/$ -
2800 RP $20.00 140 RP/$ -
5000 RP $35.00 143 RP/$ +150 RP
7200 RP $50.00 144 RP/$ +350 RP

As you can see, the $50 bundle gives you the best value at 144 RP per dollar, plus an additional 350 RP bonus. That's nearly 15% more RP than if you bought the equivalent in $10 bundles.

Best Ways to Buy RP

1. Purchase Larger Bundles

As shown in the table above, buying larger RP bundles gives you more RP per dollar spent. If you know you'll be spending RP over time, it makes financial sense to purchase the largest bundle you can afford.

2. Use Gift Cards for Discounts

One of the best ways to save on RP is by purchasing League of Legends gift cards when they go on sale. Many retailers offer discounts of 10-20% on gift cards throughout the year:

  • Amazon: Frequently offers $10 gift cards for $8-9 during sales
  • Best Buy: Often includes gift cards as part of bonus promotions
  • PayPal Digital Gifts: Regular discounts on various denominations
"I save an average of 15% on all my RP purchases by stocking up on discounted gift cards during sales. It adds up to hundreds of dollars saved each year." - Platinum-tier player

3. Take Advantage of Regional Pricing

RP prices vary by region due to currency exchange rates and local pricing strategies. Some players have found savings by:

  • Purchasing RP through the Turkish or Brazilian servers (requires account region change)
  • Using VPNs to access regional pricing (though this violates Riot's TOS)
  • Gifting between accounts in different regions

Important: Some of these methods may violate Riot's Terms of Service. Proceed at your own risk.

Maximizing Your RP Value

Now that you've acquired your RP at the best possible price, here's how to make it go even further:

1. Wait for Sales

Riot regularly holds several types of sales that can save you RP:

  • Weekly Sales: 30-50% off selected skins each week
  • Your Shop: Personalized discounts up to 70% off
  • Event Sales: Special discounts during major events
  • End-of-Season Sales: Deep discounts at season's end

2. Purchase Bundles Instead of Individual Skins

Skin bundles often offer significant savings compared to buying items individually. For example:

Bundle Individual Price Bundle Price Savings
Star Guardian 2025 Set 12,620 RP 9,900 RP 2,720 RP (21.5%)
PROJECT: Overdrive Set 10,290 RP 7,950 RP 2,340 RP (22.7%)
Battle Academia Set 7,840 RP 6,250 RP 1,590 RP (20.3%)

3. Use Hextech Crafting Wisely

The Hextech Crafting system allows you to earn skins and champions for free. To maximize its value:

  • Disenchant duplicate champion shards for Blue Essence
  • Reroll three skin shards into a permanent skin (great for cheap skins)
  • Use Orange Essence to unlock skin shards you really want
  • Save Gemstones for exclusive Hextech skins

4. Participate in Events

League's regular events often provide opportunities to earn free RP or discounted content:

  • Complete event missions for free loot
  • Earn tokens to redeem for skins or chromas
  • Take advantage of event-specific discounts
  • Purchase event passes for maximum rewards

Advanced RP Saving Strategies

For players looking to optimize every aspect of their RP spending:

1. The Skin Waiting Game

New skins are almost never discounted at release. By waiting 3-6 months, you can often:

  • Find the skin in Your Shop for 30-70% off
  • Catch it in a weekly sale
  • Get it as part of a bundle
  • Receive it from Hextech Crafting

2. Champion Shard Strategy

Instead of buying champions with RP:

  1. Use Blue Essence to buy champion shards when they're on sale
  2. Wait until you get the champion you want from a chest
  3. Use the shard to unlock the champion at a discount

This can save you thousands of RP over time.

3. Account Boosting

While controversial, some players create smurf accounts to:

  • Take advantage of new player rewards
  • Get better Your Shop discounts
  • Earn easy chests in low-level games

Final Tips for RP Efficiency

  • Set a monthly RP budget and stick to it
  • Prioritize skins you'll actually use rather than collecting everything
  • Take advantage of refund tokens for accidental purchases
  • Consider gifting when there are bonus RP promotions
  • Follow Riot's official channels for surprise giveaways

By combining these strategies, you can easily save 30-50% on your League of Legends cosmetics while still getting all the skins and content you want. Remember that the best value comes from patience and smart purchasing rather than impulse buys.
