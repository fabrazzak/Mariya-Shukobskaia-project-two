Recognizing Problem Gaming

Be aware of these warning signs that gaming may be becoming problematic

Behavioral Signs

  • Spending more time or virtual coins than intended
  • Neglecting work, studies, or personal relationships
  • Using gaming to escape from problems or negative feelings
  • Lying to others about gaming activities

Emotional Signs

  • Feeling restless or irritable when not gaming
  • Experiencing guilt or shame about gaming habits
  • Failed attempts to control or stop gaming
  • Gaming to relieve anxiety or depression

If you recognize these signs in yourself or someone you know, consider using our responsible gaming tools or seeking help from the resources below.

Gaming Habits Self-Assessment

Answer these questions honestly to assess your gaming habits:

1. Have you ever felt the need to game for increasing amounts of time?

2. Do you feel restless or irritable when attempting to cut down or stop gaming?

3. Have you lied to family members or others to conceal your gaming?
