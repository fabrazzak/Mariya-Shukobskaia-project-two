Recognizing Problem Gaming

While our platform is for entertainment only, it's important to be aware of the signs of problematic gaming behavior:

  • Spending more time or virtual currency on gaming than intended
  • Neglecting work, school, or family responsibilities due to gaming
  • Using gaming as a way to escape from problems or negative feelings
  • Lying to others about the amount of time spent gaming
  • Feeling restless or irritable when attempting to cut down on gaming

If you recognize these signs in yourself or someone you know, we encourage you to seek help from professional organizations listed below.
