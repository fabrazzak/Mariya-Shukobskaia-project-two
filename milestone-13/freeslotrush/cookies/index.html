2. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer, smartphone, or other device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently, as well as to provide information to the website owners.

Cookies may be either "persistent" cookies or "session" cookies. Persistent cookies remain on your device for the period of time specified in the cookie (usually until you delete them), while session cookies are deleted when you close your browser.
