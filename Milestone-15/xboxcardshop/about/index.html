Our Story

Founded in 2020, XboxCardShop began as a small passion project by a group of gaming enthusiasts who wanted to make it easier for players to access digital content for their Xbox consoles.

What started as a side hustle quickly grew into a trusted marketplace serving thousands of customers across North America. Our commitment to instant delivery, competitive pricing, and excellent customer service has been the cornerstone of our success.

Today, we're proud to be one of the leading providers of Xbox digital content, offering everything from gift cards to Game Pass subscriptions, all delivered instantly to your inbox.
