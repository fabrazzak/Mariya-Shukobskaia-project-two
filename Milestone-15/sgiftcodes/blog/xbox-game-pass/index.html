Xbox Game Pass July 2025 - Complete Games Lineup and Highlights

Microsoft has announced the Xbox Game Pass lineup for July 2025, featuring an impressive selection of day-one releases, AAA blockbusters, and indie gems. This month's additions continue to demonstrate why Game Pass remains the best value in gaming, with titles spanning multiple genres and playstyles.

Day One Releases

These games will be available on Game Pass the same day they launch:

Starfield: Shattered Space (July 3)

The massive expansion to Bethesda's space RPG adds new star systems, story content, and gameplay mechanics. Features include:

  • 30+ hours of new story content
  • 5 new star systems to explore
  • New ship customization options
  • Expanded base building mechanics

Contraband (July 10)

Avalanche Studios' co-op heist game set in 1970s Southeast Asia. Key features:

  • 4-player cooperative gameplay
  • Procedurally generated missions
  • Deep vehicle customization
  • Dynamic weather system

Ereban: Shadow Legacy (July 17)

A stealth platformer with a unique shadow manipulation mechanic. Highlights include:

  • Innovative light/shadow gameplay
  • Non-linear level design
  • Multiple approaches to combat
  • Stylish noir aesthetic

Notable AAA Additions

These major titles are joining the Game Pass library in July:

Assassin's Creed Mirage (July 7)

The back-to-basics Assassin's Creed entry set in 9th century Baghdad. Features:

  • Tighter, more focused gameplay
  • Return of social stealth mechanics
  • Dense urban environment
  • Complete edition with all DLC

Dead Island 2 (July 14)

The long-awaited zombie sequel brings gory combat to Game Pass. Includes:

  • Visceral melee combat system
  • Sunny Los Angeles setting
  • 8 unique character classes
  • All post-launch content

Forza Horizon 5: Rally Adventure (July 21)

The rally expansion comes to Game Pass with:

  • New off-road biome
  • 50+ rally cars
  • Co-driver navigation system
  • Multi-stage rally events

Indie Highlights

These smaller titles are worth your attention:

Cocoon (July 5)

From the lead gameplay designer of Limbo and Inside, this puzzle-adventure features:

  • Mind-bending world-within-world mechanics
  • Striking minimalist visuals
  • No text or dialogue
  • 5-6 hour focused experience

Planet of Lana (July 12)

A cinematic puzzle-platformer with:

  • Hand-painted visuals
  • Companion-based puzzles
  • Emotional storytelling
  • Original orchestral soundtrack

Viewfinder (July 19)

An innovative puzzle game where you can:

  • Turn photographs into reality
  • Manipulate perspective
  • Solve environmental puzzles
  • Experience a mind-bending narrative

Cloud Gaming Highlights

These games are particularly well-suited for Xbox Cloud Gaming:

Hi-Fi Rush (July 1)

The rhythm-action game performs perfectly on cloud with:

  • Precise timing-based combat
  • Stylish comic book visuals
  • Packed soundtrack
  • Low latency requirements

Pentiment (July 8)

The narrative-driven historical mystery offers:

  • Beautiful illuminated manuscript art
  • Deep character interactions
  • Choices with long-term consequences
  • Perfect for touch controls
"The July lineup might be the strongest Game Pass month yet, with something for every type of gamer. The day-one releases alone would cost over $150 if purchased separately." - Alex Morgan, Gaming Journalist

Games Leaving July 31

These titles will leave Game Pass at the end of July. Play them now or purchase at a 20% discount:

  • Far Cry 6
  • Marvel's Guardians of the Galaxy
  • Outriders
  • The Forgotten City
  • Undertale

Game Pass Perks

July's Game Pass perks include:

  • Free Forza Horizon 5 car pack (July 6-20)
  • Sea of Thieves cosmetic set (July 10-31)
  • Fallout 76 in-game currency (July 15-31)
  • Discord Nitro trial (July 1-31)

Tips for Getting the Most from Game Pass

  1. Try before you buy: Many games offer discounts if you decide to purchase after they leave Game Pass.
  2. Use cloud gaming: Perfect for trying games before downloading or playing on mobile devices.
  3. Check the "Leaving Soon" section: Prioritize games that will be removed from the service.
  4. Explore different genres: With so many options, it's a great time to try something new.
  5. Look for bundles: Some games include DLC or complete editions as part of Game Pass.

Final Thoughts

The July 2025 Xbox Game Pass lineup demonstrates Microsoft's continued commitment to delivering incredible value to subscribers. With major day-one releases, beloved AAA titles, and innovative indies, there's never been a better time to be a Game Pass member.

Whether you're looking for an epic RPG adventure, intense action, or thoughtful puzzles, this month's additions have you covered. The variety ensures that every type of gamer will find something to love, making the subscription fee feel like an absolute bargain.
