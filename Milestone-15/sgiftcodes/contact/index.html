Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

All digital codes are delivered instantly via email after your payment is confirmed. Typically, you'll receive your code within 1-2 minutes of completing your purchase. If you don't see it in your inbox, please check your spam folder.

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), PayPal, and select cryptocurrencies (Bitcoin, Ethereum). All payments are processed securely through our encrypted payment gateway.

Most of our codes are region-free, but some may have regional restrictions. The product page will clearly indicate if a code is region-specific. If you're unsure, please contact our support team before purchasing.

Due to the digital nature of our products, we generally don't offer refunds once a code has been delivered. However, if you encounter any issues with your code, please contact our support team immediately and we'll work to resolve the problem.

Since all our products are delivered digitally via email, there's no physical tracking. You'll receive your code in your email immediately after purchase. If you don't see it, please check your spam folder or contact our support team with your order number.
