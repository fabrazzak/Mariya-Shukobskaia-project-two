Common Questions

Browse through our most frequently asked questions below. If you can't find the answer you're looking for, don't hesitate to contact us.

You can book your stay at any of our caravan and motorhome club sites through our website by visiting the specific site page and clicking the "Book Now" button. Alternatively, you can call our reservations team at +44 141 222 2254 during office hours (9am-5pm, Monday to Friday).

We recommend booking in advance, especially for peak seasons (July-August and bank holiday weekends) as our most popular sites get booked up quickly. Some sites allow online bookings up to 12 months in advance.

Our cancellation policy varies depending on how far in advance you cancel:

  • More than 28 days before arrival: Full refund minus £10 administration fee
  • 14-28 days before arrival: 50% refund of deposit
  • Less than 14 days before arrival: No refund

We recommend considering travel insurance to cover unexpected cancellations. For cancellations due to COVID-19 restrictions, special flexible terms may apply - please contact our team.

Our standard check-in time is from 1pm onwards at all sites. Some sites may allow earlier check-in if your pitch is ready, but this cannot be guaranteed.

If you anticipate arriving after 6pm, please contact the site directly to make late arrival arrangements. Some of our smaller sites may lock their gates in the evening, so it's important to notify them if you'll be arriving late.

All our caravan and motorhome club sites offer the following standard facilities:

  • Electric hook-ups (16 amp standard)
  • Modern toilet and shower blocks
  • Chemical toilet disposal points
  • Fresh water taps
  • Waste water disposal
  • Laundry facilities (at most sites)
  • Wi-Fi (some sites may charge for this)

Many of our sites also have additional amenities such as children's play areas, dog walking areas, motorhome service points, and information rooms with local guides. Specific facilities vary by site - please check the individual site page for details.

Some of our sites do accept tents, while others are exclusively for caravans and motorhomes. This varies by location:

  • Tents accepted: Gowerton, Brecon Beacons, St David's Lleithyr Meadow, and several others have dedicated tent areas
  • Tents not accepted: Pembrey Country Park, Min-y-Don, and some other sites don't have tent facilities

When booking online, the system will automatically show you only sites that accommodate your unit type. Or you can filter sites by "Tents Accepted" on our Sites page.

Yes, we strive to make our sites accessible to all visitors:

  • All sites have accessible toilet and shower facilities
  • Many sites have specially adapted pitches closer to amenities
  • Min-y-Don site has been specially designed with smooth tarmac roads and wide parking bays
  • Assistance dogs are welcome at all sites

If you have specific accessibility requirements, please mention them when booking so we can ensure the most suitable pitch is allocated. Some sites have mobility equipment available for hire - please check with the individual site.

No, membership is not required to stay at our sites. However, members enjoy several benefits:

  • Discounted pitch fees (typically 10-15% cheaper than non-member rates)
  • Priority booking window (members can book further in advance)
  • Exclusive member offers and promotions
  • Free subscription to our quarterly magazine
  • Access to member-only events

Non-members can join on arrival at any site and immediately benefit from member rates for that stay and all future bookings.

Our membership fees are as follows:

  • Individual membership: £45 per year
  • Joint membership (2 adults at same address): £60 per year
  • Family membership (2 adults + children): £65 per year
  • Young person (18-25 years): £30 per year

Membership runs for 12 months from date of joining. You'll typically save the cost of membership after about 7-10 nights of camping across our sites.

We welcome well-behaved pets at most of our sites, with the following guidelines:

  • Maximum of 2 pets per pitch unless by prior arrangement
  • Dogs must be kept on leads at all times on site
  • Designated dog walking areas are provided - please clean up after your pet
  • Pets are not allowed in toilet blocks or other amenity buildings
  • Some breeds may be restricted - please check when booking

A small additional charge of £2 per pet per night applies at most sites. Guide dogs and assistance dogs are always welcome free of charge.

Our policies regarding BBQs and fires vary by site and season:

  • Raised BBQs: Generally allowed at all sites when placed on stands to protect grass
  • Disposable BBQs: Only permitted on hardstanding pitches with a proper stand
  • Campfires: Only allowed at designated fire pits at certain sites (Brecon Beacons and Aberbran)
  • Fire bans: During very dry periods, all open fires may be prohibited

Gas BBQs are permitted at all times. Please always check with site staff about current fire regulations when you arrive.

To ensure all our guests enjoy a peaceful stay, we enforce quiet hours at all our sites:

  • Quiet hours: 11pm to 7am daily
  • During these times, please keep noise to a minimum
  • Music must not be audible outside your unit at any time
  • Children should be supervised and on their pitch by 10pm

We operate a "good neighbor" policy and ask all guests to be considerate of others. Excessive noise may result in being asked to leave without refund.
