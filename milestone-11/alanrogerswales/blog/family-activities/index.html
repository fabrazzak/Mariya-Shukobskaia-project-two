Family-Friendly Activities Near Our Sites

Discover the best activities to keep children entertained during your caravan holiday in Wales, from beach adventures to nature trails.

Family Guide

Expert-curated activities

Planning a family caravan holiday in Wales? You're in for a treat! Our caravan and motorhome club sites are located near some of the best family-friendly attractions in Wales. Whether you have toddlers or teenagers, there's something to keep everyone entertained. Here's our guide to the top activities near our sites that your whole family will love.

1. Beach Adventures at Gowerton

The Gowerton Caravan and Motorhome Club Site is perfectly located for beach-loving families. Nearby Rhossili Bay offers:

  • Three miles of golden sand for building castles and playing games
  • Safe swimming areas (check tide times)
  • Surfing lessons for kids aged 8+ at Rhossili Surf School
  • Rock pooling at low tide
  • The famous Worm's Head coastal walk (suitable for older children)

Don't miss the National Trust visitor center with its interactive exhibits about local wildlife and history - perfect for rainy days.

"Our kids spent hours building sandcastles and exploring rock pools at Rhossili. The beach is so vast it never feels crowded, even in peak season." — The Thompson Family

2. Outdoor Adventures at Brecon Beacons

The Brecon Beacons Caravan and Motorhome Club Site is a paradise for active families. Top activities include:

  • Gentle walks to waterfalls (try the Four Falls trail)
  • Pony trekking at local riding centers
  • Bike hire and family cycling routes
  • Stargazing at the Dark Sky Reserve (special family events in summer)
  • Adventure playground on site

The Brecon Mountain Railway is a must-do for train enthusiasts, with its steam train rides through stunning scenery.

3. Wildlife Encounters at St David's

St David's Lleithyr Meadow is ideally located for wildlife-loving families. Don't miss:

  • Boat trips to Ramsey Island to spot seals and puffins
  • Orienteering trails at the National Park Visitor Center
  • Coasteering adventures for teens (with professional guides)
  • Crabbing at Solva Harbour
  • The Bug Farm - interactive insect exhibits

The nearby Oakwood Theme Park offers thrilling rides for older children and gentler attractions for little ones.

4. Historical Explorations at Pembrey

Pembrey Country Park Caravan and Motorhome Club Site combines history with fun:

  • Pembrey Castle ruins with interactive audio tour for kids
  • Medieval reenactment days (summer weekends)
  • The Kidwelly Industrial Museum with hands-on exhibits
  • Dry ski slope and toboggan run in the country park
  • Seven miles of safe cycling paths

The National Wool Museum nearby offers craft workshops where children can try traditional skills.

5. Water Fun at Penrhos

Penrhos Caravan and Motorhome Club Site on Anglesey is perfect for water babies:

  • Paddleboarding and kayaking at Benllech Beach
  • Anglesey Sea Zoo with its shark tunnel
  • Porth Wen Brickworks - fascinating industrial ruins to explore
  • On-site heated swimming pool (seasonal)
  • Seal spotting boat trips from nearby Moelfre

Tips for Family Caravan Holidays

Make the most of your family caravan adventure with these tips:

  1. Pack for all weathers - Even in summer, bring waterproofs and layers
  2. Check age restrictions - Some activities have minimum age requirements
  3. Book activities in advance - Popular options fill up quickly in peak season
  4. Use site facilities - Many of our sites have play areas and games rooms
  5. Involve kids in planning - Let each child choose one special activity

All our featured sites have excellent family facilities including baby changing, laundry, and secure play areas. Many offer board games and sports equipment for loan, and the wardens are always happy to recommend local attractions suitable for your children's ages.

With stunning locations across Wales and fantastic activities right on your doorstep, a family caravan holiday offers the perfect blend of adventure and relaxation. The kids will make memories to last a lifetime, and you'll enjoy quality time together away from screens and daily routines.
