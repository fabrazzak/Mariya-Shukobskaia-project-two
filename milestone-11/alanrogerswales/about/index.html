OUR JOURNEY

The Alan Rogers Wales Story

Founded in 1987 by Alan Rogers, a passionate traveler and outdoor enthusiast, our company began with a simple mission: to help people discover the beauty of Wales through exceptional caravan and motorhome experiences.

What started as a small guidebook publishing business has grown into Wales' premier network of caravan and motorhome club sites, each carefully selected for their outstanding facilities and breathtaking locations.

OUR HISTORY

Milestones in Our Journey

Key moments that have shaped Alan Rogers Wales into the premier caravan and motorhome network it is today.

1987

Foundation of Alan Rogers Wales

Alan Rogers publishes his first guidebook featuring 12 caravan sites across Wales, establishing the foundation for what would become a trusted travel resource.

1995

First Partner Site Established

The Gowerton Caravan and Motorhome Club Site becomes our first official partner location, setting new standards for facilities in South Wales.

2003

Expansion Across Wales

Our network grows to include sites in North, Mid, and West Wales, offering travelers comprehensive coverage of the country's most scenic locations.

2012

Digital Transformation

Launch of our online booking platform and comprehensive website, making it easier for travelers to discover and reserve stays at our partner sites.

2025

20 Premier Sites & Counting

Today, our network includes 20 of Wales' finest caravan and motorhome club sites, each maintaining our high standards for quality and location.
