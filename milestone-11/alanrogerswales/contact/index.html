Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our caravan and motorhome club sites.

You can book directly through our website by visiting the specific site page and clicking "Book Now." Alternatively, you can call our booking line at +44 141 222 2254 during office hours or email us at bookings@alanrogerswales.com.

Our standard cancellation policy allows for full refunds up to 14 days before your scheduled arrival. Cancellations between 14 and 7 days receive a 50% refund. Cancellations within 7 days are non-refundable. Some premium sites may have different policies which will be clearly stated during booking.

Most of our sites are pet-friendly, though some may have restrictions on certain breeds or require pets to be kept on leads. Please check the specific site details or contact us for more information about pet policies at your chosen location.

Yes, several of our sites offer seasonal pitches for caravans and motorhomes. Availability varies by location and season. Please contact our bookings team for current availability and pricing for seasonal pitches at your preferred site.

All our sites offer electric hookups, clean toilet and shower facilities, and waste disposal. Many sites also have laundry facilities, children's play areas, and on-site shops or cafes. Premium sites may offer additional amenities like swimming pools or restaurants. Check individual site pages for specific facilities.
