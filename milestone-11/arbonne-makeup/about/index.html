OUR JOURNEY

The Arbonne Story

Founded in Switzerland in 1980, Arbonne was born from a simple yet powerful idea: beauty should be pure, safe, and beneficial. Our founder, Petter Mørck, combined his passion for botanicals with Swiss heritage to create a new standard in beauty.

In 2000, we expanded our vision to North America, bringing our Swiss formulations to a global audience. Today, we continue to innovate while staying true to our core values of sustainability, vegan formulations, and cruelty-free practices.

PURE INGREDIENTS

What We Leave Out Matters

At Arbonne, we're proud of what you won't find in our products. Our formulations exclude over 2,000 questionable ingredients including parabens, phthalates, synthetic fragrances, and mineral oil.

Instead, we focus on plant-derived actives, vitamins, and minerals that nourish your skin while delivering beautiful, long-lasting color and coverage.
