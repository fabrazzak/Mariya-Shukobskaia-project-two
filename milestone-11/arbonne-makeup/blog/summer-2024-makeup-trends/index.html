MAKEUP TRENDS • June 15, 2025

Summer 2025 Makeup Trends You Need to Try

Discover the top 5 makeup trends that will dominate this season and how to achieve them with Arbonne's vegan, cruelty-free products.

Written by

Arbonne Beauty Team

As the seasons change, so do beauty trends. Summer 2025 is all about fresh, dewy skin, bold pops of color, and effortless glam. Whether you're heading to the beach or a rooftop party, these trends will keep you looking radiant all season long.

1. Glass Skin Glow

The "glass skin" trend from Korea has evolved into an even more luminous version for summer 2025. This look focuses on ultra-hydrated, translucent skin that appears lit from within.

How to achieve it:

  • Start with our Natural Glow Foundation for buildable, dewy coverage
  • Apply Makeup Primer to create a smooth, hydrated base
  • Use the Radiance Eyeshadow Palette in the inner corners and cheekbones for highlight
  • Finish with a hydrating mist like our Let It Set Refreshing Finishing Spray

Pro Tip:

For extra glow, mix a drop of our LipOil Hydrating Lip Serum with your foundation. The hyaluronic acid will boost hydration while creating a glass-like finish.

2. Colorful Graphic Eyeliner

Say goodbye to basic black liner—summer 2025 is all about bold, colorful graphic designs. From electric blue wings to neon lower lash lines, this trend lets you play with color in creative ways.

Our favorite products for this look:

  • Perfect Pout Lip Crayon in vibrant shades (yes, they work great as liners too!)
  • It's A Fine Line Pencil Eye Liner for precise application
  • The Essential Eye Palette for coordinating eyeshadow looks

3. Sun-Kissed Bronzed Skin

The healthy, sun-kissed glow is back with a more natural approach. Instead of heavy contouring, 2025 focuses on strategic placement of bronzer to mimic where the sun would naturally hit your face.

Application technique:

  1. Start with our Sun Kissed Bronzer in a shade 1-2 tones darker than your skin
  2. Apply in a "3" shape along the hairline, under cheekbones, and along the jawline
  3. Use a fluffy brush to blend upwards for a natural effect
  4. Add a touch to the bridge of the nose and chin

For deeper skin tones, try our Summer Glow + Chill Set which includes a deeper bronzer shade and complementary highlighter.

4. Glossy, Juicy Lips

Matte lips are taking a backseat this summer as glossy, hydrated lips make a major comeback. The 2025 version focuses on plump, juicy lips with just a hint of tint.

Create the perfect glossy lip:

  • Exfoliate with our Botaniques Vanilla Moisturizing Lip Balm
  • Apply DermResults Solutions Lip Serum to hydrate and plump
  • Add a wash of color with Glossed Over Lip Gloss in your favorite shade
  • For extra dimension, dab a bit of highlighter in the center of the lips

Trend Alert:

The "cold girl" lip trend is huge for 2025—achieved by using slightly blue-toned pink shades to mimic that just-came-in-from-the-cold flush. Try our Glossed Over Lip Gloss in Frosted Berry for this look.

5. Effortless Brows

The days of overly sculpted brows are over. Summer 2025 celebrates natural, fluffy brows that look brushed up and full.

How to get the look:

  1. Brush brows upwards with a spoolie
  2. Fill in sparse areas with Shape It Up Brow Pencil using hair-like strokes
  3. Set with Eyebrow Gel for all-day hold
  4. For extra fullness, use a clean mascara wand to brush the gel through

Final Thoughts

Summer 2025 makeup trends are all about embracing your natural beauty while having fun with color and texture. The best part? All these looks can be achieved with Arbonne's vegan, cruelty-free products that are good for your skin and the planet.

Remember, trends are meant to inspire—not dictate. Feel free to adapt these looks to suit your personal style. Whether you go all-in on colorful liner or just add a touch of gloss to your lips, the most important thing is that you feel confident and beautiful.
