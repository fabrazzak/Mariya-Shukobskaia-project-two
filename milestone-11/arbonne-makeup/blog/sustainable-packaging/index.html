Our Journey to 100% Sustainable Packaging

How we transformed our entire product line to eco-friendly packaging without compromising quality or luxury.

Written by

Jamie Chen, Director of Sustainability

Three years ago, we made an ambitious pledge: 100% sustainable packaging across all products by 2025. Today, we're proud to share not only that we've achieved this goal, but how we did it while maintaining the premium quality our customers expect.

The Problem With Conventional Beauty Packaging

The beauty industry generates over 120 billion units of packaging annually, most of which ends up in landfills. Traditional packaging poses several environmental challenges:

Material Waste

Multi-layer plastics that can't be recycled

Chemical Leaching

Harmful dyes and coatings contaminating soil

Energy Intensive

High carbon footprint from virgin materials

When we analyzed our own footprint in 2021, we discovered our packaging accounted for 38% of our total carbon emissions. That became our call to action.

Our Sustainable Packaging Solutions

After two years of research and development, we implemented these innovative solutions across our product lines:

1

Post-Consumer Recycled (PCR) Materials

We transitioned all plastic components to contain minimum 50% PCR content, with some items like our LipOil Hydrating Serum tubes reaching 100% PCR. This diverts waste from oceans and landfills while reducing production energy by up to 70%.

Innovation Highlight: Developed a proprietary blend of ocean-bound PCR plastic that maintains clarity for our transparent packaging.

2

Plant-Based Alternatives

Where plastic was unavoidable, we created bio-based solutions. Our Essential Eye Palette compacts are now made from sugarcane resin, a renewable resource that decomposes in industrial composting facilities.

Before:

Virgin ABS plastic

800yr decomposition

After:

Sugarcane bio-resin

6-12mo composting

3

Refillable Systems

We introduced refillable options for bestselling items like our Mineral Pressed Powder, reducing packaging waste by 82% per unit. The sleek metal compacts are designed to last for years.

The Challenges We Overcame

Transitioning an entire product line wasn't without obstacles:

Material Performance

Early PCR plastics couldn't maintain our luxury finish. After 37 material tests, we found a blend that met our standards for opacity and texture.

Supplier Transition

We had to audit and often replace suppliers to ensure ethical sourcing of recycled materials. This added 8 months to our timeline.

Consumer Education

Some customers initially perceived PCR packaging as "lower quality." We addressed this through transparency about our rigorous testing standards.

The Impact of Our Changes

Since implementing these sustainable packaging solutions, we've achieved remarkable results:

76%

Reduction in virgin plastic use across all products

2.3M

Pounds of ocean-bound plastic diverted annually

42%

Decrease in packaging-related carbon emissions

100%

Of packaging now recyclable, reusable, or compostable

How You Can Participate

Sustainability is a shared responsibility. Here's how our community can contribute:

Recycle Right

All our packaging is labeled with recycling codes. Rinse containers and separate components (e.g., pumps from bottles).

Join Our Take-Back Program

Return 5 empty products to any store or via mail for a free mini product. We professionally recycle what local facilities can't process.

Choose Refills

Opt for refillable versions where available, reducing packaging waste by up to 85% per purchase.

Try Our Sustainable Favorites

Experience luxury that loves the planet with our best-selling products in eco-conscious packaging.
