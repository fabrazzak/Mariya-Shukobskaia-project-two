Our Story of Reconnection

How Wild Manchester Group came to be and our mission to help people rediscover their connection with nature.

Explore Our Mission

Our Mission

At Wild Manchester Group, we believe that modern life has disconnected us from our natural roots. Our mission is to rebuild that essential connection through immersive wilderness experiences.

Nature Reconnection

We create opportunities for people to experience nature's healing power through camping, foraging, and wilderness immersion.

Education

Teaching practical skills that foster self-reliance and a deeper understanding of our natural environment.

Conservation

Promoting sustainable practices that protect and preserve our wild spaces for future generations.

Our Core Values

The principles that guide everything we do at Wild Manchester Group

Community

We believe in the power of shared experiences in nature to build meaningful connections between people.

Safety

All our adventures prioritize participant safety while maintaining the authentic wilderness experience.

Sustainability

We practice and teach Leave No Trace principles to minimize our impact on the environments we cherish.

Our Journey

Key milestones in the Wild Manchester Group story

Founding Vision

Alex Morgan, after years of solo wilderness trips, recognized the growing disconnect between urban life and nature in Manchester.

Spring 2015

First Guided Trip

Organized the inaugural wild camping experience for 12 participants in the Peak District near Manchester.

Summer 2016

Team Expansion

Sarah Chen and Jamie Wilson joined as full-time guides, allowing us to offer specialized foraging and safety programs.

Early 2018

Glasgow Base Established

Opened our current headquarters at 22 Renfield Lane to better serve our growing community across Scotland.

2020

500+ Adventures

Celebrated our 500th guided wilderness experience with participants from all over the UK and beyond.

Present Day
