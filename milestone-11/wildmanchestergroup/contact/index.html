Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our wilderness experiences

For our beginner trips, we provide all the essential camping gear. We recommend bringing:

  • Comfortable, weather-appropriate clothing
  • Sturdy hiking boots or shoes
  • Personal toiletries and medications
  • Water bottle (we provide water purification)
  • Headlamp or flashlight
  • Any personal snacks you might want

A complete packing list will be provided after booking.

Absolutely! We specialize in introducing beginners to wilderness experiences. Our beginner wild camping trips are designed for people with no prior experience. Our expert guides will teach you everything you need to know in a safe, supportive environment.

For those with more experience, we also offer intermediate and advanced level adventures.

We understand plans change. Our cancellation policy is as follows:

  • More than 30 days before trip: Full refund
  • 15-30 days before trip: 50% refund
  • 7-14 days before trip: 25% refund
  • Less than 7 days before trip: No refund

In cases of severe weather that makes the trip unsafe, we'll either reschedule or provide a full refund.

We offer specific family-friendly camping experiences designed for children ages 6 and up. These trips have modified activities and safety protocols tailored for families.

For our regular adult-oriented trips, the minimum age is typically 16. Please check the specific experience details or contact us to discuss your family's needs.

Safety is our top priority. All our guides are certified in wilderness first aid, and we maintain a maximum 6:1 participant-to-guide ratio. We carry comprehensive first aid kits and emergency communication devices on all trips.

Before each experience, we provide thorough safety briefings and ensure all participants understand emergency procedures. We also monitor weather conditions closely and will modify or cancel trips if conditions become unsafe.
