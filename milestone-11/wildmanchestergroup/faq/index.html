General Questions

What is Wild Manchester Group?

Wild Manchester Group is an organization dedicated to helping people reconnect with nature through wild camping adventures, foraging experiences, and wilderness survival training. We operate in and around Manchester, offering guided experiences for all skill levels.

Where are you located?

Our base camp is located at 22 Renfield Ln, Glasgow G2 5AR, United Kingdom. However, our wilderness experiences take place in various locations around Manchester and the surrounding areas. Each adventure has its own meeting point which we provide upon booking.

Do I need any prior experience?

Not at all! We offer experiences for all skill levels, from complete beginners to seasoned outdoors enthusiasts. Our beginner-friendly trips provide all the instruction and support you'll need to have a safe and enjoyable experience in nature.

What's the best season for wild camping?

Each season offers a unique experience. Spring brings new growth and wildlife, summer offers warm nights, autumn provides beautiful foliage, and winter offers a serene, quiet landscape. We operate year-round and provide season-appropriate gear and guidance.

Wild Camping

Is wild camping legal in the UK?

Wild camping laws vary across the UK. In England and Wales, you generally need landowner permission. In Scotland, the Right to Roam laws make wild camping legal in most unenclosed areas. We only operate in legal locations and always obtain necessary permissions.

What facilities are available during camping?

True wild camping means minimal facilities. We teach you how to be self-sufficient in nature. However, our beginner trips often have access to basic composting toilets and fresh water sources. There are no showers or electricity at our wild campsites.

What should I bring for a wild camping trip?

We provide a detailed packing list when you book, but essentials include: a good quality tent, sleeping bag suitable for the season, sleeping mat, waterproof clothing, sturdy footwear, headtorch, water bottle, and personal items. For beginner trips, we provide most camping equipment.

Can I bring my children wild camping?

Absolutely! We offer family-friendly camping experiences designed to introduce children to nature safely. The minimum age varies by trip (typically 6+ for most experiences), and we adjust activities to be engaging for younger participants. Family bookings receive special considerations.

Foraging

Is foraging safe?

When done correctly with proper knowledge, foraging is very safe. Our expert guides teach you how to positively identify edible plants and avoid dangerous lookalikes. We follow the rule: "If in doubt, leave it out." Never eat anything you're not 100% certain about.

What can I expect to find foraging in Manchester?

The Manchester area offers a surprising variety of wild edibles including wild garlic, nettles, elderflowers, blackberries, mushrooms (in season), rosehips, and many more. What's available depends on the season - we'll help you discover what's at its peak during your visit.

Do I need special equipment for foraging?

Basic foraging requires very little equipment. We recommend: comfortable walking shoes, weather-appropriate clothing, a basket or cloth bag, small knife, notebook, and a field guide (we provide these). For certain trips, we may recommend additional items which we'll specify when you book.

Is foraging legal in the UK?

Foraging is legal in the UK for personal use if done responsibly. We follow the Wildlife and Countryside Act which allows foraging of the "Four F's" - fruit, foliage, flowers, and fungi - as long as it's for personal consumption and doesn't damage the plant or environment. We always obtain landowner permission.

Booking & Payments

How do I book an experience?

You can book directly through our website by visiting the "Book Adventure" page, or contact us via phone or email. We recommend booking at least 2-4 weeks in advance for weekend trips, especially during peak seasons (spring and summer).

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit/debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), PayPal, and bank transfers. Payment is required in full at time of booking to secure your spot. For private group bookings, we may offer payment plans - please inquire.

What is your cancellation policy?

We offer full refunds for cancellations made at least 14 days before the experience date. Cancellations 7-14 days prior receive a 50% refund. Within 7 days, we cannot offer refunds but may reschedule you if possible. In case of extreme weather, we may reschedule or refund.

Do you offer gift certificates?

Yes! Our experiences make wonderful gifts. You can purchase digital gift certificates for specific amounts or particular experiences. Certificates are valid for 12 months from purchase date. Visit our "Gift Certificates" page or contact us to arrange one.

Safety & Equipment

How do you ensure participant safety?

Safety is our top priority. All guides are first aid certified, carry emergency communication devices, and are trained in wilderness first response. We maintain small group sizes, conduct thorough briefings, carry first aid kits, and choose locations with emergency access. Our risk assessments are regularly updated.

What happens in case of bad weather?

We monitor weather closely and will contact you if conditions may affect the experience. Light rain doesn't usually cancel trips - we provide guidance on appropriate clothing. For severe weather (high winds, storms), we may reschedule or offer alternative activities/refunds. Your safety is our priority.

Do you provide camping equipment?

For beginner experiences, we provide all major camping equipment (tents, sleeping mats, cooking gear). For advanced trips, participants typically bring their own gear (with our guidance). We have rental equipment available if needed - inquire when booking.

What about insects and wildlife?

Insects are part of the wilderness experience! We recommend bringing insect repellent. The UK has no dangerous wildlife, but we teach how to store food properly to avoid attracting animals. We've never had any dangerous encounters - most wildlife avoids humans.

Other Questions

Can I bring my dog?

We love dogs but generally don't allow them on group experiences due to potential distractions and wildlife interactions. However, we can sometimes arrange private experiences where dogs are welcome - please inquire. Assistance dogs are always welcome with prior notice.

Are your experiences accessible?

Some experiences can be adapted for different mobility levels, but true wilderness locations often have uneven terrain. Please contact us to discuss specific needs - we'll do our best to accommodate or suggest suitable alternatives. We're committed to making nature accessible to everyone.

Do you offer corporate or team-building experiences?

Yes! We design custom wilderness experiences for corporate groups focused on team building, leadership development, and stress reduction. These can range from half-day foraging workshops to multi-day survival challenges. Contact us to discuss your group's needs and objectives.

How can I become a guide for Wild Manchester?

We're always interested in passionate, knowledgeable individuals. Requirements include: wilderness first aid certification, extensive outdoor experience, teaching ability, and passion for nature. Formal qualifications (e.g., Mountain Leader) are preferred. Send your CV and cover letter to our contact email.
