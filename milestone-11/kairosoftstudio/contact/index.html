Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about Tennis Club Story

Tennis Club Story saves all progress locally on your device. If you've lost progress after reinstalling, unfortunately we cannot recover it as we don't store your save data on our servers. We recommend regularly backing up your device to prevent data loss.

If your game freezes or crashes, try these steps:
1. Restart your device
2. Ensure you have the latest game version installed
3. Check that your device meets the minimum requirements (Android 5.1+)
4. Clear the app cache (Settings > Apps > Tennis Club Story > Storage > Clear Cache)
If issues persist, contact our support team with details about your device and when the crashes occur.

Tennis Club Story is a premium paid app with no in-app purchases or advertisements. You pay once to download the game and get the complete experience without any additional costs.

We welcome all feedback and suggestions! Please use the contact form on this page to share your ideas. While we can't implement every suggestion, we review all feedback and consider popular requests for future updates.

Currently, Tennis Club Story is only available on Android devices. While we don't have announcements about other platforms at this time, we recommend checking our official website or following our social media for any updates about potential releases on other platforms.
