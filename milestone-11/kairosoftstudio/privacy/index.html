1. Introduction

Kairosoft Co., Ltd. ("we," "us," or "our") operates the Tennis Club Story mobile game and related services ("Service"). We are committed to respecting your privacy and protecting your personal information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

This policy describes how we collect, use, and disclose your information when you use our Service and the choices you have associated with that information.

2. Compliance with Laws

We comply with all applicable laws and regulations regarding the collection, use, and handling of personal information, including Japan's Act on the Protection of Personal Information and other relevant data protection laws.

3. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of personal information when you use our Service:

  • Contact information (name, email address, phone number)
  • Account credentials
  • Device information (IP address, device type, operating system)
  • Usage data (game progress, preferences, interactions with our Service)
  • Payment information for in-app purchases (processed by third-party payment processors)

4. Purpose of Use

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain our Service
  • To improve and optimize our website and mobile applications
  • To communicate with you regarding service updates, security alerts, and support messages
  • To provide information about our products, services, promotions, and campaigns
  • To develop and improve our games and create new applications
  • To monitor and analyze usage trends and preferences
  • To prevent fraud and ensure the security of our Service

5. Analytics & Advertising

5.1 Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to help analyze how users interact with our Service. Google Analytics uses cookies to collect standard internet log information and visitor behavior data. This information (including your IP address) is transmitted to and stored by Google. Google uses this information to evaluate website usage, compile reports on website activity, and provide other services relating to website activity.

By using our Service, you consent to the processing of data about you by Google in the manner and for the purposes set out above.

5.2 Advertising IDs

For advertising purposes, our Service may use unique advertising identifiers that may transmit certain data to advertising partners. These identifiers cannot be used to personally identify you.

6. Data Protection

6.1 Security Measures

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, loss, destruction, alteration, or disclosure. These measures include:

  • Encryption of sensitive data
  • Regular security assessments
  • Access controls and authentication procedures
  • Employee training on data protection

6.2 Data Retention

We retain personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected, including for the purposes of satisfying any legal, accounting, or reporting requirements.

7. Third-Party Disclosures

We do not disclose your personal information to third parties except in the following circumstances:

  • With your explicit consent
  • To our service providers who assist in operating our Service (under confidentiality agreements)
  • When required by law or to respond to legal process
  • To protect the rights, property, or safety of our company, our users, or the public
  • In connection with any merger, sale of company assets, financing, or acquisition of all or a portion of our business

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

We recommend that you review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.
