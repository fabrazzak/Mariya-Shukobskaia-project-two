Frequently Asked Questions

For the best results, have an idea of your preferred style and functionality needs. If possible, gather samples of your wall colors, flooring, or furniture fabrics. Also think about which rooms need privacy, light control, or energy efficiency.

Consultations typically take 30-60 minutes for smaller projects (1-10 windows) and 60-90 minutes for larger projects. We'll take the time needed to ensure you're completely satisfied with your selections.

Our consultants wear masks and sanitize before entering your home. We ask that you also sanitize your hands before handling samples. All sample books are sanitized between appointments.

Most custom window treatments take 2-4 weeks for manufacturing. We'll schedule your installation as soon as your order arrives. Rush services may be available for an additional fee.

We understand plans change. Please give us at least 24 hours notice if you need to reschedule or cancel your appointment so we can offer the time slot to another client.
