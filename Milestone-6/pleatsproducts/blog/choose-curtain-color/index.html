How To Choose The Right Curtain Colors

May 28, 2025 â€¢ 7 min read

Selecting the perfect curtain color can transform your space, but with so many options it can be overwhelming. Follow these expert tips to choose curtain colors that complement your decor and create the right atmosphere.

1. Consider Your Wall Color

For a cohesive look, choose curtains that are either slightly lighter or darker than your wall color. Matching exactly can look flat, while a 1-2 shade difference adds depth. For bold walls, neutral curtains balance the space.

2. Factor in Natural Light

North-facing rooms with cool light benefit from warm-toned curtains. South-facing sunny rooms can handle cooler colors. Dark colors absorb light while light colors reflect it, affecting both brightness and temperature.

3. Create the Right Mood

Warm colors (reds, oranges, yellows) energize a space, while cool colors (blues, greens) create calm. Neutrals offer flexibility. Consider the room's purpose when selecting colors.

4. Coordinate With Furniture

Pick up secondary colors from your upholstery or artwork. For patterned furniture, choose solid curtains in a complementary color. With neutral furniture, you have more color freedom.

5. Think About Size Perception

Light colors make rooms feel larger and airier. Dark colors create coziness but can make small spaces feel cramped. Vertical stripes add height, horizontal stripes add width.
