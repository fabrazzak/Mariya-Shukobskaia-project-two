Our Story

Family-owned and operated in Edmonton since 2008, bringing beauty and functionality to homes through premium window coverings.

Who We Are

Pleats Products was founded in 2008 by husband and wife team Michael and Sarah Chen with a simple mission: to provide Edmonton homeowners with the highest quality window coverings backed by exceptional service.

What began as a small showroom in south Edmonton has grown into one of the city's most trusted window treatment specialists, serving thousands of satisfied customers across Alberta.

As a family-owned business, we treat every customer like they're part of our family. We take pride in our workmanship, stand behind our products with industry-leading warranties, and are committed to making your window covering experience seamless from consultation to installation.

Our Mission

"To enhance homes with beautiful, functional window treatments while providing an exceptional customer experience through expert advice, quality products, and professional service."

Our Journey

2008

Founded Pleats Products with a small showroom in south Edmonton, specializing in custom blinds and shades.

2012

Expanded our product line to include premium curtains and drapery, becoming a full-service window treatment provider.

2015

Moved to our current larger showroom location, allowing us to showcase over 300 fabric samples and window covering options.

2018

Became an authorized Hunter Douglas dealer, offering their premium window covering products to our customers.

2021

Launched our motorized and smart home integration options, bringing cutting-edge technology to window treatments.

2025

Celebrated 17 years serving Edmonton with over 5,000 satisfied customers and counting.
