Our Story

Founded in Lexington, Kentucky in 2005, Murphy Door Furniture began with a simple idea: to create hidden doors that were indistinguishable from real furniture. What started as a small workshop crafting custom solutions for local clients has grown into an internationally recognized brand.

Today, we've installed over 200,000 hidden doors worldwide, from private residences to commercial spaces, government buildings, and even film sets. Our commitment to quality and innovation remains unchanged.
