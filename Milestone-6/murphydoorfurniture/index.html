Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you want to know about Hidden Doors - Is right here.

A Murphy Door is a type of hidden door that's designed to blend seamlessly into your home's decor while providing concealed access to another room or space. Unlike traditional doors, Murphy Doors are disguised as bookshelves, mirrors, or other furniture pieces, making them virtually indistinguishable from regular wall features when closed.

Hidden doors go by several names depending on their design and function. Common terms include: Murphy Doors (our brand name), secret doors, concealed doors, bookcase doors, invisible doors, and camouflage doors. Some people also refer to them as "James Bond doors" due to their frequent appearance in spy movies.

Murphy Doors operate using a specialized hinge system that allows the door to swing open smoothly while supporting the weight of the decorative facade (like a bookshelf or mirror). Our doors feature high-quality hardware that ensures quiet operation and long-term durability. When closed, the door sits flush with the wall, with no visible hardware or gaps to reveal its presence.

Yes, all our Murphy Doors can be equipped with locking mechanisms for added security. We offer several locking options including discreet magnetic locks, keyed locks, and even smart lock compatibility. The locking system is integrated seamlessly so it doesn't compromise the hidden nature of the door.

The cost of a Murphy Door varies depending on size, design complexity, materials, and any custom features.
