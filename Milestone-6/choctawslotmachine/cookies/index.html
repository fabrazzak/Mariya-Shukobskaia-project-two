Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device by websites and applications that you visit or use. They are widely used to make apps work more efficiently, as well as to provide information to the app owners.

2. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for several purposes:

  • Essential Cookies: These are necessary for the App to function and cannot be switched off. They are usually only set in response to actions made by you such as logging in or filling in forms.
  • Performance Cookies: These allow us to count visits and traffic sources so we can measure and improve the performance of our App.
  • Functional Cookies: These enable the App to provide enhanced functionality and personalization.
  • Targeting Cookies: These may be set through our App by our advertising partners to build a profile of your interests and show you relevant advertisements.

You have the right to decide whether to accept or reject cookies. You can exercise your cookie preferences by clicking on the appropriate opt-out links provided in the cookie table above or by managing your preferences in our cookie consent banner.

Browser Controls

On your mobile device, you may adjust your privacy and advertising settings to control whether you want to receive more personalized advertising.
