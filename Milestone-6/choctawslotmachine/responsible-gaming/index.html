Responsible Gaming

Our commitment to promoting safe and responsible gaming practices

Our Commitment

At Choctaw Slots, we are committed to providing an entertaining and responsible gaming experience. While our games are designed for fun and enjoyment, we recognize that gaming may not be suitable for everyone.

This page provides information about responsible gaming, potential risks, and tools to help you maintain control of your gaming experience. We encourage all players to use these resources to ensure gaming remains a positive form of entertainment.

Warning Signs

It's important to be aware of behaviors that may indicate gaming is becoming a problem:

  • Spending more time or money on gaming than intended
  • Neglecting work, school, or family responsibilities due to gaming
  • Borrowing money or selling possessions to fund gaming
  • Feeling restless or irritable when trying to cut down on gaming
  • Lying to family or friends about gaming activities

Responsible Gaming Tools

We provide several tools to help you manage your gaming experience:

Self-Assessment

Take our brief questionnaire to evaluate your gaming habits:

  1. Do you play for longer periods than intended?
  2. Have you neglected responsibilities due to gaming?
  3. Do you feel guilty about your gaming habits?
  4. Have others criticized your gaming?

If you answered "yes" to several questions, you may want to consider setting limits or seeking help.

Session Limits

Set limits on your gaming sessions to maintain balance:

  • Decide in advance how long you'll play
  • Set a timer or alarm as a reminder
  • Take regular breaks during extended play

Our app includes session reminders that you can enable in settings.

Self-Exclusion

If you need to take a break from gaming, we offer self-exclusion options:

  • Temporary account deactivation (1 week to 6 months)
  • Permanent account closure

Contact our support team to initiate self-exclusion.

Reality Check

Our app includes reality check features to help you stay aware:

  • Session duration notifications
  • Play history and statistics
  • Option to set spending limits

Enable these features in your account settings.
