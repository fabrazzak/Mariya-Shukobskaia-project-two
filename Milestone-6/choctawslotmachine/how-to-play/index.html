Blackjack

Beat the dealer without going over 21 in this classic card game. Choctaw Slots offers several blackjack variants including Three Hand and High Roller versions.

How to Play:

  1. Place your bet in the betting circle
  2. Receive two cards face up while the dealer gets one face up and one face down
  3. Decide to HIT (take another card) or STAND (keep your current hand)
  4. You can also DOUBLE DOWN (double your bet for one more card) or SPLIT (if you have two cards of the same value)
  5. The dealer reveals their face-down card and must hit until they reach at least 17
  6. Win if your hand is closer to 21 than the dealer's without busting (going over 21)

Blackjack Tips:

Learn Basic Strategy

Memorize the optimal play for every possible hand combination against every dealer up card.

Always Split Aces & 8s

Splitting these pairs gives you the best chance to improve weak hands or capitalize on strong ones.

Double Down on 11

When you have 11, you have a strong chance of making 21 with one more card.

Never Take Insurance

Insurance bets favor the house in the long run.

Roulette

Bet on numbers, colors, or sections of the roulette wheel and watch the ball determine your fate. Choctaw Slots offers both American and European roulette variants.

How to Play:

  1. Place your chips on the betting area corresponding to your desired bet
  2. Different bets have different odds and payouts (see betting table)
  3. Press SPIN to set the wheel in motion
  4. The ball will land in one of the numbered pockets
  5. All winning bets are paid according to the payout odds

Roulette Tips:

Play European Roulette

The single zero reduces the house edge compared to American roulette's double zero.

Mix Inside & Outside Bets

Combine high-probability outside bets with high-payout inside bets for balanced play.

Avoid the Five-Number Bet

This bet has the worst house edge in American roulette.

Set Win/Loss Limits

Decide in advance when to walk away, whether you're winning or losing.
