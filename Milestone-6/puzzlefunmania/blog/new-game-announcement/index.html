Puzzle Quest

Our revolutionary new puzzle adventure game combining match-3 mechanics with RPG elements

Coming Fall 2025

We're thrilled to officially announce Puzzle Quest, our most ambitious game to date! After two years in development, we're bringing you a revolutionary puzzle experience that combines the satisfying mechanics of match-3 games with deep RPG progression systems.

What is Puzzle Quest?

Puzzle Quest is a genre-blending adventure where your puzzle skills directly impact your character's journey. Solve match-3 puzzles to:

  • Battle enemies in innovative combat sequences
  • Gather resources for crafting powerful items
  • Solve environmental puzzles in the game world
  • Progress through an epic fantasy storyline

Key Features

Puzzle Combat System

Match gems to attack, defend, and cast spells. Different colors represent different abilities in battle.

Character Progression

Level up your hero, choose from multiple classes, and customize your puzzle-solving playstyle.

Epic Storyline

A 30+ hour campaign with branching quests and meaningful choices that affect the game world.

Multiplayer Modes

Challenge friends in PvP matches or team up for cooperative puzzle dungeons.

Development Journey

Puzzle Quest began as an experimental prototype in our game jam sessions. The core idea - "what if matching gems was how you cast spells?" - quickly evolved into something much bigger. Here's a timeline of its development:

2023 Q2

Initial prototype created during internal game jam. Core combat mechanics established.

2023 Q4

Alpha

First playable alpha with basic RPG systems. Internal testing begins.

2024 Q2

Beta

Closed beta with select community members. Core gameplay refined based on feedback.

2025 Q3

Release

Planned global launch on iOS, Android, and PC platforms.
