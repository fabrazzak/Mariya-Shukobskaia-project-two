Boat Attack Update 1.1 - What's New

May 28, 2025 5 min read JustCool

We're excited to announce the release of Boat Attack version 1.1, packed with new features, improvements, and bug fixes that will enhance your racing experience. This update focuses on player customization, gameplay refinements, and a brand new track to explore.

New Features

Boat Customization

The most requested feature is finally here! Update 1.1 introduces an extensive boat customization system that lets you:

  • Choose from 12 new paint colors and 8 decal patterns
  • Unlock special skins by completing challenges
  • Mix and match components from different boat classes
  • Save up to 5 custom presets for quick selection

Tropical Paradise Track

Race through our brand new environment - a stunning tropical island with:

  • Multiple racing routes with varying difficulty
  • Dynamic weather effects including rain and sunshine
  • Hidden shortcuts for experienced players
  • Spectacular waterfalls and beachside views
"The new tropical track is our most visually impressive environment yet, with technical innovations that push mobile graphics further than ever before."

Gameplay Improvements

Based on player feedback, we've made several adjustments to improve the core racing experience:

  • Revised physics for more realistic water interactions
  • Adjusted AI difficulty curve in single-player mode
  • Added new boost power-ups in strategic locations
  • Improved collision detection around track boundaries

Bug Fixes

This update addresses several issues reported by our community:

  • Fixed occasional progress loss when closing the app during races
  • Resolved multiplayer synchronization issues
  • Corrected visual glitches on certain device models
  • Addressed leaderboard display problems

What's Next?

We're already working on the next major update planned for late summer, which will include:

  • A boat editor for creating completely custom designs
  • New multiplayer game modes
  • Additional tracks in different environments
  • Special seasonal events

Update 1.1 is now available for download on the Google Play Store. We'd love to hear your feedback on the new features - join our community forums or reach out on social media to share your thoughts!
