Our Story

Our Journey

From humble beginnings to creating exciting mobile games enjoyed by thousands of players worldwide.

2023 - Foundation

JustCoolBlast was founded by a small team of passionate game developers who met while working at larger studios. We shared a vision for creating high-quality mobile games that didn't compromise on gameplay or visuals.

Early 2025 - First Prototypes

After months of experimentation, we settled on the concept for Boat Attack - a fast-paced boat racing game with realistic water physics. Our early prototypes focused on nailing the core boat handling and water interactions.

Mid 2025 - Technical Breakthrough

Our engineering team developed a custom water physics solution that worked smoothly on mobile devices. This became the foundation for Boat Attack's unique gameplay feel.

Early 2025 - Boat Attack Launch

After a successful beta test with thousands of players, we launched Boat Attack worldwide on the Google Play Store. The game received positive reviews for its visuals and satisfying gameplay.

Today - Growing the Team

With the success of Boat Attack, we've expanded our team and are working on new content updates while prototyping our next game. Our commitment to quality mobile experiences remains stronger than ever.
