Color Slide Jam v1.1 Update

Discover all the new features and improvements in our latest game update

We're excited to announce the release of Color Slide Jam version 1.1, packed with new content and improvements based on your valuable feedback. This update brings 20 brand-new challenging levels, quality-of-life improvements, and several gameplay enhancements that will make your puzzle-solving experience even more enjoyable.

New Levels & Challenges

The highlight of this update is the addition of 20 meticulously designed levels that introduce new mechanics and obstacles. Our design team focused on creating puzzles that:

  • Gradually increase in complexity to maintain a satisfying difficulty curve
  • Introduce new block types that require creative solutions
  • Offer multiple solution paths to encourage strategic thinking

Gameplay Improvements

Based on player feedback, we've implemented several quality-of-life improvements:

Undo Feature

Now you can undo your last move if you make a mistake, helping you learn from errors.

Move Counter

Visual indicator showing how many moves you've used versus the target.

Hint System

New optional hint system for when you're truly stuck on a level.

Level Preview

See the puzzle layout before starting a level to plan your strategy.

Visual & Performance Enhancements

We've polished the visual experience and optimized performance:

  • Enhanced particle effects for block matches
  • Smoother animations and transitions
  • Reduced loading times between levels
  • Optimized memory usage for better performance on older devices
