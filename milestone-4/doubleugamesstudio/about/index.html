Who We Are

DoubleUGames is a premier mobile game development studio based in Seoul, South Korea. Founded with a passion for creating engaging puzzle experiences, we specialize in games that challenge the mind while delivering vibrant, immersive experiences.

Our team of talented designers, developers, and artists work tirelessly to craft games that stand out in the mobile gaming landscape. We believe in the power of games to entertain, educate, and bring people together.

Our Journey So Far

Key milestones in our studio's evolution

2018

Studio Founded

DoubleUGames was established in Seoul by a small team of passionate game developers with a vision to create innovative puzzle games.

2020

First Game Launch

Released our debut title "Puzzle Master" which gained positive reception for its innovative mechanics and clean design.

2022

Team Expansion

Grew our team to 10 members and moved to our current office in Gangnam Finance Center to accommodate growth.

2025

Color Slide Jam Release

Launched our flagship title Color Slide Jam, reaching 5,000+ downloads in the first month with excellent reviews.
