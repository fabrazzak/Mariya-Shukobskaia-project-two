Game Update

Survival Island v1.5: New Caves & Crafting Recipes

Ani Petrosyan

Posted on May 15, 2025 • 8 min read

Our biggest update yet brings two expansive cave systems, a dozen new crafting recipes, and numerous quality-of-life improvements based on your feedback. Let's dive into what's new in Survival Island: Dinosaurs Pro!

The Crystal Caverns & Lava Tunnels

After months of development, we're excited to introduce two massive new underground environments:

Crystal Caverns

A breathtaking network of caves filled with luminous crystals that provide natural light and rare crafting materials.

  • • 5 new mineral types to discover
  • • Unique crystal-based ecosystem
  • • Hidden ancient ruins to explore

Lava Tunnels

Dangerous volcanic passages with extreme temperatures and valuable resources near molten lava flows.

  • • Thermal protection gear required
  • • Obsidian and rare metals
  • • New lava-based creatures

New Crafting System Additions

We've expanded the crafting system with 12 new recipes, including:

  • • Thermal Suit - Protects against extreme temperatures in caves
  • • Crystal Torch - Permanent light source using cavern minerals
  • • Obsidian Tools - More durable than flint but harder to craft
  • • Glider - Limited flight capability from high points

Pro Tip

The new Crystal Torch doesn't consume fuel like regular torches, making it ideal for cave exploration. However, it requires rare materials from the Crystal Caverns.

Quality of Life Improvements

Based on player feedback, we've implemented numerous improvements:

Feature Improvement
Inventory New quick-stack button and category sorting
Building Snapping system improved for easier base construction
Map Added custom markers and notes functionality
Crafting Favorites system and recent items category

Bug Fixes & Optimizations

This update addresses over 50 reported issues, including:

  • • Fixed rare crash when entering water during storms
  • • Improved dinosaur pathfinding around player structures
  • • Optimized memory usage on mobile devices
  • • Fixed inventory item duplication glitch
