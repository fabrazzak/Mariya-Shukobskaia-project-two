Cookies Policy

Last Updated: June 1, 2025

This Cookies Policy explains how Gem Jam, LLC ("we", "us", or "our") uses cookies and similar tracking technologies when you visit our website or play our games. By using our services, you consent to the use of cookies as described in this policy.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit a website or use an application. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and to provide information to the owners.

2. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for the following purposes:

  • Essential Cookies: Necessary for our website and games to function properly
  • Performance Cookies: Help us understand how visitors interact with our services
  • Functionality Cookies: Remember your preferences and settings
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us improve our services by collecting usage data
  • Advertising Cookies: Used to deliver relevant ads in our free-to-play games

3. Types of Cookies We Use

Cookie Name Purpose Duration
session_id Maintains your game session Session

4. Third-Party Cookies

We may use third-party services that set their own cookies, including:

  • Google Analytics for website traffic analysis
  • Advertising networks for in-game ads
  • Social media platforms for sharing features
  • Payment processors for in-app purchases

These third-party cookies are subject to the respective privacy policies of these providers.

5. Managing Cookies

You can control and/or delete cookies as you wish. Most web browsers allow you to:

  • See what cookies you have and delete them individually
  • Block third-party cookies
  • Block cookies from particular sites
  • Block all cookies
  • Delete all cookies when you close your browser

If you disable cookies, some features of our website and games may not function properly.

6. Game-Specific Tracking

Our games may collect anonymous usage data to:

  • Improve gameplay experience
  • Balance game mechanics
  • Identify and fix bugs
  • Understand player behavior

This data is aggregated and cannot be used to identify individual players.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookies Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page.
