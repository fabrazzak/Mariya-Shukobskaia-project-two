About Gem Jam

Crafting immersive survival adventures since 2016

Our Story

Founded in 2016 in Yerevan, Armenia, Gem Jam began as a small team of passionate game developers with a shared vision: to create immersive survival games that push the boundaries of mobile gaming.

Our journey started with simple 2D games, but we quickly realized our true calling was in crafting deep, engaging survival experiences with stunning 3D graphics.

In 2024, we released our breakout hit "Survival Island: Dinosaurs Pro," which has since been downloaded over 10,000 times and maintains a 4.7-star rating on the Google Play Store.

Today, we're a growing studio of 15 talented developers, designers, and artists dedicated to creating premium survival adventures for mobile platforms.
