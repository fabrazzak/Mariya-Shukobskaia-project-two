How to Play Sea Money

1

Deploy Your Radar

Use your submarine's radar to scan the ocean floor for hidden treasures. The radar will highlight potential finds.

2

Collect Treasures

Navigate to the marked locations and collect the treasures. Some may be easy to find, others more challenging.

3

Return to Port

Bring your treasures back to port to sell them. The more valuable the treasure, the more money you earn.

4

Upgrade & Explore

Use your earnings to upgrade your radar and submarine, then explore new, more challenging locations.
