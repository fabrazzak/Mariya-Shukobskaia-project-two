Our Story

Founded in 2023, Rollic Games began with a simple mission: to create mobile games that deliver pure, satisfying fun. Our journey started with a small team of passionate developers in Istanbul, united by a love for creative gameplay and polished experiences.

With the launch of Pressure Washing Run in early 2023, we discovered our niche in creating satisfying simulation games that players can enjoy in short bursts or for hours at a time. The game's unexpected success validated our approach and motivated us to push creative boundaries.

Today, we're proud to have reached over 1 million players worldwide and continue to grow our portfolio of unique, high-quality mobile games that stand out in a crowded market.

Our Journey

Key milestones in our development as a game studio

February 2023

Company Founded

Rollic Games was established in Istanbul with a small but passionate team of game developers and designers.

March 2023

First Game Released

Launched Pressure Washing Run on Google Play, our debut title that introduced our signature satisfying gameplay style.

June 2023

100K Downloads

Reached our first major milestone with over 100,000 downloads of Pressure Washing Run across global markets.

December 2023

Team Expansion

Grew our team to 15 full-time developers and artists to accelerate production on new game concepts.

July 2025

1M+ Downloads

Celebrated surpassing 1 million downloads across all our games, with players in over 150 countries.
