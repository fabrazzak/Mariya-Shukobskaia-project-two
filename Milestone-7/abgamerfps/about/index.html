About ABGamerFPS

Connecting passionate gamers with groundbreaking FPS experiences in development

Our Mission

At ABGamerFPS, we're dedicated to bridging the gap between innovative game developers and passionate FPS enthusiasts. Our platform serves as a curated showcase for the most exciting first-person shooter games in their alpha and beta phases.

We believe that early player feedback is crucial for shaping exceptional gaming experiences. By connecting testers with developers during these formative stages, we help create better games while giving our community exclusive access to upcoming titles.

Our Journey

From humble beginnings to becoming a trusted platform for FPS game testing

2018

Founding of ABGamerFPS

Started as a small blog reviewing indie FPS games, quickly growing into a community of passionate testers.

2020

First Developer Partnership

Established our first official partnership with an indie studio to test their upcoming horror FPS title.

2022

Platform Expansion

Launched our dedicated platform with automated testing signups, feedback systems, and community features.

2025

Current Day

Now working with dozens of studios worldwide, helping shape the future of FPS gaming through valuable player feedback.
