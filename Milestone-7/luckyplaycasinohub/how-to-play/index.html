How to Play Blackjack

Our High Roller Blackjack brings the classic card game to your mobile with authentic Vegas rules.

Pro Tip

Basic strategy can significantly improve your odds. Learn when to hit, stand, double down or split.

1 Blackjack Basics

The goal is to beat the dealer's hand without going over 21:

  • Number cards are worth their face value
  • Face cards (J, Q, K) are worth 10
  • Aces can be 1 or 11 (your choice)

2 Placing Your Bet

Before cards are dealt, place your bet:

  • Use the + and - buttons to adjust your wager
  • Minimum and maximum bets vary by table
  • Confirm your bet to start the hand

3 Playing Your Hand

After receiving your initial two cards, you have several options:

  • Hit: Take another card
  • Stand: Keep your current hand
  • Double Down: Double your bet for one more card
  • Split: If you have two of the same card, split into two hands

4 Winning Hands

After all players act, the dealer reveals their hand:

  • Blackjack: Ace + 10-value card (3:2 payout)
  • Win: Higher total than dealer without busting (1:1 payout)
  • Push: Same total as dealer (bet returned)
  • Loss: Lower total than dealer or you bust

Blackjack Strategy Chart

Use this basic strategy guide to make optimal decisions based on your hand and the dealer's up card:

Your Hand Dealer's Up Card Action
8 or less Any Hit
9 3-6 Double Down
10 or 11 2-9 Double Down
12-16 7-Ace Hit
17+ Any Stand
Ace + 2-6 5-6 Double Down

*Note: Always split Aces and 8s. Never split 5s or 10-value cards.
