The Ultimate Guide to Caring for Your Down Gear

April 12, 2025 â€¢ 10 min read

Down sleeping bags and quilts are significant investments. With proper care, they can maintain their loft and warmth for decades. Follow these guidelines to keep your down gear performing at its best.

When to Wash Your Down Gear

Down doesn't need frequent washing, but there are clear signs when it's time:

  • Visible dirt or stains on the fabric
  • Noticeable decrease in loft (fluffiness)
  • Odors that don't disappear after airing out
  • After 20-30 nights of use (average)

Pro Tip

Spot clean small stains with a damp cloth and mild soap rather than washing the entire bag. This minimizes wear on the down clusters.

Step-by-Step Washing Guide

1. Prepare Your Gear

Close all zippers and fasteners. Shake out any loose dirt. Check for and repair any tears before washing.

2. Choose the Right Detergent

Use a down-specific cleaner like Nikwax Down Wash or Grangers Down Wash. Regular detergents can strip natural oils from down.

3. Machine Washing

Use a front-loading machine (no center agitator) on gentle cycle with warm water. Add tennis balls or dryer balls to help break up clumps.

4. Drying Properly

Dry on low heat with dryer balls. This may take 2-3 cycles. Ensure all down is completely dry to prevent mildew.

Storage Recommendations

How you store down gear significantly impacts its lifespan:

  • Never store compressed in its stuff sack
  • Use the large storage bag provided by manufacturer
  • Hang in a cool, dry place if possible
  • Avoid plastic bags which can trap moisture

Field Care Tips

During trips, follow these practices to maintain your down:

  • Air out your bag daily when possible
  • Use a sleeping bag liner to reduce dirt
  • Shake your bag each morning to redistribute down
  • Keep away from campfire sparks

When to Consider Professional Cleaning

For heavily soiled gear or if you're unsure about washing yourself, professional cleaning services specialize in down products and can:

  • Remove stubborn stains
  • Re-waterproof the shell fabric
  • Ensure thorough drying
