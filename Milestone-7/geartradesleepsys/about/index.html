Our Story: The GearTrade Journey

GearTrade began in 2012 in Okotoks, Alberta, founded by a passionate outdoor adventurer who saw a need for a specialized outdoor gear marketplace. From humble beginnings in a basement, the idea grew into a trusted name in ultralight sleep systems across North America.

In 2018, Jesse and Michelle Hebenton took over with fresh ideas and industry expertise. Jesse’s background in Eco Tourism and Outdoor Leadership, paired with Michelle’s business skills, has helped GearTrade thrive.

Despite our growth, we continue to offer personal touches — handwritten notes, expert advice, and genuine care for our customers' outdoor adventures.

Key Milestones

2012 – The Beginning

Started in a basement in Okotoks, Alberta, offering a handful of quality outdoor products.

2015 – First Warehouse

Moved to a warehouse to accommodate our expanding product line and customer base.

2018 – New Ownership

Jesse and Michelle Hebenton brought fresh leadership, transforming the company with a clear vision and customer focus.

2020 – Retail Store Opens

Opened our first physical store in downtown Okotoks for local customers and explorers.

2024 – Nationwide Expansion

Expanded shipping across Canada, with a strong focus on ultralight sleep gear and high-performance products.
