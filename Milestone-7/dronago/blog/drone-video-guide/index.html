Transform aerial footage into memorable keepsakes that travelers will pay premium prices for

Unlike traditional trinkets, personalized aerial videos capture memories in breathtaking ways that command higher prices and better profit margins. 5 Must-Shot Drone Souvenir Sequences

These proven shot sequences sell best across all markets:

1. The Hero Reveal

  • • Start tight on subject (person/couple/family)
  • • Slowly ascend while zooming out
  • • Reveal stunning surroundings
  • • Perfect for landmark backdrops

2. The Orbital

  • • Circle around subject at consistent altitude
  • • Maintain subject in center frame
  • • Show changing background perspectives

3. The Fly-Through

  • • Identify natural "tunnels" (tree arches, rock formations)
  • • Fly smoothly through at constant speed
  • • Reveal subject at the end point

4. The Time Transition

  • • Capture same location at golden hour and sunset
  • • Edit with smooth time-lapse transition
  • • Showcases destination's different moods

5. The Signature Move

  • • Develop your unique shot sequence
  • • Example: Spiral up from subject to wide view
  • • Becomes your recognizable style

Legal Considerations

Stay compliant with these essential practices:

  • • Know local drone laws: FAA Part 107 in US, similar regulations elsewhere
  • • Get location permits: Many parks/venues require commercial filming permits
  • • Use model releases: Have clients sign release forms (DronaGO provides templates)
  • • Carry insurance: Liability coverage protects your business
