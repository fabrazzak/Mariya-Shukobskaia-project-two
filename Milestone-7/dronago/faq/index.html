Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about DronaGO

Getting Started

DronaGO is available for free on the Google Play Store. Simply search for "DronaGO" or visit our Google Play listing to download and install the app.

After installation, open the app and follow the onboarding tutorial to set up your account and start creating videos.

DronaGO is completely free to download and use. There are no upfront costs or subscriptions. We only take a small percentage when you make a sale through the app.

DronaGO works on most Android devices running Android 5.0 (Lollipop) or later. The app is optimized for:

  • Smartphones with at least 2GB RAM
  • Tablets with 7-inch or larger screens
  • Devices with cameras of 8MP or higher resolution

We recommend using devices with more recent Android versions for the best performance.

Creating Videos

Our AI analyzes your photos and videos to automatically create professional-looking video souvenirs. The process works in three simple steps:

  1. Select or capture your media (photos/videos)
  2. Choose a style or let the AI recommend one
  3. The AI edits, adds transitions, music and effects

You can then further customize the video before saving or selling it.

Yes! DronaGO supports media from various sources. You can import:

  • Drone footage (DJI, Autel, etc.)
  • DSLR or mirrorless camera videos
  • Action camera footage (GoPro, Insta360)
  • Smartphone videos from any device

The app will automatically adjust formats and resolutions for optimal editing.

Creating custom templates is easy:

  1. Create a video with your preferred style, transitions and music
  2. Go to "My Templates" in the app menu
  3. Tap "Save as Template" and give it a name
  4. Your template will now be available for future projects

You can create unlimited templates to match different occasions or client preferences.

Selling Videos

DronaGO uses Stripe for secure payments. Here's how it works:

  • Clients pay through the app using credit/debit cards
  • Funds are processed securely through Stripe
  • Your earnings (minus our small service fee) are available immediately
  • You can withdraw to your bank account anytime

Typical withdrawal times are 1-3 business days depending on your bank.

DronaGO takes a 20% service fee from each sale. This covers:

  • Payment processing fees
  • App maintenance and updates
  • Server costs for video storage and delivery
  • Customer support

You keep 80% of every sale, and there are no hidden fees.

You have full control over pricing:

  1. Go to "Settings" then "Pricing"
  2. Set default prices for different video lengths
  3. Adjust prices for individual projects as needed
  4. Offer discounts or packages for multiple videos

We recommend researching local market rates for similar services to set competitive prices.

Troubleshooting

Try these troubleshooting steps:

  1. Close other apps to free up memory
  2. Restart your device
  3. Update DronaGO to the latest version
  4. Clear the app cache (Settings > Apps > DronaGO > Storage > Clear Cache)
  5. For large projects, try working with shorter clips

If issues persist, please contact our support team with details about your device and when the crashes occur.

Video export times depend on several factors:

  • Length of your video (longer videos take more time)
  • Complexity of effects and transitions
  • Your device's processing power
  • Available storage space on your device
  • Internet connection speed (for cloud processing)

For best results, ensure you have at least 1GB of free storage and a stable internet connection.

Deleted projects may be recoverable for up to 30 days:

  1. Go to "My Projects" in the app menu
  2. Tap the "Trash" icon in the top right corner
  3. Find your deleted project and tap "Restore"

Note: Projects permanently deleted from the trash cannot be recovered. We recommend regularly backing up important projects to your device or cloud storage.
