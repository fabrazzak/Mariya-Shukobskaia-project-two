Our Story & Mission

Empowering creators worldwide with innovative AI video tools

Who We Are

DronaGO is a product of NOCADIS SP Z O O, a innovative technology company based in Poznań, Canada. Founded with a vision to democratize video creation, we've developed cutting-edge AI tools that make professional video editing accessible to everyone.

Our team consists of passionate developers, designers, and video professionals who believe in the power of technology to create new economic opportunities. We're committed to building tools that help people monetize their creativity without complex software or expensive equipment.

Our Mission

We're on a mission to revolutionize how people create and monetize video content. DronaGO was born from a simple idea: everyone should be able to create professional-quality videos and earn money from their creativity, regardless of technical skills or budget.

By combining AI technology with an intuitive mobile interface, we're breaking down barriers in the video production industry. Our goal is to empower millions of creators worldwide to turn their passion into income.

The Technology

DronaGO leverages advanced artificial intelligence to automate complex video editing tasks. Our proprietary algorithms analyze footage, select the best moments, apply cinematic transitions, and synchronize everything with music - all in seconds.

The app integrates secure payment processing through Stripe, ensuring creators get paid quickly and safely. Our cloud infrastructure handles the heavy processing, so users can create stunning videos even on older smartphones.

We continuously update our AI models and template library to keep DronaGO at the forefront of mobile video creation technology.
