Responsible Video Creation

Our commitment to ethical AI video production and user safety

Our Responsibility Commitment

At DronaGO, we believe in empowering creativity while maintaining the highest standards of responsibility. Our AI video maker is designed to be a force for good, helping users create meaningful content while respecting privacy and ethical boundaries.

We recognize that with powerful technology comes great responsibility. That's why we've implemented safeguards and guidelines to ensure our platform is used appropriately by all creators.

Ethical Video Creation Guidelines

Respect Privacy

Always obtain proper consent before capturing or sharing videos of individuals. Our app includes features to help you manage releases and permissions.

  • Clearly explain how videos will be used
  • Respect requests to not be filmed
  • Obtain model releases for commercial use
  • Be mindful of filming in private spaces

Create Authentic Content

While our AI enhances your videos, we encourage maintaining authenticity:

  • Don't misrepresent locations or events
  • Avoid creating misleading or deceptive content
  • Clearly label AI-enhanced elements when appropriate
  • Respect intellectual property rights

Safe Monetization Practices

When using DronaGO to earn money, we recommend:

  • Set fair and transparent pricing
  • Deliver what you promise to clients
  • Provide excellent customer service
  • Pay appropriate taxes on your earnings

Age Restrictions & Content Safety

DronaGO is intended for users aged 18 and older. While our app is rated for general audiences, we recommend:

For Creators

Be mindful of content you create and share, especially when working with minors. Always obtain parental consent when filming children.

For Clients

Review content before purchasing. Our secure payment system allows you to preview videos before completing transactions.

We employ automated systems and human review to identify and remove inappropriate content, but we rely on our community to flag any concerns.

Reporting Concerns

If you encounter content that violates our community guidelines or terms of service, please report it immediately:

In-App Reporting

Use the "Report" button within the DronaGO app to flag inappropriate content or behavior.

Email Support

Contact our support team at info@dronago.app with details of your concern.

All reports are reviewed by our moderation team, and we take appropriate action within 24 hours of receiving a valid report.
