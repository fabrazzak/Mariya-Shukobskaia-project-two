1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device (computer, smartphone, or tablet) when you visit a website or use an app. They are widely used to make websites and apps work more efficiently, as well as to provide information to the owners.

Similar technologies include:

  • Web beacons: Tiny graphics with unique identifiers
  • Local storage: Data stored in your browser
  • Tracking pixels: Used in email marketing
