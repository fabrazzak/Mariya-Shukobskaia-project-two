Group B Expansion Update Now Live

John Doe 12 Comments May 15, 2025
Home » Blog » Spring Championship

After four weeks of intense competition across eight challenging stages, we're proud to announce the winners of Art of Rally's first seasonal championship! The Spring Rally Championship brought together over 15,000 competitors from around the world, all vying for the top spot on the podium.

Overall Championship Results

The championship was decided by combining each player's best times across all eight stages. Here are our top three champions:

Position Player Country Total Time Car Used
1st RallyMaster99 Finland 32:45.21 Lancia Stratos
2nd DriftKing47 Japan 32:52.78 Ford Escort RS
3rd GravelQueen Sweden 33:01.45 Porsche 911

Championship Highlights

The competition was fierce throughout the entire season, with these particularly memorable moments:

1. The Snowy Showdown

The "Nordic Nightmare" stage proved to be the most decisive of the championship. RallyMaster99's daring run through the snow-covered forest, where they took several corners in full drift, gave them a 5-second lead that would prove crucial in the final standings.

2. Photo Finish in the Desert

The "Sahara Scramble" saw DriftKing47 and GravelQueen separated by just 0.03 seconds after 8 minutes of racing. This was the closest stage finish in Art of Rally competitive history.

3. Underdog Triumph

Special mention goes to first-time competitor Speedster22 who placed 7th overall using the humble Mini Cooper S, proving that driver skill can overcome car limitations.

Prize Breakdown

Our champions will receive these exclusive rewards:

1st Place

  • Exclusive golden livery for all cars
  • Early access to next car DLC
  • $500 gift card
  • Champion's profile feature

2nd Place

  • Exclusive silver livery for all cars
  • Early access to next car DLC

All participants who completed at least 5 stages received a special "Spring Challenger" decal for their cars.
