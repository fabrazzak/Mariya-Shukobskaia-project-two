Group B Expansion Update Now Live

John Doe 12 Comments May 15, 2025
Home » Blog » Group B Expansion Update

The wait is over! We're thrilled to announce that the 1.5 update for Art of Rally is now available, bringing the legendary Group B rally cars to your mobile devices. This major expansion adds five iconic vehicles from rallying's most extreme era, along with three new challenging stages inspired by real-world locations.

The Group B Legends

Group B was rallying's most spectacular and dangerous era, with cars pushing the limits of power and technology. We've meticulously recreated five of these beasts, each with their unique handling characteristics:

New Rally Stages

The update introduces three breathtaking new stages that will test even the most skilled drivers:

1. Monte Vertigo

Inspired by the famous Monte Carlo rally routes, this stage combines tight mountain hairpins with long downhill straights. The changing weather conditions will keep you on your toes as you navigate between dry asphalt and icy patches.

2. Safari Scramble

Our take on the legendary African rally stages, featuring wide-open savannah sections and treacherous river crossings. This is the longest stage in Art of Rally, requiring careful pace management.

3. Nordic Nightmare

A snow-covered forest stage with deceptive corners and hidden crests. The low-grip conditions make this one of the most technical challenges in the game, especially in the Group B monsters.

New Features & Improvements

Beyond the new cars and stages, the 1.5 update includes several quality-of-life improvements:

  • Advanced Physics Model: More realistic weight transfer and suspension behavior, especially noticeable with the Group B cars
  • Customizable HUD: Move and resize on-screen controls to your preference
  • Replay System: Save and share your best runs (supports up to 30 seconds of footage)
  • Performance Optimizations: Smoother experience on mid-range devices
  • New Camera Angles: Additional cockpit and bumper cam options
"Group B represents the pinnacle of rallying's golden age. Capturing the raw power and handling characteristics of these cars has been both a challenge and a privilege for our team."
- Mika Lehtinen, Lead Developer

How to Access the New Content

The Group B cars are unlocked through normal progression in Career Mode. You'll need to complete the 1980s championship to access them. The new stages are available immediately in Time Trial and Free Roam modes, and will appear in Daily Challenges.

We can't wait to see how you handle these legendary machines! Share your best times and most spectacular crashes with #ArtofRallyGroupB on social media.
