Frequently Asked Questions

You can report bugs directly through the in-game menu by going to Settings > Help > Report a Bug. Alternatively, you can use the contact form on this page and select "Bug Report" as your subject.

We're always evaluating new platforms for Art of Rally. Currently, the game is available on mobile devices through the App Store and Google Play. Follow our social media channels for announcements about new platform releases.

Refunds are processed through the platform you purchased the game on (Google Play or App Store). Please contact their customer support directly for refund requests. For in-app purchases, you may also request a refund through the platform's purchase history section.

Art of Rally requires Android 8.0 or later, or iOS 12.0 or later. The game is compatible with most devices released in the last 4 years. For optimal performance, we recommend devices with at least 2GB RAM and a modern GPU.

We occasionally run beta tests for upcoming features. You can join our Discord server or follow our social media channels to get notified about beta testing opportunities. On Android, you can sometimes opt into beta versions through the Google Play Store page for Art of Rally.
