Our Ethical Cacao Sourcing Journey

By Javier Morales, Head of Sourcing May 28, 2025 10 min read

At ChocoSol Imports, we believe exceptional chocolate begins with ethical cacao sourcing. For over a decade, we've built direct relationships with small-scale farmers across Latin America, creating a sustainable supply chain that benefits both growers and chocolate lovers. This is our story.

Why Ethical Sourcing Matters

The global chocolate industry has long been plagued by unfair practices, with many farmers earning less than $1 per day despite producing the key ingredient for a $100 billion industry. At ChocoSol, we're committed to changing this narrative through:

  • Direct trade relationships that bypass exploitative middlemen
  • Prices that average 30-40% above Fair Trade standards
  • Long-term contracts that provide stability for farming families
  • Organic and regenerative farming support
  • Community development initiatives in farming regions
  • Transparency from bean to bar

The Farmer Partnership Model

Our approach goes beyond fair pricing to create true partnerships:

Pre-Harvest

Technical Support & Financing

We provide agronomists to help farmers improve yields and quality through sustainable methods, plus advance payments to cover harvest costs.

Harvest

Quality Control Training

Our field technicians train farmers in proper fermentation and drying techniques to maximize bean quality and value.

Post-Harvest

Direct Purchase & Premiums

We buy directly at our guaranteed prices, paying additional quality premiums for exceptional lots.

Ongoing

Community Investments

A portion of profits funds school programs, clean water projects, and healthcare in partner communities.

"Before partnering with ChocoSol, we struggled to earn enough from cacao to send our children to school. Now we have stable income, training to improve our harvests, and our community has a new health clinic. This is the kind of trade that changes lives."

Maria Gutierrez

Cooperative Leader, Esmeraldas, Ecuador

Sustainability in Action

Our commitment extends to environmental stewardship through:

Agroforestry

90% of our cacao is grown under diverse canopy cover, preserving biodiversity

Organic Practices

100% of partner farms use organic methods, avoiding harmful pesticides

Water Conservation

Implemented rainwater harvesting and efficient irrigation systems

Carbon Neutral

Solar-powered drying facilities and carbon offset programs

Traceability: From Farm to Bar

We maintain complete transparency throughout our supply chain:

  1. Farm Identification

    Each harvest is tagged with the specific farm or cooperative

  2. Batch Tracking

    Beans are kept separate through fermentation, drying, and shipping

  3. Quality Control

    Every batch is evaluated for flavor profile and quality upon arrival

  4. Bar Coding

    Finished products include codes tracing back to origin
