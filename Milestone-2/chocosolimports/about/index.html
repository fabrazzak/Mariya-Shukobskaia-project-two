Our Chocolate Story

Ethically sourced, artisanally crafted, passionately shared

From Bean to Bar

The journey of ChocoSol Imports

2005

Founding Vision

ChocoSol Imports was born from a simple idea: to bring the purest, most ethically sourced cacao to chocolate lovers while supporting sustainable farming communities.

2008

First Direct Relationships

We established our first direct trade partnerships with small-scale cacao farmers in Ecuador and Peru, ensuring fair compensation and quality control from the source.

2012

Calgary Workshop

Opened our artisan chocolate workshop in Calgary, where we could control every step of the process from roasting to tempering for perfect results.

2016

Retail Store Launch

Expanded to our flagship retail location in downtown Calgary, allowing customers to experience our chocolate-making process firsthand.

Present

Growing Community

Today, we partner with 12 farming cooperatives across Latin America and continue to innovate in low-sugar, high-quality dark chocolate.

Our Mission

At ChocoSol Imports, we're committed to transforming the chocolate industry through:

  • Ethical Sourcing

    Direct relationships with farmers that pay 30-40% above fair trade standards

  • Healthier Chocolate

    Pioneering low-sugar formulations that highlight cacao's natural complexity

  • Sustainability

    Carbon-neutral shipping and 100% compostable packaging

  • Artisan Craft

    Small-batch production preserving traditional techniques with modern precision

Our Artisan Process

How we transform cacao into exceptional chocolate

1

Direct Sourcing

We personally select cacao beans from our partner farms, ensuring perfect ripeness and quality.

2

Slow Roasting

Our proprietary low-temperature roasting develops complex flavors without bitterness.

3

Stone Grinding

Traditional Mexican metate stones grind cacao for 72 hours to achieve unparalleled smoothness.

4

Precise Tempering

Expert chocolate makers carefully temper each batch for perfect snap and shine.

5

Hand Wrapping

Every bar is lovingly wrapped by hand in our Calgary workshop.
