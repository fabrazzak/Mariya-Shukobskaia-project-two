Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about Everybody's RPG: Reboot

How do I recover my lost game account?

If you've linked your game account to Google Play or Game Center, simply reinstall the game and log in with the same account. For unlinked accounts, please contact our support team with your player ID (found in settings) and any purchase receipts to help us recover your progress.

Why am I not receiving offline rewards?

Offline rewards are calculated based on your current stage progress and team power. Make sure you have stable internet connection when closing the game. If the issue persists, try restarting the app or check if your device has any battery optimization settings that might affect background processes.

How often does the game receive updates?

We release major updates with new content every 4-6 weeks, with smaller balance patches and bug fixes as needed. Follow our official social media channels or check the in-game announcements for update schedules and patch notes.

Are there any plans for PvP or guild features?

Yes! We're currently developing competitive PvP modes and guild systems based on player feedback. These features are scheduled for release in our Q3 update. Stay tuned for more details in our developer blogs!

How can I report a bug or suggest a feature?

We welcome all feedback! You can use the contact form on this page, join our Discord community, or post on our official forums. For bug reports, please include details about your device, game version, and steps to reproduce the issue.
