How to Play Everybody's RPG: Reboot

Master the idle fantasy RPG with our comprehensive guide to heroes, battles, and progression systems

  • How to Play

Getting Started

Welcome to Everybody's RPG: Reboot! This idle fantasy RPG offers endless adventure with minimal effort. Here's how to begin your journey:

1

Download and Install

Get the game from the Google Play Store and install it on your device. The game is free to play with optional in-app purchases.

2

Complete the Tutorial

The interactive tutorial will guide you through basic controls, hero recruitment, and your first battles. Pay attention as it explains core mechanics.

3

Claim Your Starter Rewards

Check your mailbox for free starter packs and login rewards. These will give you a significant boost in the early game.

Pro Tip

Don't skip the tutorial rewards! They include powerful starter heroes and resources that will help you progress faster.

Hero System

Heroes are the core of your team. Understanding how to recruit and develop them is key to success.

Recruiting Heroes

You can obtain new heroes through several methods:

  • Gacha Summons: Use premium currency or summon tickets to recruit random heroes of varying rarities
  • Event Rewards: Participate in limited-time events to earn special heroes
  • Story Progression: Unlock certain heroes by completing campaign stages
  • Hero Shards: Collect shards to piece together specific heroes

Hero Rarities

Heroes come in different rarities that determine their base stats and potential:

Rarity Tiers

  • Common (1★): Basic heroes with simple skills
  • Uncommon (2★): Improved stats and abilities
  • Rare (3★): Strong heroes with useful skills
  • Epic (4★): Powerful heroes with unique abilities
  • Legendary (5★): The strongest heroes with game-changing skills

Hero Development

Improve your heroes through various upgrade systems:

1

Leveling Up

Use EXP potions or battle to increase hero levels, improving their base stats.

2

Promotion

Gather materials to promote heroes to higher star ratings, unlocking new skills and stat growth.

3

Equipment

Equip gear to boost stats further. Upgrade equipment using materials from battles.

4

Skill Enhancement

Use skill books to upgrade hero abilities, making them more powerful or reducing cooldowns.

Battle Mechanics

Understand how combat works to build effective teams and strategies.

Basic Battle Flow

  1. Team Formation: Select up to 5 heroes to form your battle team
  2. Stage Selection: Choose a stage to challenge based on your current power
  3. Auto-Battle: Heroes automatically attack using basic skills
  4. Active Skills: Manually trigger powerful abilities when their gauges fill
  5. Victory/Rewards: Earn rewards based on your performance

Auto-Battle Tip

While the game plays itself, paying attention to skill timings and manually activating them at crucial moments can help you clear tougher content.

Team Composition

Effective teams balance different roles:

Recommended Team Setup

  • 1-2 Tanks (high defense, taunt enemies)
  • 1-2 Damage Dealers (high attack power)
  • 1 Healer (restores team health)
  • 1 Support (provides buffs/debuffs)

Adjust based on the stage requirements and your available heroes.

Progression Systems

Multiple systems work together to strengthen your account over time.

Campaign Stages

The main progression path with increasingly difficult stages that reward:

  • Hero EXP
  • Equipment and upgrade materials
  • Currency (gold, gems)
  • Story unlocks

Idle Rewards

Even when offline, your team continues to progress:

  • Earn resources based on your highest cleared stage
  • Collect rewards up to a 12-hour cap
  • Upgrade the Idle Reward system to increase efficiency

Offline Progression Tip

Check in at least twice daily to claim idle rewards before reaching the cap to maximize gains.

Mining System

Assign heroes to mines to passively gather resources:

  1. Unlock mines by progressing through the campaign
  2. Assign heroes to each mine (higher rarity heroes yield better results)
  3. Collect resources periodically
  4. Upgrade mines to improve production rates

Resources Management

Effectively managing your resources is crucial for steady progress.

Key Resources

Currency Guide

  • Gold: Basic currency for upgrades and purchases
  • Gems: Premium currency for summons and special items
  • Energy: Used to attempt stages (regenerates over time)
  • Hero EXP: Levels up your heroes
  • Upgrade Materials: Various items for hero and equipment improvement

Spending Priorities

For optimal progress, prioritize:

  1. Hero summons early game to build your roster
  2. Energy refreshes to progress faster
  3. Key hero upgrades for your main team
  4. Mine upgrades for better passive income

Resource Warning

Avoid spreading resources too thin across many heroes. Focus on a core team first before diversifying.

Advanced Strategies

Once you've mastered the basics, these tips will help you excel.

Hero Synergies

Certain heroes work exceptionally well together:

  • Heroes with complementary skills (e.g., freeze + shatter effects)
  • Faction bonuses (using heroes from the same faction)
  • Skill chains (one hero's ability sets up another's)

Event Participation

Regular events offer valuable rewards:

  • Check the event calendar frequently
  • Focus on events that reward your current needs
  • Prepare resources in advance for summoning events

Meta Shifts

The game evolves over time:

  • Balance patches may change hero effectiveness
  • New heroes may counter previous strategies
  • Stay flexible and adapt your team compositions

Frequently Asked Questions

Gameplay Questions

Q: Is this game completely idle?

A: While the game features extensive idle mechanics, active play (team building, skill activation, strategy) will yield better results than pure idle play.

Q: How often should I check the game?

A: For optimal progress, checking every 4-6 hours to claim idle rewards and manage resources is recommended, but you can play at your own pace.

Technical Questions

Q: Does the game support account linking?

A: Yes, you can link your account to Google Play or Facebook to save progress across devices.

Q: What devices are supported?

A: The game runs on most Android devices running Android 7.0 or later with at least 2GB RAM.

Progression Questions

Q: I'm stuck on a stage. What should I do?

A: Try these steps: 1) Upgrade your heroes and equipment, 2) Adjust your team composition, 3) Farm earlier stages for resources, 4) Check if you have unclaimed rewards.
