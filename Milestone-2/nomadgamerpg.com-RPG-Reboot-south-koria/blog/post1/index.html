New Hero Update: Valkyrie Queen Joins the Battle

The latest update to Everybody's RPG: Reboot introduces the Valkyrie Queen, a powerful legendary hero that brings new strategies and team compositions to the game. As the first flying-type legendary hero, she offers unique abilities that can turn the tide of battle in your favor.

Hero Overview

The Valkyrie Queen is a frontline warrior with exceptional survivability and team support capabilities. She specializes in protecting allies while dealing consistent damage to enemies. Her divine heritage grants her abilities that can resurrect fallen comrades and shield the team from devastating attacks.

Base Stats at Level 100

Stat Value Growth

Role: Frontline Warrior/Support
Faction: Celestial
Recommended Artifacts: Wings of Salvation, Divine Protector's Armor

Abilities Breakdown

Divine Slash (Basic Attack) Physical

Attacks a single enemy with 120% ATK damage. Has a 30% chance to inflict Divine Mark for 2 turns (marked enemies take 15% more damage from all sources).

Upgrade Effect (Level 5): Chance to inflict Divine Mark increases to 50%.

Aegis of the Valiant Support

At the start of battle and every 3 turns, grants all allies a Divine Shield that absorbs damage equal to 20% of Valkyrie Queen's MAX HP for 1 turn. While the shield is active, allies gain 15% ATK.

Upgrade Effect (Level 5): Shield value increases to 25% of MAX HP and ATK bonus increases to 20%.

Wings of Resurrection Ultimate

When an ally dies, there's a 50% chance to immediately resurrect them with 30% HP and grant them Divine Protection (reduces damage taken by 30%) for 2 turns. This ability can only trigger once per battle per ally.

Upgrade Effect (Level 5): Resurrection chance increases to 75% and revived allies recover 50% HP.

Queen's Authority (Passive) Aura

All Celestial faction allies gain 15% ATK and 10% DEF. Whenever an ally falls below 50% HP, Valkyrie Queen immediately taunts all enemies for 1 turn.

Upgrade Effect (Level 5): ATK bonus increases to 20% and DEF bonus increases to 15%.

Acquisition Methods

The Valkyrie Queen can be obtained through the following methods:

  • Legendary Summon: Available in the Celestial Banner with a 0.5% drop rate (increased to 1.5% during her launch event)
  • Heroic Shards: Can be collected from Celestial Faction events and high-level dungeons
  • Event Rewards: Currently available as a top reward in the "Divine Ascent" event (first week only)

Final Thoughts

The Valkyrie Queen is a versatile hero that brings both defensive utility and offensive support to any team. While she shines in Celestial faction teams, her abilities are valuable in any composition that needs protection against burst damage. Her unique resurrection mechanic can completely turn around losing battles, making her one of the most valuable heroes in the current meta.

For players looking to push high-level content or compete in PvP, investing in the Valkyrie Queen is highly recommended. Her combination of tankiness, team support, and faction bonuses makes her a future-proof addition to any roster.
