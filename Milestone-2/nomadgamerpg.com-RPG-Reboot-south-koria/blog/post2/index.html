Season 3 Battle Pass: Exclusive Rewards and Challenges

Season 3 of Everybody's RPG: Reboot is here, bringing with it an exciting new Battle Pass filled with exclusive rewards, challenges, and content. This season's theme is "Realm of the Ancients," with all rewards following an ancient civilization aesthetic.

Battle Pass Overview

The Season 3 Battle Pass runs from June 5 to August 5, 2025, giving players exactly two months to complete all 50 tiers of rewards. Like previous seasons, there are two versions of the pass:

Feature Free Pass Premium Pass (£9.99)
Total Rewards 25 50 (including all Free rewards)
Exclusive Hero Skin - Ancient Warchief (Barbarian King)
Legendary Hero - Terra Guardian at Tier 30
Premium Currency 500 Gems 2,500 Gems
XP Boost - +20% Battle Pass XP

Key Rewards Breakdown

Here are some of the most notable rewards you can earn this season:

Tier 1 (Premium): Ancient Warchief Skin

Immediately unlock this exclusive skin for Barbarian King when you purchase the Premium Pass. Changes all ability effects to ancient stone-themed visuals.

Tier 30 (Premium): Terra Guardian Hero

This earth-based tank hero specializes in protecting allies with stone barriers and earthquake attacks. Normally only available through Legendary Summons.

Tier 15 (Free): Ancient Tome Artifact

New artifact that increases all heroes' skill damage by 15% and provides a chance to reset cooldowns when using ultimate abilities.

Tier 50 (Premium): Relic Frame Avatar

Exclusive profile frame that shows your dedication to completing the entire Battle Pass. Animated with floating ancient runes.

Season Challenges

Complete these special challenges to earn bonus Battle Pass XP and accelerate your progression:

Ancient Conqueror Hard

Complete all 50 Battle Pass tiers before the season ends. Reward: 5,000 Gems and exclusive "Ancient Champion" title.

Team of the Ancients Medium

Win 50 battles using a team where all heroes are wearing Ancient themed skins. Reward: Ancient Hero Chest (guaranteed epic or better ancient hero).

Relic Collector Easy

Collect 100 Ancient Relics from the new event dungeon. Reward: 1,000 Gems and 10 Summon Scrolls.

Progression Tip

Focus on completing daily and weekly quests first, as these provide the most consistent XP. The "Ancient Conqueror" challenge requires completing about 4 tiers per day, but you can catch up later in the season by focusing on high-XP challenges.

Premium Pass owners should prioritize the "Team of the Ancients" challenge early to maximize the use of their free Ancient Warchief skin.

New Event Dungeon: Temple of the Ancients

Alongside the Battle Pass, Season 3 introduces a new limited-time dungeon that provides additional ways to earn Battle Pass XP and special rewards:

  • First Clear Rewards: Each difficulty level (Normal, Hard, Expert) gives 2 Battle Pass tiers worth of XP on first completion
  • Ancient Relics: Collect these from the dungeon to complete the Relic Collector challenge
  • Boss Drops: Chance to obtain Ancient-themed equipment with special set bonuses

Is the Premium Pass Worth It?

For players who regularly complete the Battle Pass, the Premium version offers excellent value:

  • The Terra Guardian hero alone would cost approximately £15-20 if obtained through summons
  • The 2,500 Gems included are worth about £10 at the shop exchange rate
  • The exclusive skin and avatar frame cannot be obtained any other way

Even if you only complete half the tiers, the Premium Pass still provides better value than purchasing items individually from the shop. However, casual players who don't expect to reach at least tier 30 may want to stick with the Free Pass.

Final Thoughts

Season 3 brings one of the most generous Battle Pass offerings yet, with the Terra Guardian hero being particularly valuable for both new and veteran players. The ancient theme provides a fresh visual style, and the new dungeon adds meaningful content beyond just the progression track.

Whether you choose Free or Premium, be sure to check the challenges tab regularly and focus on completing the highest-XP tasks first. With smart planning, even free players can reach tier 25 and claim the Ancient Tome artifact.
