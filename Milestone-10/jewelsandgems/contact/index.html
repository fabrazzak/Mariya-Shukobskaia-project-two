Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions before you contact us

Our team aims to respond to all inquiries within 24-48 hours. During peak times or weekends, it may take slightly longer. For urgent issues, please include "URGENT" in your subject line.

Please include your device model, operating system version, a description of the issue, and any error messages you're seeing. For account-related issues, include your username (but not password).
