1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy applies to your use of Jewels & Gems: Drop Game ("Game"), developed and published by PikehuntDev ("we," "us," or "our").

By downloading, installing, or using the Game, you consent to the collection, use, and sharing of your information as described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our Game.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed about our information practices.

2. Information Collection and Use

When you play Jewels & Gems: Drop Game, we may collect certain information about your device and gameplay to provide and improve our services:

Device Information: We may collect information about your mobile device, including device model, operating system version, unique device identifiers, and mobile network information.

Gameplay Data: We collect information about your interactions with the Game, including game progress, levels completed, items collected, and in-game purchases.

Performance Data: We may collect data about the Game's performance, including crash reports and performance metrics to help us identify and fix issues.

Advertising Data: When you interact with ads in the Game, we may collect information about those interactions, such as which ads were shown and whether you clicked on them.

We use this information to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the Game
  • Develop new features and content
  • Analyze and understand how our Game is used
  • Personalize your gaming experience
  • Show relevant advertisements
  • Detect, prevent, and address technical issues

3. Unity Analytics

We use Unity Analytics to collect data about your interactions within the Game. Unity Analytics helps us understand how players engage with our Game so we can improve the player experience.

The information collected by Unity Analytics may include:

  • Game session length and frequency
  • Level progression and completion rates
  • In-game purchases and virtual currency usage
  • Technical data such as crashes and performance metrics
  • Device information for compatibility analysis

This data is aggregated and anonymized where possible. Unity Analytics does not collect personally identifiable information unless you voluntarily provide it.

For more information about Unity's privacy practices, please review the Unity Privacy Policy.

4. Unity Ads and Ad Mediation

Jewels & Gems: Drop Game utilizes Unity Ads to display advertisements. Unity Ads may collect and use information about you to provide personalized advertising.

Unity Ads may collect the following information:

  • Device identifiers (such as Advertising ID)
  • IP address
  • Device type and operating system
  • Gameplay information related to ad interactions
  • Information about ad views, clicks, and conversions

We also use Unity's ad mediation tool to manage and optimize ad performance across different ad networks. This helps us provide you with the best possible ad experience while maximizing revenue that supports the Game's development.

You can opt out of personalized advertising from Unity Ads by following the instructions in Unity's Privacy Policy. On Android devices, you can also reset your advertising identifier in your device settings.

Please note that opting out of personalized ads does not mean you won't see ads - it just means the ads you see may be less relevant to your interests.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the information we collect from or about you from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. These measures include:

  • Using encryption technologies for data transmission
  • Implementing access controls to restrict who can access your data
  • Regularly reviewing our data collection, storage, and processing practices
  • Working with reputable third-party service providers that implement strong security measures

However, no internet or email transmission is ever fully secure or error-free. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security. You should take special care in deciding what information you send to us via email or other electronic means.

6. Children's Privacy

Jewels & Gems: Drop Game does not knowingly collect or solicit any personal information from children under the age of 13. Our Game is intended for general audiences and is not specifically targeted at children under 13.

If we learn that we have collected personal information from a child under age 13 without parental consent, we will delete that information as quickly as possible. If you believe that we might have any information from or about a child under 13, please contact us immediately at info@jewelsandgems.app.

We encourage parents and guardians to monitor their children's online activities and to help enforce our Privacy Policy by instructing their children never to provide personal information through our Game without their permission.

7. Changes to Our Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, technology, legal requirements, and other factors. When we do, we will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy.

If we make material changes to how we treat your personal information, we will notify you through a notice in the Game or via other communication methods such as email.

We encourage you to periodically review this Privacy Policy to stay informed about our information practices and the ways you can help protect your privacy.

Your continued use of the Game after we make changes is deemed to be acceptance of those changes, so please check the policy periodically for updates.
