Advanced Strategies

Take your gameplay to the next level with these expert tips

Plan Your Drops

Always look 2-3 moves ahead. Position gems to create future merging opportunities rather than just immediate matches.

Create Space

Leave empty spaces between gem clusters to allow for easier maneuvering and unexpected gem drops.

Corner Strategy

Use the corners to store high-value gems until you can merge them, preventing accidental matches.

Chain Reactions

Set up multiple potential merges in sequence to create cascading combos for bonus points.

Save for Challenges

Don't use boosters on easy levels. Save them for when you're stuck or when you can maximize their effect.

Combo Boosters

Combine different boosters for amplified effects. For example, use a color changer with a bomb for maximum impact.

Daily Rewards

Log in daily to collect free boosters and build your inventory for when you really need them.

Strategic Placement

Place boosters in the center of the board for maximum coverage rather than at the edges.

Higher Value Gems

Focus on creating the highest value gems possible rather than many small merges for better scores.

Combo Multipliers

Quick successive merges multiply your score. Plan moves that trigger multiple merges in rapid succession.

Hidden Gem Bonus

Discovering the hidden gem gives massive points. Work toward revealing it as quickly as possible.

Daily Challenges

Complete daily challenges for bonus points and special rewards that boost your overall score.
