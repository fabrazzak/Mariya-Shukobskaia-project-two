What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit a website or use an app. They help the website or app remember information about your visit, which can make it easier to visit the site again and make the site more useful to you.

Cookies: Small text files stored on your device that help us recognize you and remember your preferences.

Local Storage: Allows us to store data locally in your browser for performance and functionality.

Session Storage: Similar to cookies but deleted when you close your browser.

Pixels/Tags: Tiny invisible images or code snippets that help us understand how you interact with our services.

Device Identifiers: Unique identifiers associated with your device that help us deliver personalized experiences.
