Common Questions

How do I reset my game progress?

Game progress is stored locally on your device. To reset, uninstall and reinstall the app. Note this will erase all your progress and coins.

Why are my coins missing?

Coins are tied to your device. If you reinstalled the app or cleared data, your coins may be lost. Make sure to back up your device regularly.

Support FAQs

Quick answers to common support questions

How do I report a bug in the game?

Please use the "Bug Report" option in our contact form. Include details about your device, game version, and steps to reproduce the issue. Screenshots or screen recordings are very helpful!

Why is the game not working on my device?

Word Game Wonders requires Android 5.1 or later. If you're experiencing issues, try these steps:

  • Restart your device
  • Check for app updates in Google Play
  • Clear the app cache (Settings > Apps > Word Game Wonders > Storage > Clear Cache)
  • Ensure you have at least 100MB free storage

How can I request a refund for in-app purchases?

All in-app purchases are processed by Google Play. To request a refund:

  1. Open Google Play Store
  2. Go to "Account" > "Purchase History"
  3. Find the purchase and select "Request Refund"
  4. Follow Google's refund process

Note: Refund eligibility depends on Google's policies and purchase timing.

Will there be more levels added to the game?

Yes! We regularly release updates with new levels. To ensure you get the latest content:

  • Enable auto-updates in Google Play
  • Follow us on social media for update announcements
  • Check the "What's New" section in the app description

Our current roadmap includes 500 new levels in the next update!

How do I change the game language?

Currently, Word Game Wonders automatically uses your device's language setting. Supported languages include:

English Spanish French German Italian Portuguese Russian Japanese

To change language, update your device settings. More languages coming soon!
