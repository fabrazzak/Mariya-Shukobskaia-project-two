Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Word Game Wonders

Is the game completely free to play?

Yes, Word Game Wonders: Crossword is free to download and play. The game contains optional in-app purchases for hints and coins, but all puzzles can be completed without spending money. You also earn free coins every 30 minutes.

Does the game require an internet connection?

No, once downloaded, you can play the game offline without any internet connection. However, some features like in-app purchases may require an internet connection temporarily.

How many levels does the game have?

Currently, the game features over 2000 unique levels with varying difficulty. We regularly add new levels through updates to keep the game fresh and challenging.

What devices are supported?

The game requires Android 5.1 or later. It's optimized for both phones and tablets. Unfortunately, it's not currently available for iOS devices.

How do I unlock new themes?

You can unlock new themes by earning coins through gameplay. Complete levels, find bonus words, and collect your free coins every 30 minutes to unlock beautiful background themes.
