Gameplay Basics

Understand the core mechanics of Word Game Wonders: Crossword

Objective of the Game

The goal of Word Game Wonders: Crossword is to find all the hidden words in each puzzle by swiping letters on the grid. Each level provides a clue indicating what words you need to find.

Find All Words

Complete the puzzle by discovering every word listed in the clues.

Discover Bonus Words

Find extra words not in the clue list to earn additional coins.

Earn Coins

Collect coins to unlock beautiful themes and get hints.

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to become a Word Game Wonders master

Step 1

Starting a Level

When you begin a level, you'll see a grid of letters and a clue at the top. The clue indicates the theme or category of words you need to find.

  • Read the clue carefully to understand what words to look for
  • Examine the letter grid for possible word placements
  • Words can be horizontal, vertical, or diagonal
  • Words can read forwards or backwards
Step 2

Finding Words

To find a word, swipe your finger across the letters in order. The letters will highlight as you select them.

  • Swipe horizontally, vertically, or diagonally to form words
  • Letters must be adjacent to each other
  • Release your finger when you've selected the complete word
  • Correct words will be crossed off the clue list
Step 3

Using Tools

When you're stuck, use the tools at the bottom of the screen to help you progress.

Hint

Reveals one letter from a random word (costs coins)

Shuffle

Rearranges the letters on the grid (free)

Search

Highlights all occurrences of a selected letter

Free Coins

Collect free coins every 30 minutes

Step 4

Completing Levels

When you find all the words in the clue list, you'll complete the level and earn rewards.

  • Find all words to complete the level
  • Discover bonus words for extra coins
  • Earn coins based on your performance
  • Use coins to unlock beautiful themes

Finding all bonus words in a level gives you a special bonus reward!

Advanced Tips

Take your word puzzle skills to the next level with these pro strategies

Look for Common Prefixes/Suffixes

Words often share common beginnings or endings. Look for sequences like "ING", "TION", "RE", or "UN" to help spot multiple words at once.

Check All Directions

Words can be hidden in any direction - horizontally, vertically, diagonally, and even backwards. Scan the grid systematically in all directions.

Prioritize Bonus Words

Finding bonus words gives you extra coins. Look for longer words or words that aren't in the clue list to maximize your rewards.

Manage Your Coins

Use hints sparingly. Collect your free coins every 30 minutes and complete levels without hints to save coins for harder puzzles.

Change Themes Strategically

Some themes provide better contrast between letters and background. Find one that's easy on your eyes for long play sessions.

Take Breaks

If you're stuck, take a short break. Fresh eyes often spot words you previously missed. The game has no time limits, so play at your own pace.
