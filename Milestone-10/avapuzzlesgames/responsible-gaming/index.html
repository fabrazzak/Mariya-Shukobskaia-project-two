Responsible Gaming

At Word Game Wonders, we believe in healthy gaming habits that enhance your life without becoming a burden.

Healthy Gaming Tips

Our Commitment

We're dedicated to promoting responsible gaming practices for all our players

Age-Appropriate Content

Our game is rated for ages 3+ with no inappropriate content. We encourage parents to monitor children's screen time and engage with them about healthy gaming habits.

No Time Pressure

Word Game Wonders has no time limits or penalties for taking breaks. Play at your own pace without artificial urgency that can lead to excessive play.

Transparent Purchases

All in-app purchases are clearly marked and optional. We provide ways to earn coins through gameplay and never use deceptive "dark patterns" to encourage spending.

Healthy Gaming Tips

Strategies to maintain a balanced relationship with gaming

Set Time Limits

  • Use phone timers or app limits to manage play time
  • Take regular breaks every 30-60 minutes
  • Establish "no gaming" times (meals, bedtime, etc.)

Balance Activities

  • Schedule gaming after completing responsibilities
  • Alternate gaming with physical activity
  • Maintain diverse hobbies beyond gaming

Mindful Gaming

  • Play with purpose, not just habit
  • Notice how gaming affects your mood
  • Avoid using games to escape problems

Social Connections

  • Balance online interactions with in-person ones
  • Discuss gaming habits with friends/family
  • Play together for shared experiences

Smart Spending

  • Set monthly spending limits for in-app purchases
  • Use parental controls for children's accounts
  • Take advantage of free coins and rewards

Sleep Hygiene

  • Avoid gaming 1 hour before bedtime
  • Use night mode to reduce blue light
  • Prioritize sleep over late-night gaming

Remember Why You Play

Games should be fun and enriching, not stressful or compulsive. If you're not enjoying the experience or it's interfering with your life, it may be time to reevaluate your habits.

Word Game Wonders is designed to be a positive part of your day, not your whole day. Play responsibly and enjoy the benefits of word puzzles without overindulgence.

Recognizing Problem Gambling

Gambling should be entertaining, not a way to make money or escape problems. Warning signs include chasing losses, lying about gambling, and risking important relationships or job opportunities.
