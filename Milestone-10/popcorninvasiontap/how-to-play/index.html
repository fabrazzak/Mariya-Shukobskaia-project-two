HOW TO PLAY

Master the art of popcorn popping and defend your theater from alien invaders!

Download & Start Playing

Game Basics

Your Mission

Aliens have invaded your movie theater, disguising themselves as innocent popcorn kernels. As the theater's employee, it's your job to pop these alien imposters before they can escape!

1

Tap to Pop

Simply tap on popcorn kernels to pop them. Each successful pop earns you points!

2

Watch for Aliens

Some kernels will reveal their true alien form - these are worth extra points!

3

Avoid Mistakes

Don't tap too quickly - hitting empty space will cost you points!

Controls & Mechanics

Popcorn Invasion offers intuitive controls with depth for advanced players:

Simple Tapping

Tap anywhere on the screen to pop popcorn. The game automatically detects kernels under your finger.

Motion Controls

Tilt your device to make kernels slide around - great for grouping them together!

Haptic Feedback

Feel satisfying vibrations with each pop (enable in settings if not active by default).

Multi-Touch

Use multiple fingers to pop several kernels at once for combo bonuses!

Power-Ups & Tools

Unlock and master these special tools to boost your score and become Employee of the Month!

Butter Blast

Covers the entire screen in delicious butter, making all popcorn easier to pop for 10 seconds.

Unlocks at 5,000 points

Flame Thrower

Instantly pops all popcorn in a straight line across your screen. Perfect for clustered kernels!

Unlocks at 10,000 points

Super Scoop

Gathers all popcorn to the center of the screen for massive combo opportunities.

Unlocks at 20,000 points

Double Points

Doubles all points earned for 15 seconds. Activate when you see many alien kernels!

Unlocks at 15,000 points

Time Freeze

Stops popcorn from moving for 5 seconds, giving you perfect popping conditions.

Unlocks at 25,000 points

Mega Pop

Creates a chain reaction that pops all connected popcorn kernels at once.

Unlocks at 30,000 points

Pro Tip:

Combine the Double Points power-up with an alien streak for massive scoring potential! A single alien during double points with a 5-streak bonus can be worth up to 150 points!

Advanced Tips

Take your popcorn popping to the next level with these pro strategies

1

The Butterfly Technique

Use two fingers alternately in a butterfly motion to maximize your popping speed without triggering the anti-spam system.

2

Power-Up Sequencing

Activate Butter Blast first, then Double Points, and finish with Mega Pop for the ultimate scoring combination.

3

Alien Baiting

Let regular popcorn bounce a few times - aliens often reveal themselves after 2-3 bounces for extra points.

4

Tilt Control

Gently tilt your device to group popcorn together before using the Flame Thrower for maximum efficiency.

5

Sound Cues

Listen for the distinct "alien squeak" sound that plays when disguised aliens appear on screen.

6

Strategic Misses

Intentionally miss 1-2 taps to reset your combo timer just before big point opportunities appear.
