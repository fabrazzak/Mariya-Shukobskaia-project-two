Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about Popcorn Invasion

Popcorn Invasion is available for download on the Google Play Store for Android devices running Android 5.1 or later. Simply visit our Play Store page and tap "Install." The game is free to download with optional in-app purchases.

Currently, Popcorn Invasion is only available for Android devices. We are considering an iOS version in the future. If you'd like to be notified when an iOS version becomes available, please sign up for our newsletter using the form above.

To reset your game progress:

  1. Open the Popcorn Invasion app
  2. Go to Settings (gear icon in the top right)
  3. Scroll down and tap "Reset Progress"
  4. Confirm your choice when prompted

Note: This action cannot be undone and will permanently delete all your game data, including purchased items.

Popcorn Invasion is free to play and supported by ads. You can remove all ads by purchasing the "No Ads" upgrade in the in-game store. Alternatively, you can play in airplane mode to prevent ads from loading, though some game features may be limited without an internet connection.

If you haven't received your purchased items:

  1. Restart the game - sometimes items appear after restarting
  2. Check your internet connection and make sure it's stable
  3. Wait a few minutes - Google Play purchases can sometimes take time to process
  4. If the issue persists, contact us using the form above and include your Google Play order number (found in your purchase confirmation email)

We'll investigate and make sure you get what you paid for!
