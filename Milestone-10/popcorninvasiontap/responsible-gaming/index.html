Healthy Gaming Tips

Recommendations for maintaining a balanced approach to gaming

1

Set Time Limits

Establish reasonable time limits for gameplay sessions. Consider using device timers or parental control features to help manage play time.

Suggested Limits:

  • Children under 6: 20-30 minutes per day
  • Children 6-12: 1 hour per day
  • Teens & Adults: Take regular breaks every hour
2

Balance Activities

Ensure gaming is part of a balanced lifestyle that includes physical activity, social interaction, and other hobbies.

Healthy Balance Ideas:

  • Follow the 20-20-20 rule: Every 20 minutes, look at something 20 feet away for 20 seconds
  • Engage in physical activity before/after gaming sessions
  • Schedule gaming as a reward after completing other activities
3

Monitor Spending

While Popcorn Invasion is free to play, it offers optional in-app purchases. Set clear spending limits and use device-level controls.

Spending Controls:

  • Use password protection for purchases
  • Set monthly spending budgets
  • Review purchase history regularly
4

Play Together

For younger players, consider co-playing to make gaming a shared family activity and to monitor content appropriateness.

Family Play Ideas:

  • Take turns playing and cheering each other on
  • Discuss strategies and high scores together
  • Use gaming as a reward for completing family activities
5

Watch for Signs

Be aware of potential signs of problematic gaming behavior and take appropriate action if needed.

Warning Signs:

  • Neglecting school/work or personal hygiene
  • Becoming irritable when not playing
  • Lying about time or money spent on gaming
6

Use Parental Controls

Take advantage of built-in device features to manage play time and spending.

Control Options:

  • Android Digital Wellbeing settings
  • Google Play Store purchase authentication
  • Third-party parental control apps

Parental Controls Guide

Step-by-step instructions for setting up controls on Android devices

Android Device Controls

1

Set Up Digital Wellbeing

Go to Settings > Digital Wellbeing & parental controls. Here you can:

  • Set daily app timers for Popcorn Invasion
  • Enable Bedtime mode to restrict access during certain hours
2

Restrict Purchases

In the Google Play Store app:

  • Tap Menu > Settings > Require authentication for purchases
  • Select "For all purchases through Google Play on this device"
3

Create a Restricted Profile

For shared devices, create a restricted profile:

  • Go to Settings > System > Multiple users
  • Add a restricted profile and customize app access

In-Game Settings

Sound & Vibration
