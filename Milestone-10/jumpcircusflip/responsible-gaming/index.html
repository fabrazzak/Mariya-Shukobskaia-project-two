Responsible Gaming

Enjoy Jump Circus as part of a balanced lifestyle with our healthy gaming guidelines

Why Responsible Gaming Matters

At Games Vessel, we believe gaming should be a fun and positive experience. While Jump Circus is designed to be entertaining, we recognize the importance of maintaining healthy gaming habits.

Responsible gaming means playing in moderation, being aware of the time spent gaming, and recognizing when to take breaks. It's about keeping gameplay enjoyable without letting it negatively impact other areas of life.

Healthy Gaming Tips

Simple strategies to maintain balance while enjoying Jump Circus

Set Time Limits

Use your device's built-in screen time features to set reasonable daily limits for gameplay sessions.

Take Regular Breaks

Follow the 20-20-20 rule: every 20 minutes, take a 20-second break to look at something 20 feet away.

Stay Hydrated

Keep water nearby and stay hydrated during gaming sessions to maintain focus and energy levels.

Balance Activities

Make time for other hobbies, social activities, and physical exercise alongside gaming.

Avoid Late Night Play

Limit gameplay before bedtime to ensure quality sleep and next-day productivity.

Be Mindful

Regularly check in with yourself about why and how you're playing to maintain healthy motivations.

Recognizing Warning Signs

Be aware of these potential indicators of problematic gaming habits

Time Management Issues

Gaming significantly more than intended, losing track of time regularly, or having trouble stopping gameplay when needed.

Neglecting Responsibilities

School, work, or household responsibilities being neglected or completed with less care due to gaming.

Social Withdrawal

Choosing gaming over spending time with friends and family in person or declining social invitations to play.

Mood Changes

Feeling irritable, anxious, or restless when not gaming, or only feeling good while playing.

For Parents

Guidance for managing your child's gameplay

Set Clear Rules

Establish and consistently enforce time limits for daily/weekly gameplay.

Use Parental Controls

Utilize device settings to manage play time and restrict purchases.

Play Together

Engage with your child's gaming interests and understand what they're playing.
