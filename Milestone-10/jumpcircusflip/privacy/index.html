Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2020

Com Gamesvessel Flip Jump Circus Game is an App designed for someone who wants to relax or have some fun. Your privacy is a top priority to us and we appreciate that you put your trust in us. We are committed to the responsible management, use and protection of personal information.

This Privacy Policy (the "Policy") explains how your information is collected, used, stored and shared by us with respect to your access and use of our services. By using or accessing our App or our Service, you agree to the privacy policies outlined below.

WHAT INFORMATION DO WE COLLECT?

We collect information generated as you use our services, for example access logs, as well as information from third parties, like when you access our services through a social media account. If you want additional info, we go into more detail below.

Device Information

We collect your device and network connection information for analytical purpose only when you use our App. This information includes your device brand, the model of your device, your network status, etc. The device information that we collect does not contain any user sensitive information, such as device ID or anything that can be used to permanently identify a user or device.

Information you provide to us

You choose to give us certain information when using our services. This includes but not limited to: When you create an account, you provide us with at least your login credentials, as well as some basic details necessary for the service to work, such as your gender and date of birth.

Information collected by our Monetization Partners

Some of the third-party monetization SDKs integrated in our app may access your information for the purpose of ad optimization. Such information includes GAID (Google Advertising ID on Android devices) or IDFA (Identifier for Advertising on iOS devices), region (defined as the location where a given language is used), location information and IP address. This Policy does not govern our third-party monetization partners, as they have their own privacy policies which can be viewed below. We have no access to or control over such third parties. However, we have a routine procedure to scan all third-party SDKs to detect if they collect sensitive user information.
