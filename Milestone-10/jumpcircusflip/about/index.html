About Jump Circus

Discover the story behind your favorite arcade jumping game and the team that brought it to life

The Birth of a Circus Adventure

Jump Circus began as a simple idea in 2019 - to create an arcade game that combined the thrill of precision jumping with the whimsical charm of a carnival world. Our team at Games Vessel wanted to develop something that was easy to pick up but challenging to master.

After months of prototyping and testing different mechanics, we landed on the perfect formula: intuitive tap controls, progressively challenging levels, and an adorable clown character that players would want to guide to safety.

Launched in September 2020, Jump Circus quickly became a fan favorite, praised for its addictive gameplay and vibrant visuals. We continue to update the game with new levels and features based on player feedback.
