Signs of Problem Gaming

Gaming should be fun and entertaining. If you're experiencing any of these signs, it might be time to take a break or seek help:

Spending more time or money than intended

Feeling distressed when not gaming

Lying to others about gaming habits

Borrowing money to continue gaming

Neglecting work, school, or relationships

Chasing losses or trying to win back money
