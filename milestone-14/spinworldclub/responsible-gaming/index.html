Recognizing Problematic Gaming

While gaming can be an enjoyable activity, it's important to be aware of signs that may indicate problematic behavior:

  • Spending more time or virtual currency than intended
  • Neglecting work, studies, or personal relationships due to gaming
  • Using gaming to escape from problems or negative feelings
  • Lying to others about the amount of time or resources spent gaming
  • Feeling restless or irritable when attempting to cut down on gaming

If you recognize these signs in yourself or someone you know, we encourage you to seek help from professional organizations listed below.
