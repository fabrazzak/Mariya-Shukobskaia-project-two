HDZ Calculator: A First-in-Class Feature

The Holdover Data Zero (HDZ) calculator represents a paradigm shift in how shooters establish their zeros. Traditional zeroing methods often leave shooters with suboptimal trajectories for holdover stages in competition or hunting scenarios.

How HDZ Works

The HDZ calculator analyzes your specific:

  • Rifle and ammunition combination
  • Typical engagement distances
  • Environmental conditions
  • Target sizes at various ranges

It then calculates the optimal zero distance that minimizes holdover adjustments across your entire expected engagement envelope.

During field testing with competitive PRS shooters, the HDZ calculator reduced holdover adjustments by an average of 23% across typical match stages. Hunters reported similar benefits when engaging game at varying distances in mountainous terrain.
