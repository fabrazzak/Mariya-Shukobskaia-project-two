Cookies Policy

Last Updated: February 18, 2025

1. Introduction

This Cookies Policy explains how GeoBallistics ("we", "us", or "our") uses cookies and similar tracking technologies when you visit our website at geoballistics.app or use our mobile application. By using our website or app, you consent to the use of cookies as described in this policy.

2. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device (computer, smartphone, tablet) when you visit a website or use an app. They are widely used to make websites and apps work more efficiently, as well as to provide information to the owners.

Similar technologies include web beacons, pixels, and local storage, which we may also use for the purposes described below.

3. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for the following purposes:

  • Essential Cookies: Necessary for the website to function and cannot be switched off.
  • Performance Cookies: Help us understand how visitors interact with our website.
  • Functional Cookies: Enable enhanced functionality and personalization.
  • Targeting Cookies: Used to deliver relevant ads and measure ad campaign effectiveness.

4. Types of Cookies We Use

Cookie Name Purpose Duration

5. Third-Party Cookies

We may use services from third parties that place cookies on your device:

  • Google Analytics: For analyzing website traffic and usage patterns.
  • Google Ads: For measuring the effectiveness of our advertising campaigns.
  • Facebook Pixel: For tracking conversions from Facebook ads.
  • Hotjar: For understanding user behavior through heatmaps and session recordings.

These third-party services have their own privacy policies, and we recommend you review them.

6. Managing Cookies

You can control and/or delete cookies as you wish:

  • Browser Settings: Most browsers allow you to refuse or accept cookies and to delete existing cookies.
  • Mobile Devices: Check your device settings for cookie controls specific to mobile apps.
  • Third-Party Tools: Various tools are available to help you manage cookies.

However, please note that if you disable cookies, some features of our website may not function properly.

Browser-Specific Instructions:

  • Google Chrome
  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Safari
  • Microsoft Edge

7. Local Storage

In addition to cookies, we may use local storage technologies such as:

  • LocalStorage: Stores data with no expiration date
  • SessionStorage: Stores data for one session
  • IndexedDB: For more complex client-side storage

These technologies are used to enhance performance and remember your preferences.

8. Do Not Track Signals

Our website does not currently respond to Do Not Track (DNT) signals. However, you can control tracking through your browser settings as described above.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookies Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.

We recommend reviewing this policy periodically for any changes.
