FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Find quick answers to common questions about GeoBallistics.

GeoBallistics requires Android 7.0 or later. It's optimized for most modern smartphones and tablets. The app is also compatible with Vortex Razor HD 4000 GB and Impact 4000 devices. Check the Google Play store for specific compatibility with your device.

The free version includes all core ballistics calculation games with one rifle profile. The PRO version unlocks additional games like unlimited rifle profiles, online backup, Kestrel LiNK compatibility, range card exporting, and more. The PRO version is a one-time purchase with no subscriptions.

GeoBallistics uses an advanced 3 Degree of Freedom (3DOF) solver that accounts for factors like gyroscopic drift, Coriolis effect, wind influence, and shot angle. When properly configured with accurate input data, our calculations match field results within practical shooting tolerances for most scenarios.

GeoBallistics offers free integration with WeatherFlow weather meters and compatibility with Vortex Razor HD 4000 GB and Impact 4000 devices. The PRO version adds Kestrel LiNK compatibility. We're continuously working to add support for additional hardware platforms.

With the PRO version, you can backup your rifle profiles and range cards online and restore them on any device. In the free version, you'll need to manually recreate your profiles. We recommend exporting your data as CSV files (PRO feature) for manual transfer if needed.
