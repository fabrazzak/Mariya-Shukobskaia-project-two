Our Story

Discover the passion behind Mighty Ape Gifts - New Zealand's favorite destination for unique gifts and novelties.

Our Mission

At Mighty Ape Gifts, we believe that the perfect gift can create unforgettable moments. Our mission is to help you discover unique, high-quality products that bring joy to both the giver and the receiver.

Founded in 2010, we've grown from a small boutique to New Zealand's premier destination for creative gifts and novelties. Every item in our collection is carefully curated to ensure it meets our standards of quality, originality, and craftsmanship.

"We don't just sell gifts - we help create memories that last a lifetime."

Our Journey

Founded in Rotorua

2010

Sarah Johnson opens the first Mighty Ape Gifts store in Rotorua with a small but carefully curated selection of unique gifts.

Online Store Launch

2012

We launch our e-commerce platform, allowing customers across New Zealand to enjoy our unique products with nationwide delivery.

Auckland Store Opens

2015

Our second physical location opens in Auckland, expanding our reach and allowing more customers to experience our products in person.

Jungle Express Delivery

2018

We introduce our same-day delivery service in major cities, ensuring customers get their gifts when they need them.
