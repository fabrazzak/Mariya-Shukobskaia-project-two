Cookies Policy

Last Updated: May 15, 2025

This Cookies Policy explains how Sumato Coffee Deals ("we," "us," or "our") uses cookies and similar tracking technologies when you visit our website sumatocoffeedeals.com. By using our website, you consent to the use of cookies as described in this policy.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device (computer, smartphone, tablet) when you visit a website. They help the website remember information about your visit, which can make it easier to visit the site again and make the site more useful to you.

2. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for several purposes:

  • Essential Cookies: Necessary for the website to function properly
  • Performance Cookies: Help us understand how visitors interact with our website
  • Functionality Cookies: Remember your preferences and choices
  • Targeting/Advertising Cookies: Used to deliver relevant ads and measure ad performance

3. Types of Cookies We Use

The table below explains the cookies we use and why:

Cookie Name Purpose Duration Type
session_id Maintains your session when logged in Session Essential
cart_token Remembers items in your shopping cart 7 days Essential
_ga Google Analytics - distinguishes users 2 years Performance
_gid Google Analytics - distinguishes users 24 hours Performance
_gat Google Analytics - throttles request rate 1 minute Performance

fbp Facebook Pixel - tracks conversions from Facebook ads 3 months Targeting

4. Third-Party Cookies

We may also use various third-party cookies including:

  • Google Analytics: To understand how visitors use our site
  • Facebook Pixel: To measure ad performance and deliver targeted ads
  • Hotjar: To analyze user behavior and improve user experience
  • Stripe: For payment processing functionality

These third-party services may set their own cookies when you visit our site. We don't control these cookies and you should check the respective third-party's cookie policy for more information.

5. Similar Tracking Technologies

In addition to cookies, we may use other tracking technologies:

  • Web Beacons: Small graphic images used in emails to track opens
  • Local Storage: Stores data in your browser for functionality
  • Session Storage: Temporary storage during your browsing session

6. Managing Cookies

You can control and/or delete cookies as you wish. Most web browsers allow some control of cookies through browser settings. Here's how you can manage cookies in popular browsers:

6.1 Browser Settings

  • Google Chrome
  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Apple Safari
  • Microsoft Edge

6.2 Cookie Consent Tool

When you first visit our website, you'll see our cookie consent banner where you can choose which types of cookies to accept. You can change these preferences at any time by clicking the "Cookie Settings" link in our website footer.

6.3 Opting Out of Analytics

To opt out of Google Analytics tracking across all websites, you can install the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

7. What Happens If You Disable Cookies?

If you disable cookies:

  • Some website features may not function properly
  • Your shopping cart may not remember items between visits
  • You may need to re-enter preferences each time you visit
  • Some personalized content may not be available

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookies Policy from time to time. The "Last Updated" date at the top of this page indicates when changes were made. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
