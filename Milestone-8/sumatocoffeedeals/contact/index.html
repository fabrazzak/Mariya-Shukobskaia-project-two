Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about Sumato Coffee

We roast and ship orders within 1-2 business days. Domestic orders typically arrive within 3-5 business days after shipping. We offer free shipping on orders over $50 and flat-rate shipping for smaller orders. International shipping is available with rates calculated at checkout.

You can manage your subscription anytime by logging into your account on our website. From there you can change frequency, skip a shipment, update payment methods, or cancel entirely. Changes must be made at least 3 days before your next roast date to take effect for that shipment.

We stand behind our coffee 100%. If you're not completely satisfied with your order, contact us within 30 days of purchase for a refund or replacement. Due to the perishable nature of coffee, we can't accept returns of opened products, but we'll work with you to make it right.

For maximum freshness, store coffee in an airtight container at room temperature, away from light, heat, and moisture. Avoid refrigeration as it can cause condensation. Whole bean coffee stays fresh for about a month after roasting if stored properly, while ground coffee is best used within 2 weeks.
