Our Story

Founded in Zoetermeer, Netherlands, Outsiders Store is dedicated to providing high-quality outdoor footwear for adventurers and urban explorers alike.

We carefully curate our collection to bring you the best in comfort, durability, and style. Our partnership with KEEN ensures that every product meets the highest standards of quality and performance.

Visit our store at Argonstraat 11, 2718 SM Zoetermeer to experience our products in person and get expert advice from our team.
