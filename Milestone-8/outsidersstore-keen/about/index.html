Our Story

Born from a passion for adventure and quality footwear

Welcome to Outsiders Store

Founded in 2015 in Zoetermeer, Netherlands, Outsiders Store began as a small shop with a big vision: to provide premium outdoor footwear for adventurers and urban explorers alike. What started as a passion project has grown into a destination for those who demand quality, comfort, and style from their footwear.

From Local Shop to Community Hub

Our journey began at Argonstraat 11, where we first opened our doors with a carefully curated selection of KEEN footwear. Word quickly spread about our knowledgeable staff and exceptional products, transforming our small store into a gathering place for outdoor enthusiasts.

Today, while we've expanded our offerings and reach, we remain committed to the personal service and community focus that defined our early days. We're proud to be part of Zoetermeer's growing outdoor culture.

Our Partnership with KEEN

We chose to specialize in KEEN footwear because their values align perfectly with ours. KEEN's commitment to quality, comfort, and sustainability makes them the ideal partner for our mission.

From their innovative waterproof technologies to their environmentally conscious manufacturing processes, KEEN products represent the best in outdoor footwear - exactly what we want to offer our customers.
