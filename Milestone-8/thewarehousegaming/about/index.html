OUR JOURNEY

Our Story

From humble beginnings to New Zealand's favorite gaming destination

Our Mission

At The Warehouse Gaming, our mission is to bring the joy of gaming to everyone in New Zealand. We believe gaming is more than just entertainment - it's a way to connect, learn, and grow.

We're committed to providing the latest gaming technology at competitive prices, backed by expert advice and exceptional customer service.

Our Vision

To be New Zealand's most trusted gaming retailer, known for our passion, expertise, and dedication to the gaming community.

We aim to create a space where gamers of all levels can find what they need, discover new favorites, and be part of a thriving gaming culture.

2010 - Humble Beginnings

Founded in a small store in Te Puke by gaming enthusiasts Liam and Sarah, The Warehouse Gaming started as a passion project to share their love of gaming with the local community.

2013 - First Expansion

After gaining popularity for our knowledgeable staff and curated selection, we opened our second location in Auckland, marking the beginning of our nationwide expansion.

2016 - Online Store Launch

Recognizing the need to serve gamers across New Zealand, we launched our e-commerce platform, offering nationwide shipping and competitive online prices.

2019 - Community Hub

Our flagship store in Te Puke was renovated to include a gaming lounge and event space, hosting tournaments, launch parties, and community gatherings.

2022 - Next-Gen Focus

We became New Zealand's top retailer for PS5 and Xbox Series X consoles, with record-breaking sales and customer satisfaction ratings.

2025 - The Future

Today, we continue to grow, innovate, and serve the New Zealand gaming community with 5 physical locations and our thriving online store, always staying true to our gaming roots.
