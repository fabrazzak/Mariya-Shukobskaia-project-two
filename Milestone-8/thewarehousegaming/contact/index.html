Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our products, services, and policies.

What are your shipping options and delivery times?

We offer standard shipping (3-5 business days) for $5.99, express shipping (1-2 business days) for $12.99, and free shipping on orders over $100. Delivery times may vary during peak periods or for remote locations.

What is your return policy for gaming consoles?

Gaming consoles can be returned within 30 days of purchase with original packaging and receipt. The product must be in like-new condition with no damage or signs of heavy use. Refunds will be issued to the original payment method.

Do you offer price matching for products?

Yes, we price match identical products from authorized New Zealand retailers. The item must be in stock at the competitor at the time of your request. Simply provide us with the competitor's details and we'll verify the price match.

Can I pre-order upcoming game releases?

Absolutely! We offer pre-orders for all major game releases. Pre-orders typically require a $20 deposit, with the balance due upon release. Pre-order customers often receive exclusive bonuses like in-game content or collectible items.

Do you repair gaming consoles and accessories?

Yes, our in-store technicians can repair most gaming consoles and accessories. We service products under warranty and also offer paid repairs for out-of-warranty items. Contact us for a diagnostic assessment and repair estimate.
