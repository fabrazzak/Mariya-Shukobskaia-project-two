Blackjack Basics

  • Goal: Beat dealer without exceeding 21
  • Cards 2-10 = face value
  • Face cards = 10, Ace = 1 or 11
  • Blackjack = Ace + 10-value card

Blackjack Guide

Blackjack is one of the most popular casino card games where players compete against the dealer rather than each other. The objective is to have a hand value closer to 21 than the dealer's without going over.

How to Play Blackjack

1

Place your bet - Select your chip value and place it in the betting circle before cards are dealt.

2

Receive your cards - You and the dealer each get two cards. One dealer card is face up (the "up card").

3

Make your play decisions - Choose to Hit (take another card), Stand (keep current hand), Double Down (double bet for one card), or Split (if you have two of the same card).

4

Dealer plays - After all players act, the dealer reveals their hole card and must hit on 16 or less, stand on 17 or more.

5

Determine winner - If your hand is closer to 21 than dealer's without busting, you win 1:1. Blackjack pays 3:2.

Blackjack Strategy Tips

  • Always stand on hard 17 or higher
  • Hit on hard 11 or lower
  • Double down on 11 unless dealer shows an Ace
  • Never take insurance - it's a bad bet statistically
  • Split Aces and 8s, never split 10s or 5s

Texas Hold'em Basics

  • Each player gets 2 private cards
  • 5 community cards dealt face-up
  • Best 5-card hand wins the pot
  • Blinds force betting action

Texas Hold'em Poker Guide

Texas Hold'em is the most popular poker variant worldwide. In Galaxy Casino, you can join 5-player tables and compete against others in this exciting game of skill and strategy.

How to Play Texas Hold'em

1

Post blinds - The two players to the left of the dealer post small and big blinds to start the pot.

2

Receive hole cards - Each player is dealt two private cards (hole cards) face down.

3

First betting round - Players can call, raise, or fold starting with the player to the left of the big blind.

4

The Flop - Three community cards are dealt face up in the middle of the table.

5

Second betting round - Another round of betting occurs with the same options.

6

The Turn - A fourth community card is dealt face up.

7

Third betting round - Another round of betting occurs.

8

The River - The fifth and final community card is dealt face up.

9

Final betting round - The last chance to bet before the showdown.

10

Showdown - Remaining players reveal their hands. The best 5-card combination using any combination of hole cards and community cards wins the pot.

Texas Hold'em Strategy Tips

  • Play tight-aggressive: few hands but bet/raise when you do play
  • Position is power - play more hands in late position
  • Fold weak hands pre-flop to preserve your chips
  • Pay attention to opponents' betting patterns
  • Bluff selectively and with purpose

Roulette Guide

Roulette is a classic casino game of chance where players bet on where a ball will land on a spinning wheel. Galaxy Casino features American roulette with numbers 1-36 plus 0 and 00.

How to Play Roulette

1

Place your bets - Select your chip value and place bets on the numbered layout before the wheel spins.

2

Spin the wheel - The dealer (or virtual dealer) spins the wheel in one direction and rolls the ball in the opposite direction.

3

Wait for the outcome - The ball eventually loses momentum and falls into one of the numbered pockets.

4

Payout winning bets - The dealer announces the winning number and color, then pays all winning bets according to the payout odds.

Roulette Bet Types

Inside Bets
  • Straight - Single number (35:1)
  • Split - Two adjacent numbers (17:1)
  • Street - Three numbers in a row (11:1)
  • Corner - Four-number square (8:1)
  • Line - Six-number block (5:1)
Outside Bets
  • Red/Black - Color bet (1:1)
  • Odd/Even - Number type (1:1)
  • 1-18/19-36 - Low/high numbers (1:1)
  • Dozens - 1-12, 13-24, 25-36 (2:1)
  • Columns - Vertical column (2:1)

Roulette Strategy Tips

  • Outside bets have better odds but lower payouts
  • Mix inside and outside bets to balance risk/reward
  • No betting system can overcome the house edge long-term
  • Set a budget and stick to it - roulette is pure chance
  • Have fun with neighbor bets for exciting coverage

Baccarat Basics

  • Player vs Banker - bet on which will win
  • Cards 2-9 = face value, 10/J/Q/K = 0, Ace = 1
  • Hand value is sum modulo 10 (e.g. 7+6=13→3)
  • Natural win with 8 or 9

Baccarat Guide

Baccarat is an elegant card game where players bet on whether the Player or Banker hand will be closer to 9. It's a simple game with relatively good odds for players.

How to Play Baccarat

1

Place your bet - Choose to bet on Player, Banker, or Tie before cards are dealt.

2

Initial deal - Two cards each to Player and Banker positions.

3

Check for naturals - If either hand totals 8 or 9 (natural), that hand wins.

4

Player draws third card - If Player's total is 0-5, they draw a third card.

5

Banker draws third card - Banker may draw based on complex rules depending on Player's third card.

6

Determine winner - The hand closest to 9 wins. Banker pays 0.95:1 (5% commission), Player pays 1:1, Tie pays 8:1.

Baccarat Strategy Tips

  • Banker bet has slightly better odds (1.06% house edge)
  • Avoid Tie bets despite high payout (14.4% house edge)
  • No strategy affects outcome - cards follow fixed rules
  • Track results but remember each hand is independent
  • Set win/loss limits before playing
