Our Commitment to Responsible Gaming

Fantasy Springs Slots is designed for entertainment purposes only. We are committed to ensuring our games remain a fun and safe experience for adult players. While our games simulate casino-style gaming, they do not offer real money gambling or the opportunity to win real money or prizes.

Tips for Responsible Play

Set Time Limits

Decide in advance how much time you want to spend playing and stick to it. Take regular breaks to maintain perspective.

Set Spending Limits

If you choose to make in-app purchases, set a budget and never exceed what you can comfortably afford to spend.

Play for Fun

Remember that our games are for entertainment only. Never play to recover losses or solve financial problems.

Balance Activities

Make sure gaming doesn't replace other social activities, exercise, or time with family and friends.

Warning Signs of Problem Gaming

While most people play our games responsibly, for some it can become problematic. Be aware of these warning signs:

  • Spending more time or money on gaming than you intended
  • Neglecting work, school, or family responsibilities due to gaming
  • Borrowing money or selling possessions to fund gaming
  • Feeling restless or irritable when trying to cut down on gaming
  • Lying to family or friends about time or money spent gaming

If you recognize several of these signs in yourself or someone you care about, it may indicate a problem with gaming. We encourage you to seek help from professional resources.
