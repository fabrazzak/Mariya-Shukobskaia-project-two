Healthy Gaming Tips

To maintain a healthy relationship with gaming, we recommend following these guidelines:

Set Time Limits

Decide in advance how much time you want to spend playing and stick to it. Take regular breaks to maintain balance with other activities.

Set Spending Limits

If you choose to make in-app purchases, set a budget and never spend more than you can afford. Remember all purchases are for virtual items only.

Play for Fun

Remember that our games are meant for entertainment purposes only. The outcomes are based on random number generation.

Maintain Balance

Ensure gaming remains one of many leisure activities. Don't let it interfere with work, study, or personal relationships.

Warning Signs

Be aware of these signs that may indicate gaming is becoming problematic:

Spending more time or money on gaming than intended

Neglecting work, studies, or personal relationships due to gaming

Using gaming to escape from problems or negative feelings

Borrowing money or selling possessions to fund gaming

Feeling restless or irritable when trying to cut down on gaming

Lying to family or friends about gaming habits

If you recognize these signs in yourself or someone you know, consider seeking help from professional organizations that specialize in gaming-related issues.
