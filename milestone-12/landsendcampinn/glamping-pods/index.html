Our Luxury Glamping Pods

We offer seven luxury glamping pods, each designed and finished to the highest standards. These fully self-contained retreats combine the adventure of camping with the comfort of a boutique hotel.

Because of our unique setting at Land's End - the first and last campsite in the country - we're lucky enough to witness some of the most spectacular sunsets. With this in mind, we've designed each pod with a rooftop sunset deck where you can relax and soak in the views.

Pod Features

Designed for comfort in all seasons with everything you need for a perfect stay.

Fully Self-Contained

Each pod includes a kitchen area with microwave, kettle, toaster, two-ring hob, fridge with small freezer compartment, crockery, glasses, cutlery and basic cooking utensils.

Comfortable Sleeping

Our three-sleeper pods accommodate two adults and one child, while our four-sleeper pods accommodate two adults and two children. All bedding is provided.

Rooftop Sunset Deck

Each pod features a private rooftop deck with 4-seat lounging set - the perfect spot to enjoy Cornwall's spectacular sunsets with a glass of wine.

Year-Round Comfort

Highly insulated construction with wood-burning stove keeps you cozy in winter. A starter pack of fuel is supplied, with more available to purchase on site.

Sheltered Deck

In addition to the rooftop deck, each pod has a covered front deck with bistro table and chairs - perfect for al fresco dining whatever the weather.

Chimenea

Each pod comes with its own chimenea to help lengthen those summer evenings. Firewood is available to purchase on site (only our firewood can be used).

Additional Information

Peak Seasons

High season (1st July - 1st September) and bank holiday weekends have a minimum 3-night stay requirement.

Children

We classify children as 15 years or under. Each additional child beyond the pod capacity is £10 per night.

Pets

Well-behaved dogs are welcome in selected pods for £15 per stay (max 1 dog per pod). Please request when booking.

Firewood

Starter pack included. Additional firewood bundles available for £6 each (only our firewood can be used).
