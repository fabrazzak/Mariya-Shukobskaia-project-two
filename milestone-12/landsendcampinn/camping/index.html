Authentic Camping Experience

Our large, flat camping field offers distant sea views from most pitches. You'll be blessed with amazing sunsets and sunrises throughout much of the year, with minimal light pollution to obscure the starry skies above.

We've designed our campsite to remain as natural as possible, without roads or hardstandings on our fields. Despite this, our well-drained ground makes for year-round camping suitability - we've been pitching motorhomes year-round since opening without any issues.

Camping Features

Everything you need for a comfortable and memorable camping experience.

Handmade Burners

We lend out handmade burners free of charge and sell firewood, kindling, and starters on site. Open fires create a wonderful atmosphere that enhances the camping experience.

Electric Hookups

We offer 20 pitches with electric hookup available. Perfect for those who want to combine traditional camping with a few home comforts.

Facilities

Chemical toilet disposal for cassette WCs, drive-over grey water disposal, and water fill points for motorhomes. Our modern shower block is just a short walk away.

Free Super-Fast WiFi

Stay connected with our complimentary high-speed internet available throughout the campsite, perfect for sharing those stunning sunset photos.

Dog Friendly

Well-behaved dogs are welcome on leads. Enjoy coastal walks right from the campsite and discover dog-friendly beaches nearby.

Group Bookings

We welcome larger bookings and club events like cycling, climbing, and hiking groups. Contact us to discuss your requirements.
