Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your personal information

Introduction

At Land's End Camping & Glamping, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our website or services.

We comply with the UK Data Protection Act 2018 and the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). By using our services, you consent to the data practices described in this policy.

Key Principles:

  • We only collect necessary personal data
  • We never sell your personal information
  • We implement strong security measures
  • You have rights over your data

Data We Collect

We may collect the following types of personal information when you interact with our website or make a booking:

Data Type Purpose Legal Basis
Name, address, contact details Booking processing, communication Contractual necessity
Payment information Transaction processing Contractual necessity
Booking details (dates, party size) Service provision Contractual necessity
Email address Marketing (with consent) Consent
Website usage data Analytics, improvements Legitimate interest
Special requests (accessibility, etc.) Service personalization Explicit consent

We may also collect non-personal data such as browser type, operating system, and website usage statistics to improve our services.

How We Use Your Data

Your personal data may be used for the following purposes:

  • Booking Processing: To manage your reservation, process payments, and provide confirmation details.
  • Customer Service: To respond to inquiries, requests, or complaints.
  • Improvements: To analyze website usage and improve our services.
  • Marketing: To send promotional offers (only with your explicit consent).
  • Legal Compliance: To meet our legal obligations (e.g., tax records).
  • Security: To detect and prevent fraud or other illegal activities.

Data Retention:

We retain personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected, including legal, accounting, or reporting requirements. Booking information is typically retained for 7 years for tax purposes.

Data Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your data with:

  • Service Providers: Payment processors, booking system providers, and IT service providers who assist in delivering our services.
  • Legal Authorities: When required by law or to protect our legal rights.
  • Business Transfers: In connection with any merger, sale of company assets, or acquisition.

All third parties are carefully vetted and required to maintain appropriate security standards. We only share the minimum necessary information.

International Transfers:

Some service providers may be located outside the UK/EEA. In such cases, we ensure appropriate safeguards are in place, such as Standard Contractual Clauses approved by the European Commission.

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

Technical Measures

  • SSL/TLS encryption
  • Secure payment processing
  • Regular security updates
  • Access controls

Organizational Measures

  • Staff training on data protection
  • Confidentiality agreements
  • Data minimization practices
  • Incident response plans

Despite our safeguards, no internet transmission is completely secure. We cannot guarantee the security of your data transmitted to our site; any transmission is at your own risk.

Your Rights

Under data protection laws, you have rights regarding your personal data:

Access & Information

  • Right to access your personal data
  • Right to know how we use your data
  • Right to request a copy of your data

Control & Correction

  • Right to correct inaccurate data
  • Right to request deletion of data
  • Right to restrict processing

Objections & Complaints

  • Right to object to processing
  • Right to withdraw consent
  • Right to complain to the ICO

Data Portability

  • Right to receive your data in a portable format
  • Right to have data transferred to another provider

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at info@landsendcampinn.com. We may need to verify your identity before processing your request.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify you of significant changes by posting the new policy on our website with an updated revision date.

We encourage you to periodically review this page for the latest information on our privacy practices. Your continued use of our services after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Last Updated:
