Frequently Asked Questions

Check-in is from 3pm to 7pm daily. Check-out is by 11am on your departure day. Early check-in or late check-out may be available by prior arrangement.

Yes, we welcome well-behaved dogs. They must be kept on leads at all times due to our free-range chickens and other animals. Please clean up after your dog.

Yes, we offer free super-fast broadband WiFi across the campsite. The network name and password will be provided on arrival.

We have a communal campfire area and provide chimineas for individual pitches. Only firewood purchased on site may be used. During very dry periods, fires may be restricted for safety.

For cancellations made more than 14 days before arrival, we offer a full refund minus a £10 administration fee. Cancellations within 14 days are non-refundable. We recommend travel insurance.
