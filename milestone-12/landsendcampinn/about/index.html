Welcome to Our Family Campsite

Land's End Camping & Glamping was born from our love of Cornwall's breathtaking landscapes and our desire to share this special place with others. What began as a small family project has grown into a beloved destination for campers seeking an authentic Cornish experience.

Located on Cornwall's most westerly point, we're proud to be the first and last campsite in the country. Our location offers the perfect balance - close enough to enjoy all the attractions of Land's End, yet peaceful enough to feel truly immersed in nature.

"We believe in creating experiences, not just accommodations. Our goal is to help you make memories that will last a lifetime against the stunning backdrop of Cornwall's coastline."

Mark & Tina

Owners & Founders

Our Journey

From humble beginnings to becoming one of Cornwall's most loved camping destinations.

The Dream Begins

2005

Mark and Tina fell in love with this plot of land during a coastal walk. Despite its exposed location, they saw potential for a campsite that would showcase Cornwall's raw beauty.

First Campers Arrive

2008

With just 10 basic pitches and a single shower block, we welcomed our first guests. Word quickly spread about our incredible sunset views and proximity to Land's End.

Expansion & Facilities

2012

We expanded to 50 pitches and built our modern shower block. Added electric hookups and a communal campfire area, becoming a year-round destination.

Glamping Revolution

2016

Introduced our first luxury glamping pods with rooftop sunset decks, combining comfort with our stunning natural setting. Quickly became our most popular option.

Today & Beyond

Present

Now with 7 luxury pods, the Showman's Wagon, and 50+ pitches, we continue to innovate while staying true to our roots. Our focus remains on sustainable tourism and creating unforgettable experiences.

Our Core Values

The principles that guide everything we do at Land's End Camping & Glamping.

Passion for Cornwall

We're proud to call Cornwall home and are committed to sharing its beauty responsibly. Our team are experts on local history, wildlife, and hidden gems.

Sustainable Tourism

From solar panels to wildlife-friendly practices, we minimize our environmental impact while maximizing your connection to nature.

Family Atmosphere

We treat every guest as part of our extended family. Our welcoming, laid-back approach creates the perfect environment for making memories.
