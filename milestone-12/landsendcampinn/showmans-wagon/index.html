Nelly - The Showman's Wagon

Step back in time with our beautifully restored 1920s wagon - a unique blend of historic charm and modern comfort.

Home / Showman's Wagon

A Unique Historic Experience

Our newly refurbished Showman's Wagon, affectionately named "Nelly", offers a glamping experience like no other. Originally built in Scotland in the 1920s, this historic wagon spent its first hundred years traveling with fairs and carnivals around the country before finding its forever home at Land's End.

Now beautifully restored to a very high standard, Nelly combines vintage charm with modern comforts to create a truly memorable stay. The interior is cozy and comfortable, featuring a wood burning stove that's perfect after walks on the coast path or strolls on the beautiful beaches in the area.

Quick Facts

  • Sleeps up to 2 adults and 2 children
  • Cozy wood burning stove (starter pack included)
  • Kitchen area with basic cooking facilities
  • Private pitch with fire pit and picnic table
  • Access to our excellent shower and toilet facilities (100m away)
