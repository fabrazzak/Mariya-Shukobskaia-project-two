Welcome to Land's End Camping & Glamping

Nestled on Cornwall's most westerly point, our family-run campsite offers a unique blend of traditional camping and luxury glamping experiences, just a stone's throw from the dramatic cliffs and breathtaking scenery of Land's End.

As the first and last campsite in the country, we're perfectly positioned for you to enjoy both the attractions of Land's End and the peace of the Cornish countryside. Whether you're a family looking for adventure, a couple seeking romance, or a solo traveler chasing stunning sunsets, we have the perfect spot for you.

Why Choose Land's End?

We combine stunning natural beauty with excellent facilities to create unforgettable camping experiences.

Prime Location

Just 10 minutes from Land's End and 3 minutes from Sennen Cove beach. Perfect base to explore Cornwall's attractions.

Modern Facilities

Newly built shower block, dish washing area, charging points, and communal campfire space for social evenings.

Free Super-Fast WiFi

Stay connected with our complimentary high-speed internet available throughout the campsite.

Pet Friendly

We welcome well-behaved dogs. Enjoy walks along coastal paths right from our campsite.
