Cookies Policy

Last updated: July 25, 2025

1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently, as well as to provide information to the website owners.

Like most online services, Canada League uses cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience, analyze usage, and improve our services.

2. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for several purposes:

  • Essential Cookies: Necessary for the website to function properly (e.g., maintaining your login session)
  • Performance Cookies: Help us understand how visitors interact with our website (e.g., which pages are most popular)
  • Functionality Cookies: Remember your preferences to provide enhanced features
  • Targeting/Advertising Cookies: Used to deliver relevant ads to you (we don't currently use these but may in the future)

We do not use cookies to collect personally identifiable information without your permission.

3. Types of Cookies We Use

The specific cookies we use include:

Cookie Name Purpose Duration
session_id Maintains your logged-in state Session
user_prefs Stores your UI preferences 30 days
analytics_id Anonymous usage tracking 1 year
cookie_consent Remembers your cookie preferences 1 year

4. Third-Party Cookies

We may use services from third parties that place their own cookies on your device. These include:

  • Google Analytics: Helps us understand how users interact with our site
  • Stripe: For payment processing (only when making purchases)
  • Hotjar: For user experience analysis (optional)

These third-party services have their own privacy policies and cookie policies, which we recommend you review.

5. Managing Cookies

You can control and/or delete cookies as you wish. Most web browsers allow some control of cookies through browser settings. You can:

  • Delete all cookies from your device
  • Block all cookies from being set
  • Set preferences for certain websites

To manage cookies in your browser:

  • Google Chrome
  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Safari
  • Microsoft Edge

Please note that if you disable cookies entirely, some features of our website may not function properly.

6. Your Consent

When you first visit our website, we show a cookie consent banner where you can:

  • Accept all cookies
  • Decline non-essential cookies
  • Customize your preferences

Your consent applies to the following domains: canadaleague.com and all subdomains.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookies Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.

We encourage you to periodically review this page for the latest information on our cookie practices.
