Redefining Space with Hidden Doors

At Murphy Door Ogden, we believe that every space holds untapped potential. Our mission is to help homeowners and designers unlock that potential through innovative hidden door solutions that combine functionality with aesthetic beauty.

Our Journey

From humble beginnings to industry leaders in hidden door solutions.

Founded in Sydney

2010

What began as a small workshop crafting custom furniture evolved into a passion for space-saving solutions when founder James Murphy designed his first hidden bookcase door for a client's cramped apartment.

First Commercial Success

2013

Our signature bookcase door was featured in a popular home design magazine, leading to a surge in demand and the establishment of our first dedicated showroom in Darlinghurst.

Product Line Expansion

2016

Introduced our first mirror doors and cabinet solutions, responding to customer demand for more diverse hidden door options. Launched our e-commerce platform to serve customers nationwide.

Today & Beyond

Present

With over 5,000 hidden doors installed across Australia and a growing international presence, we continue to innovate with new materials, mechanisms, and designs while maintaining our commitment to craftsmanship.
