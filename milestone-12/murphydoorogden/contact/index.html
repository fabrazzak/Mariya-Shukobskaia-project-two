Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about our products and services.

The production time for custom hidden doors varies based on the complexity of the design and our current order volume. Typically, custom doors take between 4-6 weeks from the date of order confirmation to shipment. We'll provide you with an estimated timeline during your consultation.

Yes, we offer professional installation services for all our hidden door solutions in the Sydney metropolitan area. Our experienced installers will ensure your door is perfectly fitted and functioning smoothly. Installation typically takes 1-2 days depending on the complexity of the project.

We accept returns of standard, non-custom products within 14 days of delivery, provided the items are in their original condition and packaging. Custom-made doors cannot be returned unless there is a manufacturing defect. Please review our full for complete details.

In some cases, existing doors can be modified to function as hidden doors, depending on the structure of your wall and the door's current configuration. We offer consultation services to assess whether your existing door is suitable for conversion or if a new custom solution would be more appropriate.

Yes, we ship our hidden door solutions worldwide. International shipping costs and delivery times vary by destination. Please contact our sales team for a shipping quote to your location. Note that some larger custom pieces may have shipping restrictions to certain countries.
