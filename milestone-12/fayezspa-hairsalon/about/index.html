Our Story of Excellence

35 years of transforming hair and building confidence in Spruce Grove and beyond.

35 Years of Hair Artistry

1989 - Humble Beginnings

Founded by award-winning stylist Sarah Fayez, our salon started as a small studio with just two chairs. From day one, we focused on precision cuts and personalized service.

1995 - First Expansion

After winning multiple regional hairstyling awards, we expanded to accommodate growing demand, adding four new stations and our first spa services.

2008 - Bumble and Bumble Partnership

We became an exclusive Bumble and Bumble salon, sending our stylists to New York City for training at the famous Bumble University.

Today - Continuing the Legacy

Now with a team of 12 master stylists, we continue Sarah's tradition of excellence while embracing the latest trends and techniques in hair care.
