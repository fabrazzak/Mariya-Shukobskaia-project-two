Summer Hair Trends 2025: This Season's Hottest Looks

As the temperature rises, it's time to refresh your look with the hottest summer hair trends of 2025. At Fayez Spa, our Bumble and Bumble trained stylists have been busy mastering these runway-inspired looks that combine effortless beauty with modern sophistication. Whether you're dreaming of sun-kissed balayage or bold statement colors, we've got the inside scoop on this season's must-have styles.

The Top 5 Summer Hair Trends for 2025

This summer is all about embracing natural texture while adding playful touches of color and dimension. After analyzing the latest fashion shows and consulting with our color experts, we've identified the top five trends that will dominate this season:

1

Sun-Kissed Balayage 2.0

The next evolution of balayage with warmer, more dimensional tones that mimic natural sun exposure.

2

Effortless Beach Waves

Lived-in waves with a modern twist - less crunchy, more natural movement and texture.

3

Bold Money Pieces

Face-framing highlights in unexpected colors like peach, lavender, or electric blue.

4

The Modern Shag

Updated 70s-inspired shag cuts with softer layers and more wearable shapes.

1. Sun-Kissed Balayage 2.0

Balayage continues to reign supreme, but for summer 2025, we're seeing a shift toward warmer, more multidimensional tones. Our stylists are calling this "Balayage 2.0" - a technique that creates even more natural-looking results by incorporating up to four different shades.

What's Different in 2025?

  • Warmer tones: Think honey, caramel, and toasted coconut instead of ashy blondes
  • More dimension: Incorporating 3-4 tonal variations for depth
  • Lower placement: Starting further down the hair for a more natural grow-out
  • Face-framing focus: Brighter pieces around the face for a luminous effect

"This technique is perfect for clients who want that 'just back from vacation' look all year round," says Adam, our lead colorist. "The multiple tonal variations create incredible movement and shine."

2. Effortless Beach Waves

The beach wave is getting a major upgrade this summer. Gone are the days of stiff, uniform curls. The 2025 version is all about natural-looking, undone waves with plenty of texture and movement.

How to Achieve the Look

Our stylists recommend these steps for perfect beach waves:

  1. Start with texturizing spray on damp hair
  2. Use a 1.25" curling iron, alternating curl directions
  3. Leave the ends out for a more relaxed feel
  4. Once cooled, run fingers through to break up the curls
  5. Finish with a light-hold sea salt spray

Pro Tip from Our Stylists

For second-day waves, simply refresh with a mixture of water and conditioner in a spray bottle, scrunch, and let air dry.

3. Bold Money Pieces

The money piece highlight gets a colorful twist this season. While the classic blonde face-framing pieces remain popular, we're seeing more clients opt for unexpected pops of color in these strategic placements.

Popular Color Choices

Peach Fuzz
Lavender
Electric Blue
Chartreuse

"The beauty of this trend is its versatility," explains Mo, our creative director. "You can go subtle with a pastel peach or make a bold statement with vibrant blue. Either way, it's an easy way to experiment with color without full commitment."

4. The Modern Shag

The shag haircut makes a triumphant return this summer, but with contemporary updates that make it more wearable for everyday life. The 2025 version features softer layers, more blending, and customizable lengths.

Who Does the Shag Work For?

Contrary to popular belief, the modern shag can be adapted for nearly all hair types and face shapes:

  • Fine hair: Adds volume and movement
  • Thick hair: Removes bulk while maintaining shape
  • Curly hair: Enhances natural curl pattern
  • Straight hair: Creates texture and dimension

5. Glass Hair

The ultimate summer shine trend, "glass hair" refers to ultra-sleek, glossy styles that resemble polished glass. This look is all about healthy hair and high-shine finishes.

How to Achieve Glass Hair

Our stylists recommend these professional techniques:

  1. Start with our Silk Touch Smoothing Treatment for frizz control
  2. Use a boar bristle brush when blow-drying
  3. Apply a shine serum before flat ironing
  4. Finish with a light mist of hairspray for hold

Summer Hair Care Essentials

Protect your color and keep your hair healthy all summer with these must-have products:

  • UV-protectant leave-in conditioner
  • Chlorine-removing shampoo
  • Anti-humidity smoothing cream
  • Sea salt texturizing spray
