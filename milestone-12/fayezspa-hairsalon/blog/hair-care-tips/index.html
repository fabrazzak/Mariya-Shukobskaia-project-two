5 Essential Hair Care Tips From Our Stylists

June 15, 2025 â€¢ 6 min read

At Fayez Spa, we believe beautiful hair starts with proper care. While regular salon visits are important, what you do at home between appointments makes all the difference. We've asked our team of Bumble and Bumble trained stylists to share their top professional tips for maintaining healthy, gorgeous hair. These five essential practices will help you extend the life of your style and keep your hair looking its best.

1. Wash Your Hair Less Frequently

One of the most common mistakes people make is overwashing their hair. Our lead stylist Adam explains: "Shampooing too often strips your hair of its natural oils, leading to dryness, breakage, and even increased oil production as your scalp tries to compensate."

Transitioning to Less Frequent Washing

If you're used to daily washing, transition gradually:

  • Start by adding an extra day between washes
  • Use dry shampoo on non-wash days
  • Try hairstyles that disguise oiliness (braids, buns)
  • Be patient - it takes 2-6 weeks for your scalp to adjust

2. Use the Right Products for Your Hair Type

Our stylist Mo emphasizes: "Using products formulated for your specific hair type is like giving your hair a custom-tailored suit - the difference is remarkable."

Product Recommendations by Hair Type

Fine Hair

  • Volumizing shampoo/conditioner
  • Lightweight leave-in conditioner
  • Texturizing spray
  • Root-lifting mousse

Thick/Curly Hair

  • Moisturizing shampoo/conditioner
  • Cream-based leave-in
  • Curl-defining cream
  • Oil for sealing ends

3. Protect Your Hair From Heat Damage

Heat styling is one of the biggest culprits of hair damage. Our stylist Sarah shares: "With proper protection and technique, you can minimize damage while still achieving beautiful styles."

Pro Tip: Air Dry When Possible

For healthier hair, let it air dry 80% of the way before using hot tools. This dramatically reduces heat exposure.

4. Get Regular Trims

Many clients try to stretch time between trims to maintain length, but this often backfires. As Adam explains: "Split ends travel up the hair shaft, causing more damage and ultimately requiring more length to be cut off."

Recommended Trim Schedule

  • Short styles: Every 4-6 weeks
  • Medium length: Every 6-8 weeks
  • Long hair: Every 8-12 weeks
  • Chemically treated hair: Add 2 weeks to above

5. Protect Your Hair While You Sleep

Nighttime can be surprisingly harsh on your hair. Friction from cotton pillowcases, tossing and turning, and loose hairstyles can lead to breakage and frizz.

Bonus: Professional Treatments Make a Difference

While at-home care is crucial, professional treatments provide benefits you can't replicate at home. Our stylists recommend:

  • Monthly deep conditioning: Penetrates deeper than store-bought masks
  • Seasonal protein treatments: Strengthens hair structure
  • Scalp treatments: Promotes healthy hair growth
  • Gloss treatments: Enhances shine and tone between color services
