What is the Silk Touch Smoothing Treatment?

The Silk Touch Smoothing Treatment is an advanced hair smoothing system that uses an all-natural complex mix of botanical and organic extracts in perfect balance to deliver smooth, frizz-free hair. This innovative treatment works by fortifying and repairing hair fibers from within, resulting in impressive reduction in volume without damaging the keratin disulphide bonds that give your hair its strength.

How Does It Work?

The magic of the Silk Touch Treatment lies in its unique blend of organic amino acids, sugars, cellulose derivatives and plant proteins that work in unison to transform your hair. During the treatment process, these natural ingredients penetrate the hair shaft, filling in porous areas and creating a protective layer around each strand.

This blend helps to reduce water absorption from the surrounding environment such as humidity or rain, which is the primary cause of frizz. The result is hair that remains smooth and straight regardless of weather conditions, while maintaining its natural movement and body.

The Treatment Process

Our expert stylists follow a meticulous 4-step process to ensure optimal results:

  1. Hair Analysis & Preparation: We begin with a thorough consultation to assess your hair type and condition. Your stylist will shampoo your hair with a clarifying treatment to remove any product buildup.
  2. Application: The Silk Touch solution is carefully applied section by section to ensure even coverage from roots to ends.
  3. Processing: The formula is left to work its magic for approximately 45-60 minutes, depending on your hair type and desired results.
  4. Sealing & Styling: After processing, your stylist will blow-dry and flat iron your hair to seal in the treatment, followed by a final rinse and styling.

Who Can Benefit From This Treatment?

The Silk Touch Smoothing Treatment is suitable for all hair types, from fine and straight to thick and curly. It's particularly beneficial for:

  • Those with frizzy, unmanageable hair
  • People living in humid climates
  • Individuals with coarse or thick hair who want to reduce styling time
  • Those looking for a healthier alternative to traditional keratin treatments
  • People with color-treated hair (safe for use on colored hair)

Aftercare & Maintenance

To maximize the longevity of your Silk Touch Treatment, we recommend following these aftercare guidelines:

  • Wait 48-72 hours before washing your hair after the treatment
  • Use sulfate-free shampoos and conditioners
  • Avoid chlorine and saltwater for the first two weeks
  • Use heat protectant when styling with hot tools
  • Schedule a touch-up treatment every 3-6 months

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the Silk Touch Treatment safe for color-treated hair?

Yes, the treatment is completely safe for color-treated hair. In fact, it can help extend the life of your color by sealing the cuticle and preventing color molecules from escaping.

How often should I get this treatment?

Most clients find that the treatment lasts 3-6 months, depending on hair type and how often you wash your hair. We recommend scheduling your next treatment when you notice your hair starting to revert to its original texture.

Can I still curl my hair after the treatment?

Absolutely! While the treatment significantly reduces frizz and makes hair straighter, you can still create beautiful curls and waves with hot tools. The difference is that your styled curls will last longer and be more manageable.

Does the treatment work on very curly hair?

While the treatment won't make very curly hair completely straight (unless that's your goal with additional flat ironing), it will dramatically reduce frizz, loosen curl pattern, and make hair much more manageable.

What's the difference between this and a Brazilian blowout?

The main difference is that our Silk Touch Treatment is completely formaldehyde-free, while many Brazilian blowouts contain formaldehyde or formaldehyde-releasing ingredients. The results are similar in terms of frizz reduction, but our treatment is much safer for both clients and stylists.
