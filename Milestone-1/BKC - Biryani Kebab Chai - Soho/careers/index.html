Our hiring process typically includes:

  1. Application review (1-3 business days)
  2. Phone screening (15-20 minutes)
  3. In-person interview (30-60 minutes)
  4. Practical assessment (for certain roles)
  5. Job offer and onboarding

We aim to complete the process within 1-2 weeks from application.

Our benefits package includes:

  • Competitive wages and tips
