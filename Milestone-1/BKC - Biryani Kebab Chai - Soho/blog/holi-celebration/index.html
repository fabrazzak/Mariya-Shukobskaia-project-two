"Holi is the day to express love with colors. It is a time to show affection. All the colors that are on you are of love." At BKC London, we're bringing this vibrant festival of colors to your plate with our special Holi menu, blending traditional flavors with festive joy.

A Festival of Flavors

Holi marks the arrival of spring and the victory of good over evil. It's a time when people come together, forgetting all differences, to celebrate with colors, music, and of course, delicious food. Our chefs have created a menu that captures the essence of this joyful festival.

Our special Holi thali featuring traditional festival dishes

Festive Starters

Begin your celebration with our colorful starters:

  • Dahi Bhalla Chaat: Lentil dumplings in yogurt with sweet and tangy chutneys
  • Holi Special Platter: Assorted kebabs and tikkas representing the colors of Holi
  • Papdi Chaat: Crisp wafers topped with potatoes, chickpeas, and chutneys

Main Course Highlights

For the main celebration, enjoy these Holi specials:

Gujiya Biryani

Our festive twist on the classic biryani, incorporating sweet elements reminiscent of traditional Holi sweets, served with raita and salan.

Thandai Butter Chicken

Butter chicken infused with thandai spices, creating a unique festival flavor that's both familiar and surprising.

"Holi is not just about colors; it's about the coming together of communities over shared meals. Our menu celebrates this spirit of unity through food."

- Chef Raj Patel, Head Chef at BKC London

Sweet Celebrations

No Holi celebration is complete without traditional sweets:

  • Gujiya: The quintessential Holi sweet - crispy pastry filled with khoya and dry fruits
  • Thandai Panna Cotta: A modern take on the festival drink
  • Malpua with Rabri: Sweet pancakes served with thickened milk

The Significance of Holi Foods

Traditional Holi foods are designed to complement the festival's energetic nature. Cooling foods like dahi bhalla and thandai help balance the body during the spring season, while the sweets provide quick energy for all the dancing and celebration.

At BKC London, we've carefully balanced our menu to include both traditional festival foods and dishes that appeal to modern palates, ensuring everyone can find something to enjoy during this special time.
