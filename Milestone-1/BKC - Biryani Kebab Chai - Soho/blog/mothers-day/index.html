Celebrate Mother's Day At BKC

A Culinary Tribute to the Incredible Woman in Your Life

March 10, 2025 Special Events
Home / Blog / Mother's Day Celebration

"Every day is a day to celebrate a mother's love." At BKC London, we believe that the extraordinary women in our lives deserve recognition not just on one special day, but every day. This Mother's Day, we invite you to honor the incredible woman in your life with a culinary experience that speaks from our heart to hers.

A Menu Crafted With Love

Our chefs have created a special Mother's Day menu that blends traditional Indian flavors with contemporary presentation. Each dish tells a story of heritage, love, and the nurturing spirit that mothers embody.

Our specially decorated dining area for Mother's Day celebrations

Starter Selection

Begin your celebration with our amuse-bouche selection featuring:

  • Pani Puri Shots: Crisp puris filled with spiced potato and tamarind water, served with edible flowers
  • Rose Petal Kebabs: Succulent minced lamb kebabs infused with rose petals and delicate spices
  • Mom's Favorite Samosa: A modern deconstruction of the classic samosa with mint chutney foam

Main Course Highlights

For the main event, choose from our signature dishes that celebrate the diversity of Indian cuisine:

Royal Biryani Feast

Our signature dum biryani cooked with fragrant basmati rice, tender meat (chicken, lamb, or prawn), and a secret blend of spices, served with raita and mirchi ka salan.

Thali of Blessings

A beautifully presented thali featuring an assortment of curries, dals, vegetables, breads, and rice - a complete meal that represents the nurturing nature of mothers everywhere.

The Story Behind Our Celebration

At BKC London, we draw inspiration from the matriarchs in our own lives. Many of our recipes have been passed down through generations of mothers and grandmothers, each adding their own touch while preserving the essence of tradition.

Head Chef Arjun Patel shares: "My earliest memories are of standing on a stool next to my mother in the kitchen, watching her transform simple ingredients into magical dishes. The patience, love, and care she put into every meal taught me that cooking is more than nourishment - it's an expression of love."

This philosophy is at the heart of everything we do at BKC London. Whether it's the slow-cooked biryanis that simmer for hours or the freshly baked breads that require precise timing, each dish is prepared with the same dedication and attention that a mother would give.

Beyond Mother's Day

While we're excited to celebrate this special day, we believe that showing appreciation shouldn't be limited to just one occasion. That's why we've created dishes throughout our regular menu that pay homage to maternal culinary wisdom:

  • Grandma's Chicken Curry: A recipe passed down through three generations of Chef Arjun's family
  • Mom's Special Chai: Our version of the comforting tea that soothes and revives
  • Sunday Biryani: Inspired by the tradition of families gathering for a special meal at the end of the week

We invite you to experience these dishes any time of year and create your own family traditions at BKC London.

About the Author

Chef Raj Patel is the Head Chef at BKC London, bringing over 15 years of culinary experience from kitchens in Mumbai, Dubai, and now London. His cooking philosophy centers on honoring traditional techniques while embracing modern creativity.
