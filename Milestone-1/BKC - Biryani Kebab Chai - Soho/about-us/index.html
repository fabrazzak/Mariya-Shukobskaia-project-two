Our Story

A journey of flavors from India to London

A Biryani Like Home

From the Heart of BKC London

Founded in 2018, BKC London was born from a simple idea: to bring the authentic flavors of Indian cuisine to the heart of London while maintaining the warmth and hospitality of a home kitchen.

Our name, BKC, stands for Biryani Kebab Chai - three pillars of Indian culinary tradition that represent our commitment to quality, authenticity, and comfort.

What started as a small family-run restaurant has grown into a beloved destination for food lovers across London, but our core values remain the same - to serve food made with love, passion, and the finest ingredients.

Our Philosophy

What makes BKC London special

Authentic Recipes

We preserve traditional cooking methods passed down through generations, ensuring every dish tells a story of India's rich culinary heritage.

Premium Ingredients

We source the finest spices directly from India and use locally-sourced, high-quality meats and produce for all our dishes.

Family Values

At BKC, every guest is treated like family. We believe great food tastes even better when shared with loved ones.

Meet Our Chefs

The talented team behind our flavors

Raj Patel

Head Chef

With over 25 years of experience in Indian cuisine, Chef Raj brings authentic flavors from his hometown of Hyderabad, specializing in slow-cooked biryanis and rich curries.

Priya Sharma

Pastry Chef

Chef Priya's desserts are inspired by her grandmother's recipes from Delhi. Her signature gulab jamun and kheer have become customer favorites.

Ameen Khan

Kebab Master

Trained in Lucknow's famous kebab houses, Chef Ameen's expertise in marinating and grilling meats brings the authentic taste of Awadhi cuisine to London.

Our Ingredients

We believe great food starts with great ingredients. That's why we go the extra mile to source the very best:

  • Premium basmati rice imported directly from the foothills of the Himalayas
  • Hand-picked spices from Kerala, Kashmir, and other regions of India
  • Locally-sourced, high-quality meats from trusted British suppliers
  • Fresh vegetables and herbs from London markets
