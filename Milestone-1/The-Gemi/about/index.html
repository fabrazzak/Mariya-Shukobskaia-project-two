Our Story

Bringing authentic Korean café culture to Sydney since 2018

The Gami Caffè Journey

Founded in 2018 by Korean-Australian couple Min-jun and Soo-ah, Gami Caffè began as a passion project to share the unique flavors of Korean café culture with Sydney. After years of visiting traditional tea houses and modern cafés in Seoul, they noticed a gap in Sydney's vibrant food scene for authentic Korean coffee and dessert experiences.

Starting as a small kiosk in Chinatown, Gami Caffè quickly gained popularity for its signature injeolmi bingsu and dalgona coffee. In 2020, we moved to our current George Street location, where we've been serving both the Korean community and curious food lovers ever since.

The name "Gami" comes from the Korean word for "flavor" (가미), representing our commitment to delivering authentic taste experiences that transport you to the bustling cafés of Seoul.

Our Philosophy

Quality Ingredients

We source premium Korean ingredients like roasted soybean powder and traditional rice cakes, while supporting local Australian producers for dairy and seasonal fruits.

Authentic Recipes

Our menu stays true to traditional Korean flavors while adapting presentation and serving styles for modern café culture.

Community Focus

We're proud to be a gathering place for Sydney's Korean community and a bridge introducing Korean culture to new audiences.
