Our Story

From a small neighborhood market to Calgary's premier destination for handcrafted gifts

Since 2010

The Bridgeland Market Story

2010

Humble Beginnings

Founded by Sarah and Michael Thompson, Bridgeland Market started as a small neighborhood shop specializing in local produce and handmade goods. Our goal was simple: to bring the community together through quality local products.

2013

Discovering Our Niche

After noticing how much our customers loved our seasonal gift baskets, we decided to focus on curating exceptional gifts. Our first dedicated gift collection featured Alberta-made products and was an instant hit.

2016

Expanding Our Space

We moved to our current location on 1st Avenue, doubling our retail space and adding a floral workshop. This allowed us to expand our offerings while maintaining our commitment to quality and personal service.

2020

Weathering the Storm

During challenging times, we adapted by launching our online store and local delivery service. The community's support helped us not just survive, but thrive, reinforcing our belief in the power of local connections.

Today

A Calgary Institution

What began as a small market has grown into a beloved destination for thoughtful gifts. We're proud to support over 50 local artisans and producers while serving thousands of happy customers each year.
