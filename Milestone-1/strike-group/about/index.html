Who We Are

Strike Group is a leading Canadian industrial construction and maintenance company headquartered in Calgary, Alberta. For over two decades, we've been delivering exceptional results for clients across the energy, infrastructure, and industrial sectors.

What began as a local contractor has grown into a nationally recognized firm, honored for four consecutive years as one of Canada's Top 40 Contractors by On-Site Magazine. This recognition reflects our commitment to excellence in every project we undertake.

Our History

From humble beginnings to national recognition - our journey of growth and achievement

2003

Company Founding

Strike Group was established in Calgary, Alberta, with a small team of dedicated professionals focused on local industrial projects.

2010

Western Canada Expansion

We expanded our operations across Western Canada, establishing regional offices and taking on larger-scale projects.

2015

National Recognition

Our work on major energy infrastructure projects earned us recognition as an industry leader in safety and innovation.

2019

First Top 40 Award

We were honored with our first Top 40 Canadian Contractor award by On-Site Magazine, beginning our consecutive streak.

2025

Current Operations

Today, we employ over 1,000 professionals and operate across Canada, delivering complex projects with excellence.
