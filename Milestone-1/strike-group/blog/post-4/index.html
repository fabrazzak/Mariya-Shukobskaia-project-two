Strike Group Recognized as Top Employer for Fourth Consecutive Year

What makes Strike Group one of Canada's best places to work in the construction industry

  • Home
  • Blog
  • Company News
  • Top Employer 2025

Strike Group team members at our annual employee appreciation event

For the fourth consecutive year, Strike Group has been named one of Canada's Best Employers by Forbes and Statista, solidifying our position as a leader in workplace excellence within the construction industry. This prestigious recognition reflects our ongoing commitment to creating an exceptional work environment that attracts, retains, and develops top talent.

What Makes Strike Group a Top Employer

The annual Best Employers list is compiled through independent surveys of over 12,000 Canadian employees working for companies with at least 500 employees. Rankings are based on direct and indirect recommendations from employees who were asked to rate their willingness to recommend their own employers to friends and family.

Here are the key factors that earned Strike Group this recognition:

💼

Career Development

Our comprehensive training programs and clear advancement paths help employees grow their skills and careers. Over 78% of management positions are filled through internal promotions.

❤️

Health & Wellness

Industry-leading benefits including extended health coverage, mental health support, and wellness programs that go beyond standard offerings.

⚖️

Work-Life Balance

Flexible work arrangements, generous vacation policies, and family-friendly initiatives support employees in all life stages.

💰

Competitive Compensation

Pay scales that exceed industry averages, performance bonuses, and profit-sharing programs reward employees for their contributions.

🌎

Diversity & Inclusion

Our DEI initiatives create opportunities for all employees, with women representing 32% of our workforce (vs. industry average of 14%).

🏗️

Safety Culture

Our safety-first approach protects employees with industry-leading training, equipment, and protocols that make safety everyone's responsibility.

By the Numbers

Our employee-focused approach delivers measurable results:

92% Employee satisfaction rate
4.8/5 Average Glassdoor rating
14% Below industry turnover rate
85% Employees would recommend us
