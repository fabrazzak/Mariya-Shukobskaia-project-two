About Strike Group

Founded in Calgary, Alberta, Strike Group has grown from a local contractor to a nationally recognized leader in industrial construction. Our team of over 1,000 skilled professionals brings expertise, innovation, and dedication to every project.

What sets us apart is our commitment to operational excellence, safety, and building strong relationships with our clients, partners, and the communities where we work.
