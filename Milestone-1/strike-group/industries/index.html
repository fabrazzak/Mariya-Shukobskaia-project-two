Building Across Sectors

With our diverse expertise and adaptable approach, Strike Group delivers exceptional results across multiple industries. Each sector presents unique challenges, and we rise to meet them with specialized solutions, innovative techniques, and an unwavering commitment to safety and quality.

Energy Sector Solutions

Strike Group has extensive experience in energy construction, including oil and gas facilities, pipelines, and renewable energy projects. We understand the complex regulatory environment and technical requirements of energy projects.

  • Pipeline construction and maintenance
  • Compressor and pump stations
  • Gas processing plants
  • Renewable energy projects
  • Energy storage facilities
