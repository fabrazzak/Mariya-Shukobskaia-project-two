Bringing Thoughtfulness to Gifting

Founded in 2018 in Calgary, Alberta, Sentisenti Gifts began with a simple mission: to help people find meaningful gifts that truly resonate with their loved ones. Our founder, Sarah Johnson, noticed how challenging it was to find unique, high-quality gifts that went beyond the ordinary.

What started as a small boutique with carefully curated items has grown into a beloved Canadian destination for thoughtful gifting. We pride ourselves on discovering unique products from local artisans and international makers who share our commitment to quality and creativity.

"A gift should tell a story - about the giver, the recipient, and the special bond they share. That's the philosophy behind every item we select." - Sarah Johnson, Founder
Sarah Johnson in our first store location
